Five quality loops, six humour patterns, one delta shield

Quality in the memoir is not a final-stage approval gate. It is five silent checks that run around every edit, six rules for what humour is allowed to do, and a shield that makes Asif's own words untouchable.

The six humour patterns

All humour in the memoir follows one of these — extracted from Asif's actual writing. The skill never invents a new pattern.
·· pattern A ··

Deadpan after emotional setup

The setup is serious. The punchline arrives as a calm observation, never flagged as humour.

"She took me to a therapist, presumably to fix me, the way you take a clock to a repairman because the ticking annoys you."

·· pattern B ··

Absurd comparison

Elevated language applied to trivial things.

"The point was not to understand the material. The point was to become a photocopier with anxiety."

·· pattern C ··

Unexpected reversal

The punchline IS the decision.

"I had already been punished for the crime, so I may as well commit it."

·· pattern D ··

Mock-formal concession

Ironic formality about absurd situations.

"Convenient arrangement, really, if your goal is absolute control with divine customer support."

·· pattern E ··

Mathematical / formula humour

Equations and logic applied to emotional situations.

"Fat Cat + Hamila + Pregnant = Bad Company ==> WTF?"

·· pattern F ··

Self-deprecation without self-pity

Acknowledges flaws without wallowing.

"carrying enough unresolved confusion to qualify as a public hazard"

·· hard rules ··

Humour never appears inside emotional peaks (the pillow / ceiling, the caning, the therapist verdict). Maximum 2–3 humour beats per 1,000 words in narrative. Babu's advice uses humour sparingly — one per section max. The funniest lines are also the most insightful: humour is compressed wisdom.

·· the shield ··

Delta protection — Asif's edits are sacred

When he changes a chapter file between sessions, the system protects what he wrote.
Seven steps · one principle · the writer wins
·· the principle ··

The quality system exists to protect the writing, not to police it. Five loops keep the voice consistent, six humour patterns keep the laughter authentic, the delta shield keeps Asif's hand on every paragraph he has touched. The combination is what lets the skill help him write the book faster without ever writing the book for him.
