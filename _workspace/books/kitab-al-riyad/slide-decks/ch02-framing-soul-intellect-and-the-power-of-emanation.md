# Slide Deck Framing, EP02: Soul, Intellect, and the Power of Emanation

## Audience

Asif's children, thoughtful young adults familiar with the audio episode. They carry the identity-versus-rank formula from EP01 and need to see it bend each new analogy *Samuel* offers in Chapter One.

## Core Principle

Each slide must add visual information the audio cannot carry. Slides hold *Samuel's* reading and the author's re-reading at once, map the Two Weighty Things hadith onto the cosmological pair, and gather the chapter's analogies under one formula.

## Visual Priorities

- Sun-and-moon: receptivity-as-completeness against lesser-light-as-deficiency, side by side.
- Two Weighty Things hadith mapped onto Soul-and-*Hayuli* as a comparison matrix with cross-mapping cells.
- *Samuel's* four-move attack cascade as a directed genealogy, paired with the author's counter-cascade.
- The twelve analogies gathered into one comparison matrix, the formula re-applied across every row.

## Prohibited Patterns

- No slides whose only content is a sub-chapter title.
- No literal illustrations: no calligraphy, no sun-moon photos, no rope photos.
- No bullet-list slides masquerading as diagrams.
- No slides that restate a sentence the audio already speaks.

## Steering Phrases

- Each slide must add visual information not present in the audio.
- When two readings are contrasted, show them as columns, attribute by attribute.
- For the Two Weighty Things, use a comparison matrix, not a rope illustration.
- Place the author on the left and *Samuel* on the right in every contrast.
- Slow down on sub-chapter sixteen; give it two slides at different angles.

Do not read this prompt aloud.
