- **Yusuf Ali** is the named translator for every Quranic citation, locked in on first per-chapter use. This matches the prior workspace's editorial decision and was confirmed on the v1.3 challenger run.
- **Sahih Sitta** appears in source as "Sahah Sitta"; chapter uses canonical *Sahih Sitta* form. Framing locks the phonetic.
- **No Ismaili philosopher** is quoted by name — Tier 5 representation comes through the Holy Du'a reference in ch05. Acceptable per the source: Ghazali is Sunni Ash'ari, and the Ismaili thread is intentionally light. The series mix (5 tiers across 5 chapters, with Tier 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 6 always present and Tier 5 in ch05) satisfies the "mix" requirement at the series level.
- **No `[CONTEXT NEEDED]` markers** in any chapter. Every quote is attributable. Where the source quotes a hadith without a collection number, the chapter follows the source's attribution and does not invent one.
