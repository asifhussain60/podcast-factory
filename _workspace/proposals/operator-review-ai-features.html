Reading-time compression · Strongly recommend MVP

Per-page 1-line summaries

Above each page marker in the transcript, show a 1-sentence summary: "§2.3 introduces the doctrine of tawḥīd al-mubdiʿ; voice shifts to first-person."

The summary lets you SKIM. Pages whose summary matches your mental model = skip. Pages whose summary is surprising or unclear = dive in. Compresses 200 pages of dense prose to 200 line-summaries (~5 min skim).

Quality assurance · Strongly recommend MVP

Pre-flight error scan

Before you start reading, the AI scans the full transcript and surfaces a punch-list of suspect passages: OCR scrambles, impossible word pairs ("angles" near "spheres"), broken citations, numeric values that don't add up.

Each finding has a confidence score, page anchor, and quoted text. You triage the AI's list in ~3 min instead of reading the whole book to find them yourself. The AI's misses + adds you find while skimming combine to a complete review.
