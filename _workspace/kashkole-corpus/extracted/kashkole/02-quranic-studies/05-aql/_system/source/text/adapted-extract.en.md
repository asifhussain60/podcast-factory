To answer this question, lets think about the one primary difference between us human beings and animals.... Did Allah give us something that he did not give to the animals? He did.... He blessed us with an intellect, ⟪ar:العَقْلُ⟫.... Al-Aql in Ancient Arabic is used for 2 things... First, Aql is used for a container that can hold something ..... and secondly Aql is used for a restraint.... the rope that is used to tie a camel is called an ⟪ar:عِقَال⟫....

Let's start with the first one... which is a container that can hold something.... we'll discuss the other one at a later time InshaAllah...

So linguistically speaking, Aql basically means a container... right? But what's really interesting is that in Arabic it signifies a very flexible container... A container that can expands to fit its contents.... The more contents you put in it, it'll stretch and expand to fit it... In other words, a container that can expand as its content increases.... You understand?  In contrast, a container that is fixed..... that does not expand, is called ⟪ar:حاویۃ، أِناءَ، صندوق⟫ . These are containers that can hold only a fixed amount ... but not Al-Aql... The more you store into AlAql, it will expand to accomodate its contents.... Is that clear?

So then what is Aql a container for? What does it hold?

It's a container for ⟪ar:العِلْمُ⟫... knowledge! The more your knowledge expands, the more your intellect will expand with it.... to accomodate it.... And this is what we see in a child... as he grows older.... as he keeps assimilating knowledge, his intellect keeps growing... SubhanAllah!

Now what is Ilm or knowledge? Ilm in Arabic means the true quality of something... or the true reality of something... ⟪ar:حقیقۃ الاشیاء⟫..... And the faculty that can not only store... but also measure and differenciate between the quality of things is called Aql or intellect.... So two things... There is Ilm (knowledge) which is the true reality of something, and then there is the container for that knowledge that can store it, understand it, apply logic and reasoning to it... which is Al-Aql or intellect...

So gold is valuable metal .. that is a reality... a rock is worthless that is also a reality.... Intellect can not only store these realities as knowledge... but can also compare and differenciate between the two... It can understand that gold is more valuable than rocks... And as knowledge grows... intellect will also expand to accomodate more of it...
