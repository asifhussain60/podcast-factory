RAOOF vs RAHEEM

RAOOF and RAHEEM. These are both attributes of Allah. And Allah(Subhanhu Wa Ta'ala) identifies his messenger (Salallahu alayhi wa aalihee wa sallam) with these same attributes. Now what's the difference between the two?. Ar-Ra’uf. is “the One who has compassion. which is the intensification of mercy. Therefore it has the same meaning as rahim. but in a mroe intensified form. Scholars have said that if a calamity hits you. the Raheem has mercy on you. after that calamity is over. But Ra’uf is one who is SO merciful. that his mercy extends BEFORE the calamity hits. And it involves him taking care of you and warning you. so that you do not become the victim of a calamity. 

I'll give you an example. A mother. who is very protective of her children. she dresses her children in warm clothes during the winter. Why?. So that they do not suffer from the cold.  That is ra’fa. But if the child does not listen to the mother. and it takes off those warm clothes and pays in the cold and falls sick. Now the mother's heart aches. because her child has become sick. and so she does everything in her power to ease her child’s suffering. That is RAHEEMIYYAH. So ra’fa. is the highest form of mercy. where Allah protects His servants by warning them of the deeds that will necessitate punishment.

And that is why the Quran. is the most EXTREME form of mercy. Because it is full of warnings. and it is full of love, care, concern and mercy. This is why Allah called his beloved messenger RAOOF . Because he came to us. to WARN us of the dangers of this world. so that we don't have to suffer. He didn't need to do that!. He is already the beloved of Allah. Jannah was MADE for him. But he took upon himself. to accept this mission. of our guidance. because of the RA'FA that he had in his heart for us. And because he is RAHEEM. he formulated the shareeah for us. as a medicine. to cure our sick souls. so that they can be elevated. and they can become deserving of the RAHEEMIYAH of Allah in the next world. That is why he is called ⟪ar:رحمۃ لی العالمین⟫. Isn't that interesting. Allah is ⟪ar:رب العالمین⟫.  Rasul Allah (Salallahu alayhi wa aalihee wa sallam) is Rehmatul-lil-Alameen. **Summary of the 17 Benefits of Tribulation**

1. You realise the power of Lordship over you

2: You realise your abject servanthood and your complete state of resignation and brokenness before the will and power of Allah

3: Sincerity to Allah

4. You return to Allah and are suddenly fervent in your desire of your Lord

5. It leads to a humbled state before God

6. You are forbearing to the One who has afflicted you.
