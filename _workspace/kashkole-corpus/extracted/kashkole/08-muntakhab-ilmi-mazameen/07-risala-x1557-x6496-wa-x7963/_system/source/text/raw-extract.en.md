### Food Enjoyment:

Allah Almighty has placed a stomach in the stomach of man, which is like a bag. The material that reaches it from the food works for digestion through heat on it. Through heat he starts burning food so that he can generate energy for the whole body. When the stomach burns and digests all the contents, it starts the process of digestion on its own if the bag is empty. An example of this is that when the entire candle is burned, the fire then starts burning the candle. In the same way, when the heat of the stomach starts to work on itself, the person starts to suffer. From this pain, his self awakens and at that time he begins to develop hunger and cravings. When he eats something and the food reaches the bag again, the stomach starts burning the food again with its own heat. In this way, a person gets relief and comfort from suffering, which is called pleasure. The heat of the stomach produces hunger and the heat of the liver produces thirst. When the liver is not hydrated, it starts burning itself like the stomach. When water is given to him, his thirst is quenched and he gets peace, which is called lizt.

On the other hand, if a person eats too much food, or drinks too much water, and the stomach or liver sac is filled too much, its heat is not enough to digest the substance that causes the human body to become burdensome and cause discomfort. Hunger and thirst cause discomfort in the body due to distance from the balance or moderation. The feeling of pain is called grief. When his mood is moderated through food or water, the discomfort is removed, the feeling of which is called relief or pleasure. There are two sides to any process on which suffering exists. The name of the state between them is Lizt, which is why the Messenger of Allah (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) said: "The best way is the best way in any matter." The state between two extremes of suffering is called pleasure. As soon as the balance is bad, the pain arises.

Allaah has made it obligatory for all animals to eat and drink for the rest of their lives. If this is not the case, the body parts will start eating themselves, which causes discomfort. The function of the stomach is to digest food and deliver nutrients to the body through the blood. He's emptying his stomach so he can digest the next thing. For this reason, the animal eats and produces constantly. That is, after every sorrow, the system of pleasure and after every pleasure the system of pain has been set. Maulana Ali says that this entire universe is human beings. When the universe is like a human being, it is necessary to have a stomach, stomach and liver. And when these parts are in place, there will be hunger and thirst, and the same principle of pleasure and sorrow is also applied to the world. In the same way, there is the hunger of the self which is called desires.

### Sleep Pleasure:

One finds pleasure and relief, through beastliness, tranquility, silence and sleep. When the animal moves a lot, the friction in the body organs causes heat and constipation in the temperament. Because of this, the organs and muscles begin to dry up and become thick and stiff, and they sleep in the animal to moderate the temperament. Through sleep, the body's organs begin to cool down. This cooling creates moisture and moisture in them, which cools the nerves and makes all the nerves able to move easily.

For this reason, lifting more weight creates fatigue and removing this weight gives relief. This process of pain and relief is also going on one after the other. Too much movement warms up the mood, causing the moods to go out of moderation. Then sleep and tranquility again equalize the balance. For this reason, sleep is a comfort and a pleasure. When the oppressors become cold, then they have a need for movement, due to which they awaken the person. At that time, if a person is not awake and sleeps more than necessary, then the temperament becomes too cool and the temperament deteriorates again.
