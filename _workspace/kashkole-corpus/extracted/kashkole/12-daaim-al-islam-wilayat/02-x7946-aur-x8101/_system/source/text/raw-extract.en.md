The light of faith comes from within and comes from outside, whereas Islam is the name of external light. Islam is related to the outward Shari'ah, while faith is related to the inner meaning of this Shari'ah.

### Verification and Security:

Faith literally means to confirm or affirm, and in another sense, to give assurance. Faith is a continuous verb that does not end only with a verb, but also requires a participle. That is, two things are required for faith: the verb and the participle. Therefore, if the meaning of faith is to affirm, then it must be asked, who should be confirmed? The person who will be confirmed will ⟪ar:مُصَدِّق⟫be called and he will be the object. Even if the meaning of faith is meant to give security, then there will be a need for the participle, who or what is being given security?

The Prophet (peace be upon him) combined these two meanings of faith in one of his hadiths and said: ⟪ar-quote:ھِینَ اَلمُؤمِن مَن آمَنَ جَورِہِ بِوَایقَۃ⟫ "A believer is one who protects his neighbor from his harm." It is because of these two meanings that a person is called a believer, and he has a name ⟪ar:اَلمُؤمِنُ⟫from Allaah⟪ar:اسمآء الحسنی⟫, so a person is called a believer because of his affirmation of ⟪ar:وَعَدُ وَعِیدْ⟫Allaah, and Allaah has protected ⟪ar:اَلمُؤمِنُ⟫him from His fatal punishment.

### Faith of the Heart:

The heart, from which a person derives understanding and insight, is the owner of his whole body. Without His opinion and command, no organ is allowed to enter or excrete anything in the body. The faith that Allah has enjoined upon the heart is confession, knowledge, belief, acceptance and acceptance. That is, the believer should confess in his heart that Allah is one and that there is no god but Him. He is ⟪ar-quote:وحدہ لا شریک لہ⟫. He is alone and unselfish. He has no wife and no children. ⟪ar:محمد مصطفی صلی اللہ علیہ و آلہ وسلم⟫They are the servants of Allah and His true Messengers. After that, it is incumbent upon the heart to acknowledge all that has been sent down by Allah, such as the Book and the Prophet. After this affirmation, it is obligatory on the heart to acquire the knowledge of the knowledge of the limits, which strengthens the faith. Therefore, this is the practical and scientific faith that Allah has enjoined upon the heart! This is indicated in the following verses:

### Pure Faith:

Those who have pure faith are those whom Allah Himself praises and gives them the glad tidings of Paradise. These are the people who are the two pillars of faith (Sadiq and Aman). They have the status of 'righteousness' in the sense that although they exist in the world in terms of their bodies, they ⟪ar:دار الکذب⟫are in the purity of their souls⟪ar:دار الصدق و صفا⟫. That is, although these people are ostensibly present in the world, they are in reality keeping an eye on the world while living in the Hereafter. Whereas ordinary Muslims keep an eye on the Hereafter while living in the world.

'Aman' comes upon them in the sense that when they ⟪ar:نفوس لطیفۃ⟫reach Paradise⟪ar:دار السلام⟫, they will be safe from all the difficulties that befall their bodies after death, and for them death will be a soul and soul. Outward faith refers to the verbal confession of five things:

| 1 | Affirmation of Allah: ** Affirmation that there is one Creator of this world, who is One, One, Living, Almighty, Wise, and Creator of all creation. He is the master of all these creatures, and there is no partner in His plan.
