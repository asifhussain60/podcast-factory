## Al-Ḥamdu lillāh Khāliq al-Wujūd min al-ʿAdam — Praise to God, Creator of Being from Nothingness

⟪ar-quote:الحمدُ للہِ خَالقِ الوُجودِ مِنَ العَدَم ، وَ جَاعِلِ النُّورِ مِنَ الظُّلَم

وَ مُخرِجِ الصَّبرِ مِنَ الاَلَم ، وَ مُلکِ التَّوبَۃِ عَلی النَّدَم⟫

All praise belongs solely to Allah, who brought forth existence from non-existence, who created light from darkness, who extracted patience from pain, and who granted the kingdom of repentance to those who feel remorse. We render thanks to Him equally during our calamities as during His blessings, for in both states His wisdom is manifest.[^cite-1]

⟪ar-quote:فَنَشکُرُہُ عَلیَ المصَائِبِ کما نَشکُرُہُ عَلی النِّعَم⟫

We are grateful to Him in our trials just as we are grateful to Him for His favours.

⟪ar-quote:وَ نُصَلِّی عَلی رَسُولِہِ الاکرَم ، لِشَّرَفِ الاَشَم ، وَ النُّورِ الاتَم

وَالکِتاَبِ المُحکَم ، وَ کمالِ النَّبِیِّنَ وَ الخَاتَم⟫

Upon him rest the blessings and salutations of Allah—upon his noble messenger, for his towering honour, his perfect luminosity, the decisive book entrusted to him, and for his station as the perfection and seal of all prophets.[^cite-2]

⟪ar-quote:سَیِّدِی وَلَدِ آدَم ۔ اَلَّذِی بَشَّرَ بِہِ عِیسیَ ابْنُ مَریَم

وَ دَعَا لِبِعثَتِہِ اِبرَاھِیمُ عَلیہِ السَّلام ، حِینَ کاَنَ یَرفَعُ قَواَعِدَ بَیتِ اللہِ المحَّرم⟫

He is the master of the children of Adam—he of whom ʿĪsā son of Maryam was given glad tidings, and for whose coming Ibrāhīm, upon him be peace, supplicated whilst raising the foundations of the sacred house of Allah.[^cite-3]

⟪ar-quote:فَصَلَّ اللہ علیہ و آلہ و سلم ، و علی أَتباَعِہِ خَیرِ الاُمَم

الَّذِینَ بَارکَ اللہُ بِہِم کاَفَّۃَ النَّاس العَرَبَ مِنھُم وَالعَجَم⟫

May the blessings of Allah descend upon him and his family and those who followed him as the best of all peoples—those through whom Allah has sent blessing to all humanity, both Arab and non-Arab alike.

⟪ar-quote:و صل اللہ علی أمیر المؤمنین، الانزع البطین، وَ قائد الغر المحجلین

غالب علی کل غالب، مطلوب کل طالب⟫

And upon the Commander of the Faithful (amīr al-muʾminīn), highest in rank and deepest in knowledge, leader of those whose foreheads shine with the mark of faith—the victor over all victors and the ultimate aspiration of every seeker.

---

⟪ar-quote:الحمد للہ ، الَّذِی أَنزَلَ عَلی عَبدِہِ الکتابَ وَلَم یَجعَل لَّہُ عِوَجَا⟫

All praise belongs to Allah, who sent down the book upon His servant containing no crookedness therein.

⟪ar-quote:والحمد للہ ، الذی لم یتخذ ولداً ولم یکن لہ شریک فی الملک

و لم یکن لہ ولی من الذل و کبرہ تکبیرا⟫

All praise belongs to Allah, who took no son, who has no partner in dominion, who has no need of a protector against weakness—and all creation glorifies Him with the greatest glorification.

⟪ar-quote:والحمد للہ ، الذی نَحمَدُہُ وَ نَستَعِینُہُ وَ نَستَغفِرُہُ وَ نُئمِنُ بِہِ وَ نَتَوَکَّلُ عَلَیہ⟫

And praise be to Allah, whom we praise, from whom we seek aid, to whom we turn in repentance, in whom we believe, and upon whom we rely.

⟪ar-quote:و نغوذُ باللہ مِن شُرُورِ أَنفُسِنَا ، وَ مِن سَیِّئاَتِ اعمَالِنَا⟫

We seek refuge in Him from the evils of our souls and from the ugliness of our deeds.

⟪ar-quote:مَن یَھدِہِ اللہُ فَلاَ مُضِلَّ لَہ وَ مَن یُضلِل فَلا ھادِیَ لَہ⟫

Whosoever Allah guides none can lead astray, and whosoever He leads astray none can guide aright.

⟪ar-quote:و نشھدُ ان لا الہ الا اللہ وحدہ لا شریک لہ⟫

And we testify that there is no god save Allah alone, without any partner unto Him.

⟪ar-quote:و نشھد ان محمد عبدُ اللہ و رسولہ

أَرسَلَہُ اللہُ تَعاَلی بِالھُدَی وَ دِینِ الَحقِّ لِیُظھِرَہُ عَلی الدِّینِ کُلِّہ ، وَ کفَا بِاللہِ شَھِیدَا⟫

And we testify that Muḥammad is His servant and His messenger. The Most High Allah sent him bearing guidance and the true religion, that He might make it prevail over all other religions—and Allah suffices as witness.

⟪ar-quote:و نشھد ان مولانا علی ابن ابی طالب، وصیہ و وزیرہ⟫

And we testify that our master ʿAlī, son of Abū Ṭālib, is his successor and his minister.

⟪ar-quote:فصل اللہ علیہم و علی آلہم و سَلِّمُھُم تَسلِیماً کثیراً کثیرا⟫

May Allah's blessings descend upon them and upon their progeny, with abundant and endless salutations.

---

⟪ar-quote:اللّٰھم لا عِلمَ لَنا اِلَّا ما عَلَّمتَنَا۔ أِنَّک أَنتَ العَلِیمُ الحکیم⟫

O Allah, we possess no knowledge save that which Thou hast taught us. Verily, Thou art the All-Knowing, the All-Wise.

⟪ar-quote:اللّٰھم عَلِّمنَا بِما یَنفَعْنَا ؛ وَ یَنفَعنَا بِمَا عَلَّمتَنَا⟫

O Allah, teach us that which benefits us, and let us benefit from what Thou dost teach us.

⟪ar-quote:اَللّٰھُمَّ اِنِّا نَعُوْذُ بِکَ مِنْ عِلْمٍ لاَّ يَنْفَعُ ، وَمِنْ قَلْبٍ لاَّ يَخْشَعُ

وِ مِن عَینٍ لا تَدمَع ، وَمِنْ نَّفْسٍ لاَّ تَشْبَعُ⟫

O Allah, we seek refuge in Thee from knowledge that is of no benefit, from a heart that does not fear Thee, from eyes that do not weep in penitence, and from a soul that is never satiated.

---

⟪ar-quote:فأما بعد ، یقول سبحانہ و تعالی فی کتابہ الکریم ، بعد أَقولُ أعوذ باللہ من الشیطان الرجیم

بسم اللہ الرحمن الرحیم⟫

Now, as for what follows: Thus speaks the Most Exalted in His noble Qurʾān—and I preface this by saying: I seek refuge in Allah from the accursed Satan. In the name of Allah, the Compassionate, the Merciful.

⟪ar-quote:رب اشرحلی صدری و یسرلی أمری ۔ وحلل عقدۃ من لسانی یفقھو قولی⟫

"My Lord, expand for me my breast, and ease my task for me, and loosen the knot from my tongue that they may understand my speech."

⟪ar-quote:اللہم ثَبِّتنا عِندَ الموتی بی لا الہ الا اللہ

و اللھم جعلنا من الذین آمنو و عمل الصالحات و تواصو بالحق وتواصو بالصبر⟫

O Allah, strengthen us at the moment of death upon the belief that there is no god save Allah. O Allah, make us among those who have believed and done righteous deeds, and who have counselled one another toward truth and counselled one another toward patience.

⟪ar-quote:آمین یا رب العلمین⟫

Āmīn, O Lord of all the worlds.

---

## Lā Yablugh Madḥatahu al-Qāʾilūn — 'No Praisers Can Attain His Praise'

⟪ar-quote:الحَمْدُ للهِ الَّذِي لَا يَبْلُغُ مِدْحَتَهُ الْقَائِلُونَ، وَلَا يُحْصِي نَعْمَاءَهُ الْعَادُّونَ، وَلَا يُؤَدِّي حَقَّهُ الْمُجْتَهِدُونَ، الَّذِي لَا يُدْرِكُهُ بُعْدُ الْهِمَمِ، وَلَا يَنَالُهُ غَوْصُ الْفِطَنِ، الَّذِي لَيْسَ لِصِفَتِهِ حَدٌّ مَحْدُودٌ، وَلَا نَعْتٌ مَوْجُودٌ، وَلَا وَقْتٌ مَعْدُودٌ، وَلَا أَجَلٌ مَمْدُودٌ، فَطَرَ الْخَلَائِقَ بِقُدْرَتِهِ، وَنَشَرَ الرِّيَاحَ بِرَحْمَتِهِ، وَوَتَّدَ بِالصُّخُورِ مَيَدَانَ أَرْضِهِ.⟫

All praise belongs to Allah, whose excellence no speakers can attain, whose blessings no reckoners can enumerate, whose due no striving servants can fully render. He cannot be reached by the heights of aspiration, nor grasped by the depths of keen intellect. His attributes have no bounded limit, His descriptions no actual existence separate from His essence, His span no measured time, His term no extended duration. He created all creatures by His power, spread the winds by His mercy, and fixed the earth with mountains as pegs.[^cite-4]

⟪ar-quote:وَالصَّلَوَاتُ وَالسَّلَامُ عَلَى سَيِّدِ الْأَنْبِيَاءِ وَالْمُرْسَلِينَ، خَاتَمِ النَّبِيِّينَ، سَيِّدِنَا الْمُمَجَّدِ، الْبَشِيرِ الْمُصَدَّقِ، النَّذِيرِ الْمُؤَيَّدِ، الْمُصْطَفَى الْأَمْجَدِ، مَحْمُودٍ الْأَحْمَدِ، أَبِي الْقَاسِمِ مُحَمَّدٍ.⟫

Upon the master of all prophets and messengers, the seal of the prophets—our lord the most glorious, the confirmed bearer of glad tidings, the supported warner, the chosen most noble, Muḥammad the Praised, Abū al-Qāsim.[^cite-5]

⟪ar-quote:وَالصَّلَوَاتُ وَالسَّلَامُ عَلَى وَصِيِّهِ عَلِيِّ بْنِ أَبِي طَالِبٍ، أَشْرَفِ الْوَصِيِّينَ وَأَمِيرِ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ.⟫

And upon his successor ʿAlī son of Abū Ṭālib, the noblest of successors and Commander of the Faithful.

⟪ar-quote:وَالصَّلَوَاتُ وَالسَّلَامُ عَلَى سَيِّدِنَا الْإِمَامِ الطَّيِّبِ صَاحِبِ الدَّعْوَةِ النَّبَوِيَّةِ، وَصَوْلَةِ الْحَيْدَرِيَّةِ، وَالْعِصْمَةِ الْفَاطِمِيَّةِ، وَالشَّجَاعَةِ الْحُسَيْنِيَّةِ، وَالْعُلُومِ الْبَاقِرِيَّةِ، وَالْمَعَاقِلِ الْجَعْفَرِيَّةِ، وَالْحِكْمَةِ الْإِسْمَاعِيلِيَّةِ، وَالْجُودِ الْمُحَمَّدِيَّةِ.⟫

And upon our master, the Pure Imam, bearer of the prophetic summons (daʿwa), possessing the might of Ḥaydar, the infallibility of Fāṭima, the courage of Ḥusayn, the sciences of Bāqir, the sagacity of Jaʿfar, the wisdom characteristic of the Ismāʿīlī tradition, and the generosity of Muḥammad.[^cite-6]

⟪ar-quote:فَصَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِمْ وَعَلَى آلِهِمْ وَسَلَّمَ تَسْلِيمًا كَثِيرًا كَثِيرًا.⟫

May Allah's blessings descend upon them, upon their progeny, with countless and abundantly increasing salutations.

⟪ar-quote:اللَّهُمَّ لَا عِلْمَ لَنَا إِلَّا مَا عَلَّمْتَنَا، إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ الْعَلِيمُ الْحَكِيمُ. اللَّهُمَّ عَلِّمْنَا بِمَا يَنْفَعُنَا، وَانْفَعْنَا بِمَا عَلَّمْتَنَا. اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّا نَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ عِلْمٍ لَا يَنْفَعُ، وَمِنْ قَلْبٍ لَا يَخْشَعُ، وَمِنْ عَيْنٍ لَا تَدْمَعُ، وَمِنْ نَفْسٍ لَا تَشْبَعُ، وَمِنْ دَعْوَةٍ لَا يُسْتَجَابُ لَهَا، وَمِنْ مُنْكَرَاتِ الْأَخْلَاقِ وَالْأَعْمَالِ وَالْأَهْوَاءِ.⟫

O Allah, we possess no knowledge except that which Thou hast taught us; Thou art the All-Knowing, the All-Wise. O Allah, teach us that which benefits us and let us benefit from what Thou dost teach us. O Allah, we seek refuge in Thee from knowledge that profits not, from a heart that fears not, from an eye that weeps not, from a soul that is never satisfied, from a prayer that is not answered, and from base qualities in character, action, and inclination.

⟪ar-quote:فَأَمَّا بَعْدُ، يَقُولُ سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَى فِي كِتَابِهِ الْكَرِيمِ، بَعْدَ أَنْ نَقُولَ أَعُوذُ بِاللهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ، بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ. رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي، وَيَسِّرْ لِي أَمْرِي، وَاحْلُلْ عُقْدَةً مِنْ لِسَانِي، يَفْقَهُوا قَوْلِي.⟫

Now, as to what follows: Thus speaks the Most Exalted in His noble Qurʾān, and I commence by seeking refuge in Allah from the accursed Satan. In the name of Allah, the Compassionate, the Merciful. "My Lord, expand for me my breast, and ease my task, and loosen the knot from my tongue, that they may understand my speech."

⟪ar-quote:اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ أَنْ أَضِلَّ أَوْ أُضَلَّ، أَوْ أَزِلَّ أَوْ أُزَلَّ، أَوْ أَظْلِمَ أَوْ أُظْلَمَ، أَوْ أَجْهَلَ أَوْ يُجْهَلَ عَلَيَّ.⟫

O Allah, I seek refuge in Thee lest I go astray or am led astray, lest I slip or am made to slip, lest I commit injustice or be treated unjustly, lest I act ignorantly or be approached with ignorance.

⟪ar-quote:اللَّهُمَّ ثَبِّتْنَا عِنْدَ الْمَوْتِ بِـ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللهُ. وَاللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنَا مِنَ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ، وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالْحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالصَّبْرِ. آمِينَ يَا رَبَّ الْعَالَمِينَ.⟫

O Allah, establish us at the moment of death upon "There is no god save Allah." O Allah, make us among those who believe and do righteous deeds, and counsel one another toward truth and counsel one another toward patience. Āmīn, O Lord of all the worlds.

---

## Al-Ḥamdu lillāh alladhī Lahu Mulk al-Samawāt wa-l-Arḍ Yuḥyī wa-Yumīt — 'Praise to God who has the Kingdom of the Heavens and Earth, Who gives life and death'

⟪ar-quote:اَلْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ الَّذِي لَهُ مُلْكُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ؛ يُحْيِي وَيُمِيتُ.⟫

All praise belongs to Allah, to whom belongs the dominion of the heavens and the earth; He gives life and He causes death.[^cite-7]

⟪ar-quote:وَهُوَ الْأَوَّلُ وَالْآخِرُ وَالظَّاهِرُ وَالْبَاطِنُ، وَهُوَ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيمٌ.⟫

He is the First and the Last, the Manifest and the Hidden; and He has knowledge of all things.

⟪ar-quote:اَلْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ مُوجِدِ الْوُجُودِ مِنْ عَدَمٍ غَيْرِ مَوْجُودٍ، وَمُقِيمِ مَرَاتِبِ الْحُدُودِ، تَفَضُّلًا وَإِنْعَامًا عَلَى مَنْ دَنَا.⟫

All praise to Allah, who brings existence into being from non-existence, who establishes the ranks of limits and boundaries, through grace and bestowal upon all who draw near.[^cite-8]

⟪ar-quote:اَلْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ الَّذِي أَنْزَلَ الْفُرْقَانَ عَلَى عَبْدِهِ لِيَكُونَ لِلْعَالَمِينَ نَذِيرًا.⟫

All praise to Allah, who sent down the Criterion upon His servant, that it might be a warning to all the worlds.

⟪ar-quote:اَلْحَمْدُ لِلّٰهِ الَّذِي هَدَانَا لِهَٰذَا، وَمَا كُنَّا لِنَهْتَدِيَ لَوْلَا أَنْ هَدَانَا اللّٰهُ.⟫

All praise to Allah, who has guided us to this, and we would not have been guided had Allah not guided us.

⟪ar-quote:وَنَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللّٰهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ، وَنَشْهَدُ أَنَّ سَيِّدَنَا مُحَمَّدًا عَبْدُهُ وَرَسُولُهُ، وَنَشْهَدُ أَنَّ مَوْلَانَا عَلِيًّا وَصِيُّهُ وَوَزِيرُهُ.⟫

And we testify that there is no god save Allah alone, without any partner; and we testify that our master Muḥammad is His servant and His messenger; and we testify that our master ʿAlī is his successor and his minister.

⟪ar-quote:وَالصَّلَاةُ وَالسَّلَامُ عَلَى خَيْرِ الْخَلْقِ وَنُورِ الْعَرْشِ، أَفْضَلِ الْأَنْبِيَاءِ وَالْمُرْسَلِينَ، وَأَشْرَفِ الْمُوَحِّدِينَ، حَبِيبِنَا وَسَيِّدِنَا وَسَنَدِنَا وَشَفِيعِنَا أَبِي الْقَاسِمِ مُحَمَّدٍ.⟫

And blessings and peace upon the best of creation and the light of the Throne, the noblest of prophets and messengers, the most honourable of those who profess Allah's oneness—our beloved, our master, our support, our intercessor, Abū al-Qāsim Muḥammad.

⟪ar-quote:وَعَلَى أَخِيهِ وَوَصِيِّهِ وَوَزِيرِهِ، أَمِيرِ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ، الْأَنْزَعِ الْبَطِينِ، وَيَعْسُوبِ الدِّينِ، غَالِبٍ عَلَى كُلِّ غَالِبٍ، مَطْلُوبِ كُلِّ طَالِبٍ، مَوْلَانَا عَلِيِّ بْنِ أَبِي طَالِبٍ.⟫

And upon his brother, successor, and minister, the Commander of the Faithful, highest in rank and deepest in knowledge, the guardian of the faith, victor over all victors, the ultimate aspiration of every seeker—our master ʿAlī son of Abū Ṭālib.

⟪ar-quote:وَعَلَى الْأَئِمَّةِ مِنْ ذُرِّيَّتِهِمُ الطَّيِّبِينَ الطَّاهِرِينَ، اَلْخُلَفَاءِ الرَّاشِدِينَ، آلِ طه وَيٰس.⟫

And upon the Imāms from their noble and pure progeny, the rightly-guided successors, the people of Ṭāhā and Yāsīn.

⟪ar-quote:اَللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ فِي كُلِّ وَقْتٍ وَكُلِّ أَوَانٍ وَعَلَى كُلِّ حَالٍ؛ عَدَدَ مَا صَلَّيْتَ عَلَى مَنْ صَلَّيْتَ عَلَيْهِ؛ وَأَضْعَافَ ذَلِكَ كُلِّهِ بِالْأَضْعَافِ الَّتِي لَا يُحْصِيهَا غَيْرُكَ.⟫

O Allah, bless Muḥammad and the family of Muḥammad at every moment and every time and in every state—a blessing equal in number to all those upon whom Thou hast blessed, and multiplied by multiplicities that none but Thou can count.

⟪ar-quote:اَللّٰهُمَّ لَا عِلْمَ لَنَا إِلَّا مَا عَلَّمْتَنَا، إِنَّكَ أَنْتَ الْعَلِيمُ الْحَكِيمُ. اَللّٰهُمَّ عَلِّمْنَا بِمَا يَنْفَعُنَا، وَانْفَعْنَا بِمَا عَلَّمْتَنَا. اَللَّهُمَّ إِنَّا نَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ عِلْمٍ لَا يَنْفَعُ، وَمِنْ قَلْبٍ لَا يَخْشَعُ، وَمِنْ عَيْنٍ لَا تَدْمَعُ، وَمِنْ نَفْسٍ لَا تَشْبَعُ، وَمِنْ دَعْوَةٍ لَا يُسْتَجَابُ لَهَا، وَمِنْ مُنْكَرَاتِ الْأَخْلَاقِ وَالْأَعْمَالِ وَالْأَهْوَاءِ.⟫

O Allah, we possess no knowledge except that which Thou hast taught us; Thou art the All-Knowing, the All-Wise. O Allah, teach us that which benefits us, and let us benefit from what Thou dost teach us. O Allah, we seek refuge in Thee from knowledge that profits not, from a heart that reveres not, from an eye that weeps not, from a soul that is never satisfied, from a prayer that goes unanswered, and from detestable qualities in character, deed, and desire.

⟪ar-quote:فَأَمَّا بَعْدُ، يَقُولُ سُبْحَانَهُ وَتَعَالَى فِي كِتَابِهِ الْكَرِيمِ، بَعْدَ أَنْ نَقُولَ أَعُوذُ بِاللهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ، بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ.⟫

Now, as for what follows: Thus speaks the Most Exalted in His noble Qurʾān, and I commence by seeking refuge in Allah from the accursed Satan. In the name of Allah, the Compassionate, the Merciful.

⟪ar-quote:رَبِّ اشْرَحْ لِي صَدْرِي، وَيَسِّرْ لِي أَمْرِي، وَاحْلُلْ عُقْدَةً مِنْ لِسَانِي، يَفْقَهُوا قَوْلِي.⟫

"My Lord, expand for me my breast, ease for me my task, and loosen the knot from my tongue, that they may understand my speech."

⟪ar-quote:رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا. رَبِّ يَسِّرْ وَلَا تُعَسِّرْ وَتَمِّمْ بِالْخَيْرِ.⟫

"My Lord, increase me in knowledge. My Lord, make easy and do not make difficult, and complete with goodness."

⟪ar-quote:اَللَّهُمَّ ثَبِّتْنَا عِنْدَ الْمَوْتِ بِلَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ، وَاللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنَا مِنَ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ، وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالْحَقِّ وَتَوَاصَوْا بِالصَّبْرِ. آمِينَ يَا رَبَّ الْعَالَمِينَ.⟫

O Allah, establish us at the moment of death upon "There is no god save Allah," and O Allah, make us among those who believe and do righteous deeds, and counsel one another toward truth and counsel one another toward patience. Āmīn, O Lord of all the worlds.

## Al-Ḥamdu lillāh Ṣāniʿ al-Khalq ʿalā Akmal Niẓām — 'Praise to God, Maker of Creation in the Most Perfect Order'

⟪ar-quote:الحمد للہِ صَانِعِ الخَلقِ عَلی أکمَلِ نِظَام وَ أَحسِنِ قَوام⟫

All praise belongs to Allah, who fashioned creation according to the most perfect order and the most beautiful constitution.

⟪ar-quote:الواَضِعِ الاَشیَاءَ فی أَشْدَرِ مُقَام و أرْفَقِ مَصاَم⟫

He placed all things in their most secure position and most harmonious arrangement.[^cite-9]

⟪ar-quote:والصلواۃ و السلام علی المُؤَیِّدِ أَبلَغِ الکَلام⟫

And blessings and peace upon him who was strengthened with the most eloquent speech—

⟪ar-quote:مُحمدٍ المَبعُوثْ صلی اللہ علیہ و علی آلہ و سلَّم⟫

upon Muḥammad, the Sent One, upon whom and upon his family be the blessings of Allah and peace.

⟪ar-quote:لِھِدایَۃِ کَافَّۃِ أَنَام اِلی سُبُلِ السَّلام و الی مَدَیٰ الَّیاَلِ وَ الأَیَّامْ⟫

He was sent for the guidance of all humanity toward paths of peace, extending through the span of nights and days.[^cite-10]

⟪ar-quote:الذی أَرسَلَہُ اللہُ تَعاَلی بِالھُدَی وَ دِینِ الَحقِّ لِیُظھِرَہُ عَلی الدِّینِ کُلِّہ ، وَ کفَا بِاللہِ شَھِیدَا⟫

Allah the Most High sent him bearing guidance and the true religion, that He might make it prevail over all other religions; and Allah suffices as a witness thereto.

And we testify that there is no god save Allah alone, without any partner in His dominion, and that all creation glorifies Him with the greatest glorification.

⟪ar-quote:و نشھد ان محمد عبدُ اللہ و رسولہ⟫

And we testify that Muḥammad is the servant of Allah and His messenger.

⟪ar-quote:و نشھد ان مولانا علیاً ، وصیہ و وزیرہ⟫

And we testify that our master ʿAlī is his successor and his minister.

⟪ar-quote:فصل اللہ علیہم و علی آلہم و سَلِّمُھُم تَسلِیماً کثیراً کثیرا⟫

May Allah's blessings descend upon them and upon their progeny, with abundant and ever-increasing salutations.

⟪ar-quote:اللّٰھم لا عِلمَ لَنا اِلَّا ما عَلَّمتَنَا۔ أِنَّک أَنتَ العَلِیمُ الحکیم⟫

O Allah, we possess no knowledge except that which Thou hast taught us. Verily, Thou art the All-Knowing, the All-Wise.

⟪ar-quote:اللّٰھم عَلِّمنَا بِما یَنفَعْنَا ؛ وَ یَنفَعنَا بِمَا عَلَّمتَنَا⟫

O Allah, teach us that which benefits us, and let us benefit from what Thou dost teach us.

⟪ar-quote:اللھم آتنا نُفُوسِنَا تَقوَاھا وَ زَکَّنا فأنت خیرُ من زَکَّاھا۔⟫

O Allah, grant our souls their reverence, and purify them, for Thou art the best of those who purify.

⟪ar-quote:أنت وَلِیُّنا و مولانا و نعم المولی و نعم النصیر۔⟫

Thou art our protector and our guardian; excellent is the protector and excellent is the helper.

⟪ar-quote:ولا حول ولا قوۃ الا باللہ العلی العظیم⟫

There is no power nor strength save in Allah, the Most High, the Most Great.

⟪ar-quote:فأما بعد ، یقول سبحانہ و تعالی فی کتابہ الکریم ، بعد أَقولُ أعوذ باللہ من الشیطان الرجیم بسم اللہ الرحمن الرحیم⟫

Now, as for what follows: Thus speaks the Most Exalted in His noble Qurʾān, and I commence by seeking refuge in Allah from the accursed Satan. In the name of Allah, the Compassionate, the Merciful.

⟪ar-quote:رب اشرحلی صدری و یسرلی أمری ۔ وحلل عقدۃ من لسانی یفقھو قولی⟫

"My Lord, expand for me my breast, and ease for me my task, and loosen the knot from my tongue that they may understand my speech."

⟪ar-quote:اللہم ثَبِّتنا عِندَ الموتی بی لا الہ الا اللہ و اللھم جعلنا من الذین آمنو و عمل الصالحات و تواصو بالحق وتواصو بالصبر⟫

O Allah, establish us at the moment of death upon the belief that there is no god save Allah. O Allah, make us among those who believe and do righteous deeds, and counsel one another toward truth and counsel one another toward patience.

⟪ar-quote:آمین یا رب العلمین⟫

Āmīn, O Lord of all the worlds.
