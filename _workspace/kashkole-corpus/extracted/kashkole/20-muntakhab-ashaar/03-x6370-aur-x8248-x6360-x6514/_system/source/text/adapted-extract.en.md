## Ay Ḥusayn — O Ḥusayn! (Urdu marsiya, fully extracted)

Every breath rises from your breath, O Ḥusayn!
The warmth of your pulse moves still, O Ḥusayn!

You have led us to the very summit—
For this we owe you thanks, O Ḥusayn!

The trial of thirst and water—
You endured it to its limit, O Ḥusayn!

Your steadfastness emerged with the same intensity
as the secret of the covenant pledged to you, O Ḥusayn!

Your slayer is the slayer of the Prophet of Islam;
your blood is, in truth, the Prophet's blood, O Ḥusayn!

Every kingdom upon the earth shall fall—
until the Day of Judgement, O Ḥusayn!
