## Ay Ḥusayn — O Ḥusayn!

Thy breath has risen from thy breath, O Ḥusayn! The heat of thy pulse courses through us with efficacy, O Ḥusayn. Thou hast guided us toward the pinnacle of understanding; we render thanks unto thee, O Ḥusayn.

The question of nature and water—perhaps thou hast tested its depths, O Ḥusayn! Thy perseverance emerged with equal intensity, mirroring the secret oath of allegiance sworn unto thee, O Ḥusayn. Thy slayer stands as the murderer of the Prophet of Islam; thy blood is in truth the blood of the Prophet himself, O Ḥusayn.[^cite-1] Every dominion shall crumble in this world, until the Day of Judgment arrives, O Ḥusayn!
