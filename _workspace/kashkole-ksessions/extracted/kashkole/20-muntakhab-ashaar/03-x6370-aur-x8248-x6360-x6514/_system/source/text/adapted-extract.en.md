## Ay Ḥusayn — O Ḥusayn!

Thy breath has risen from thy breath, O Ḥusayn! The heat of thy pulse remains ever efficacious, O Ḥusayn! Thou hast guided us unto the pinnacle of understanding, and for this we render thanks, O noble one, O Ḥusayn![^cite-1]

The question of Nature and Water presents itself before thee. Thou mayest have tested it, O Ḥusayn! Thy perseverance hath emerged with the same intensity, even as the secret of the oath of allegiance to thee remaineth steadfast, O Ḥusayn![^cite-2]

Thy murderer is the murderer of the Prophet of Islam; thy blood is verily the blood of the Prophet himself, O Ḥusayn! Every government shall fall in this world until the Day of Judgment, O Ḥusayn![^cite-3]
