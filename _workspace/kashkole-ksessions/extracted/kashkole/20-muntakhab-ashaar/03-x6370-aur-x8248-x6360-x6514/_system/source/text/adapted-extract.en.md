## Ay Ḥusayn — O Ḥusayn!

Thy breath has risen from thy breath, O Ḥusayn!
The heat of thy pulse is effective, O Ḥusayn!

Thou hast guided us to the pinnacle;
We render thanks unto thee, O man of virtue, O Ḥusayn![^cite-1]

The Question of Nature and Water—
Thou may have proved it, O Ḥusayn!
Thy perseverance emerged with the same intensity,
Even as the secret of the oath of allegiance to thee, O Ḥusayn!

Thy killer is the murderer of the Prophet of Islam;
Thy blood is in truth the blood of the Prophet, O Ḥusayn![^cite-2]
Every dominion shall fall in this world,
Until the Day of Judgment, O Ḥusayn!
