## Their Error—a Momentary Slip

Along with Asher's repentance, a group offered their repentance which ⟪ar:تاَبُو بِتَوبَتِہِ⟫was called. This was followed by the feast of Asher (⟪ar:تابو بتوبتہ⟫the last person in the circle), which led to another group called ⟪ar:لب النداہ⟫the Brotherhood. All three⟪ar:عاشر، تابو بتوبتہ، لبو النداہ⟫ of them achieved perfection. He invited the disobedient group and called them to the truth and forbade them from disobeying Allah. All of them denied it and were arrogant, which made their souls unhappy.

When the ten intellects had attained perfection with their circles, the first intellect (al-ʿAql al-Awwal) knew through its omniscience that these disobedient groups would no longer come from their stubbornness. Therefore, the first intellect decided to remove this disobedient group from this spiritual realm and ordered for ⟪ar:اھبطو⟫ them.[^cite-1]

> ⟪quran 7:24⟫

> *(Allah) said: "Get down, one of you an enemy to the other [i.e.&nbsp;Adam, Hawwa (Eve), and Shaitan (Satan), etc.]. &nbsp;On earth will be a dwelling&shy;place for you and an enjoyment, - for a time." *

This command proceeded from the first intellect and passed through all the intellectual circles (dawāʾir ʿaqliyya) to Ashir (⟪ar:لب الندا⟫the last person). It was incumbent upon him to instruct this disobedient group to achieve perfection again. Although the second source did not mislead anyone from the invitation, if he ⟪ar:وحم مساوات⟫did not do so, the whole group would not have been corrupted. Because of this, and according to the justice of Allah, Asher was commanded to release them because he had become their chief because he had caused them to go astray. ## Formation of the World of Life

The group hesitated to accept Asher's invitation and refused to accept it, but ⟪ar:اھبطو⟫repented before his order could be carried out. Here came a very delicate moment for Asher (⟪ar:لب النداہ⟫the last person in his circle) to determine what he could do with the group so that they would be punished or not. He looked at those above him with longing and asked them for help and succour, as it is said in the Qur'an:

> ⟪quran 1:5⟫
> ⟪ar:‏إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ ‎⟫
> *You (Alone) we worship, and You (Alone) we ask for help (for each and everything).*
> guide us to the straight path.
> — **Quran 1:5 (inline widget)**

All the intellects above expressed their views and favours upon Ashir and suggested to him that he should not be allowed to live in the spiritual world nor be reduced to the physical world, but that a place should be made for him between the spiritual and physical worlds and that he should be placed there.[^cite-4] In this regard, the hadith of the Prophet (upon him be peace) indicates in its most profound sense: ⟪ar-quote:خیر أمور اوسطھا⟫ "The best of all matters is the middle of it."

Thus his group ⟪ar:کلمم⟫was not overwhelmed, and he was not allowed to fall into the physical world. But since ⟪ar:لب انداہ⟫he heeded the invitation and sinned, he was no longer able to dwell in the spiritual realm—the world of spiritual perfection where there is neither possibility nor existence of sin. Therefore, the group ⟪ar:کلمم⟫was thrown out of there and placed between the spiritual world and the physical world, which ⟪ar:عالم حیات⟫is called.[^cite-5] This place has also been referred to by several other names: ⟪ar:تامور⟫, ⟪ar:کعبۃ الروحانی⟫, ⟪ar:مرکز⟫, ⟪ar:باب عالم قدس⟫, ⟪ar:قلب روحانی⟫, ⟪ar:عالم محتجبین⟫.

All the members of his group ⟪ar:کلمم⟫were remorseful and there was no fault in anyone's remorse, but they did not attain perfection. Although it was possible for him to be included in the realm of reason (al-ʿAql al-Awwal) because of the perfection of his remorse, it so happened that when he denied for a moment, some people looked at him, accepted his influence, and deteriorated. It was only because of this that they did not achieve perfection. The same principle was affixed to them as it had been applied to the intellect of Ashir. ⟪ar:من کسر عظم فعلیہ جبر⟫ Now it is incumbent upon these people ⟪ar:کلمم⟫to reform those who have been corrupted by seeing them.

## The Vertical Light (al-ʿAmūd al-Nūr)

In fact, the vertical light (al-ʿamūd al-nūr) is another one that has been passed from the Creator to the First Intellect. Whoever came into contact with this light actually succeeded. Then from the first intellect, this light connected with the second intellect and then descended from the seven intellects and their circles to Asher, who has been entrusted with the command of the world of physics and who is in the position of the first intellect in the world of nature.[^cite-10] All of them are called the pure and abstracted intellects (ʿuqūl al-abdāʾiyya al-mujarrada). Then it connects with the light of Ashir to every place that emanates from the world of nature, which is the Speaker (nāṭiq), the Foundation (asās), and the Imam (imām). And then it connects to his Gate (Bāb). The Imam's Gate is in the place of the Imam in the world because it is made there. And then this vertical light is related from the Gate to the Proof (Ḥujja), from the Proof to the Summoner (dāʿī), from the Summoner to the Helper (ma'dhūn), from the Helper to the Believers (muʾminīn), and from the Believers to the Respondents (mustajīb) while he takes the Noble Covenant.

For the vertical light, it is said that that light is all and the knowledge is continuous, it is the soul, the word—the light of the declaration of divine oneness (nūr kalimmat lā ilāha illā allāh). All the spiritual and physical boundaries are connected to this vertical light (from the first intellect to the believer) and are connected to it. An example of this is a long wooden pole with honey on it, and when the fly comes on it, it never leaves it. In the same way, the forms (with which the souls are related) never leave this vertical light. It is a honeycomb from which all the bees (believers) adhere. Everybody comes and connects to it. Whoever comes into contact with this vertical light succeeds and becomes one of the people of mercy. It is much higher and superior than the light of the sun (nūr al-shams). The sun's light, despite its intensity and heat, is dense and enveloped in physicality. There is no density in the vertical light, which is why it came into contact with the first intellect. We know the Creator because of this light. This light does not fall on our bodies, but upon the rational soul (nafs al-nāṭiqa). The same is also called the divine care (ʿināya ilāhiyya) or another divine power (qudrat ilāhiyya).

The intellect (the tenth of the circle) moves this vertical light. And this movement is connected to the Imam of that time; he understands that the Imam is trying to make a believer die. That is, the Imam indicates that the believer's connection has been purified and he is now absorbed into the vertical light. And then the Imam moves the vertical light, through which the form of the believer (nafs al-nāṭiqa + nafs al-ḥassiyya) is separated from his body and absorbed into the vertical light.

### Developing the Soul of the Believers (Tatawwur Nafs al-Muʾminīn)

Thus the vertical light absorbs the forms and conveys them to their forms. It is a magnet that holds the form and the soul together. That is, when the form rises, it also develops with the sensible soul (nafs al-ḥassiyya), and it also progresses at a higher level in the vegetative soul (nafs al-nāmiyya). The vertical light combines this form and soul.

The part of the body that has evolved into a form can no longer be corrupted because it has been attached to it by the rational soul (al-nāṭiqa). If that form goes to a living limit and that upper limit is destroyed, then the more pure the form of that upper limit is purified, the vertical light will take it away and the form will be separated from it. The rest of it is spoiled. When David (al-Quṭb) Hamam's intentions failed, he pulled his present form with the help of a vertical light and gave him for training. After that, David al-Quṭb deteriorated. In the same way, when it deteriorated, Maulana Ali drew his face with the help of a vertical light and gave it to his son for training.

### The Provider of Provision to the Believers

It is also the work of the vertical light to deliver his physical sustenance to man. The vertical light draws man's sustenance and delivers it to him, and protects every part of that sustenance (for the one who eats it). He makes it possible that no one else eats the goods of a believer, but that the believer receives what he has been brought into this world. When a goat is slaughtered, it has several parts and a goat is eaten by many people together. The vertical light conveys every part and every piece of this goat to the person whose body it has come to be.

### The One Who Sanctifies the Water of Ablution

In the same way, the water of ablution (istinjāʾ) that reaches a person—it reaches his body during ablution—also delivers the vertical light. As soon as the invocation "In the name of Allah, the Merciful, the Compassionate" (bismi-llāh al-raḥmān al-raḥīm) is said, a person's relationship with the vertical light is established. Otherwise, even though it's our thing, it takes a while to reach us. Therefore, it is very important to say everything at the beginning with "In the name of Allah" and without it, ablution is not acceptable. At the same time, the relationship with the vertical light is established and its effect begins.

### The Protector of the Believers' Virtues

The vertical light is the protector of the virtues of the saints of Allah (the lover of Allah or the leaven of the guardianship of spiritual kinship) and the one who takes them to special places. In the same way, when a person is buried after death, his components are dispersed in fire, air, water, and soil, but after a thousand years, the same vertical light reconnects these components from the community. That's what it's called by the divine determinations (muqaddirāt ilāhiyya). Verticals are working for all things—the forms (ṣūrat), the benefits (faḍlāt), and the essences (rīḥiyyāt).

### The Leaven of the Believers (Khamīrat al-Nadāma)

All the believers are preserved in this vertical light as the leaven of remorse (khamīrat al-nadāma). When a believer takes the pledge of the Imam of the Age (imām al-zamān), he is moved in the vertical light and comes into contact with the believer's soul. This is the name of the connection through the rational soul (nafs al-nāṭiqa).

### When Does the Relationship with the Vertical Light Break?

When one of the boundaries or circles is made intimate (through ritual impurity), the vertical light reverses from it and separates. It is said in the supplication that when a person has intercourse, the angels of Allah turn away from him until he returns to a state of purity. Intimacy breaks the connection with the intimacy, which also breaks the connection with the vertical light. As soon as a person takes a bath (ghusl), he is re-connected to the vertical light through the rational soul (nafs al-nāṭiqa).

As soon as the genitalia (fraj) is naked, the connection with the vertical light is broken, and the angels turn away. The hair on a woman's head is included in her private parts, so it is permissible to cover her hair while praying. When Ali proceeded to kill him at the Battle of the Camel (jaṅg-i Siffīn), he opened his private parts to save his life. At that moment, he turned his gaze.

### The Calculation of Limits (Ḥesāb al-Ḥudūd wa-l-Ḥudūd)

The vertical light is in the form of a prayer-beads (tasbīḥ) in which all the levels are connected to each other. The Summoner (dāʿī) is connected to the Proof (ḥujja), the Proof is connected to the Gate (bāb), and the Gate is connected to the Imam. When a believer dies, the vertical light draws his face and places it in the right place of the prayer-beads, and this is called Paradise. The paradise of the Summoner is different, the paradise of the Proof is different, and the paradise of the Gate is different. When the number in one Paradise is complete, then in the other Paradise there is a renaissance. That is, when the number is completed in the Paradise of the Proof (the tenth rank is attained), you ⟪ar:تلک عشرۃٌ کاملۃ⟫ enter the Paradise of the Gate of Gates (bāb al-abwāb). And the vertical light that keeps track of all these degrees and developments is the Light.
