### Cannot tell the owner what to do

Now let's understand the implications of the word OWNER. When someone is Malik. when someone OWNS property. he can do whatever he wants with that property. No one has the right to question that owner about his decisions regarding that property. Let's look at an example to understand this better.

Annu khala and Ali Khaloo own a house in Joplin. That house. and everything WITHIN that house is THEIR property. Which means they can make whatever decisions they want about that house. They can paint the walls whatever color they choose. They can decorate it any way they want. They can put things in whatever places they think works BEST for them. They can also discard and throw away whatever they want. Right?.

In fact they make me feel SO comfortable. that very soon I forget that I am only a guest in their house. I actually start behaving like I OWN the house. YAUM

Next is the word Yaum. which is quite an interesting word in Arabic.Generally the word YAUM is translated as a Day. But linguistically. YAUM does not actually mean 24 hours. It simply means a time interval. So a YAUM could be used for a moment. it could mean an hour. it could mean a day. a month. a year. and it could also mean several thousand years. It is simply a duration of time allocated for a specific purpose.
⟪ar-quote:تَعْرُجُ ٱلْمَلَٓئِكَةُ وَٱلرُّوحُ إِلَيْهِ فِى يَوْمٍۢ كَانَ مِقْدَارُهُ خَمْسِينَ أَلْفَ سَنَةٍۢ⟫

The angels and the Spirit will ascend to Him during a Day.  the extent of which is fifty thousand years. He says that YAUM will be 50,000 years. In Surah Muzammil he says.
⟪ar-quote:يَوْمَ تَرْجُفُ ٱلْأَرْضُ وَٱلْجِبَالُ. وَكَانَتِ ٱلْجِبَالُ كَثِيبًۭا مَّهِيلًا ‎⟫

That **moment.**when the earth and the mountains will convulse.  and mountains will become like a heap of sand being poured down.  Here it is used for a moment. So the word YAUM is used for various periods of time. including TIME itself.

Deen

Now the word DEEN. The word DEEN in the Arabic language comes from the root letters. DA YAA NAA.  ⟪ar:دَیْن⟫. which has several meanings. One of the meanings. is a type of RAIN. that falls again and again. until it brings the dead earth back to life. 

Another meaning. is to discipline something. ⟪ar:من دان نفسہ⟫.  whoever disciplines his soul.

It also means. A way of life.⟪ar:لکم دینکم ولیدین⟫. You have your way of life and we have ours.

But the root meaning of the word ⟪ar:دَیْن⟫. is actually DEBT. It means a DEBT. And all other meanings that I just mentioned. they all trace their meaning from this meaning of debt. So the rain keeps coming back to the earth to bring it back to life. because that is what it OWES the earth. The rain is fulfilling its debt to the earth. We OWE it to our souls. to discipline it. The true  way of life. IS to repay your debts.  So all the various  derivatives of this word are based on the meaning of  ⟪ar:دَیْن⟫. debt. 

And our religion is called.  ⟪ar:دینُ الاسلام⟫. The deen of Islam. Literally this can be translated as.  the DEBT of submission. In other words the idea is. we have been endowed or given uncountable blessings by our Rabb.  and so the DEBT that we owe. is submission to the Creator.  Allah says in the Quran
