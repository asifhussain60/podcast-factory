# The Holy Quran and Al-Furqan Al-Hamid
*Source: KASHKOLE, Binder 8 (Interpretations of the Divine Words), Chapter 132. 2 topics. Language: ur.*<!-- section 1 (id=2486, raw_sort=1): قرآن الکریم -->The word is derived from the weight of the Qur'an ⟪ar:’قرآن‘⟫ (the word from which actions and attributes are formed). It means to read aloud. Since the day it was revealed, it has been recited continuously and its recitation continues in the world for 24 hours and will continue until the Day of Judgment.

The term "Qur'an" refers to a book written by divine revelation, which was revealed in Arabic by Gabriel Amin to the blessed heart of Prophet Muhammad Mustafa (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him), as Allaah says:

> ⟪quran 26:193-195⟫
> ⟪ar:‏نَزَلَ بِهِ ٱلرُّوحُ ٱلْأَمِينُ ‎ ‏عَلَىٰ قَلْبِكَ لِتَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلْمُنذِرِينَ ‎ ‏بِلِسَانٍ عَرَبِىٍّۢ مُّبِينٍۢ ‎⟫
> *Which the trustworthy Ruh [Jibrael (Gabriel)] has brought down; Upon your heart (O Muhammad PBUH) that you may be (one) of the warners, In the plain Arabic language.*
> the angel trustworthy has come to your heart, so that you may be of the warners, in a clear Arabic language.
> — **Quran 26:193-195 (inline widget)**

The Qur'an is the name of its combination of words and meanings. The Qur'an is what was revealed in Arabic. It will not be translated into any language, it will not be called the Qur'an. This is the reason why Arabic verses of the Qur'an have been made obligatory in prayer, and if the translation of these verses is recited in any language instead, then the prayer is not permissible.

It is also called the book ⟪ar:’فرقان‘⟫ which means the one who makes a difference, as he said:

> ⟪quran 25:1⟫
> ⟪ar:‏تَبَارَكَ ٱلَّذِى نَزَّلَ ٱلْفُرْقَانَ عَلَىٰ عَبْدِهِۦ لِيَكُونَ لِلْعَلَمِينَ نَذِيرًا ‎⟫
> *Blessed be He Who sent down the criterion (of right and wrong, i.e. this Quran) to His slave (Muhammad PBUH) that he may be a warner to the 'Alamîn (mankind and jinns).*
> Blessed is He who has sent down the Qur'an to His servant, that he may be a warner to the worlds.
> — **Quran 25:1 (inline widget)**<!-- section 2 (id=2487, raw_sort=2): نزول قرآن -->The meaning of revelation is to be revealed. ### Duration of the Revelation of the Qur'an:

Allaah says (interpretation of the meaning): "We have sent down this Qur'an in the month of Ramadan:

> ⟪quran 2:185⟫
> ⟪ar:‏شَهْرُ رَمَضَانَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أُنزِلَ فِيهِ ٱلْقُرْآنُ هُدًۭى لِّلنَّاسِ وَبَيِّنَتٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلْهُدَىٰ وَٱلْفُرْقَانِ ۚ فَمَن شَهِدَ مِنكُمُ ٱلشَّهْرَ فَلْيَصُمْهُ ۖ وَمَن كَانَ مَرِيضًا أَوْ عَلَىٰ سَفَرٍۢ فَعِدَّةٌۭ مِّنْ أَيَّامٍ أُخَرَ ۗ يُرِيدُ ٱللَّهُ بِكُمُ ٱلْيُسْرَ وَلَا يُرِيدُ بِكُمُ ٱلْعُسْرَ وَلِتُكْمِلُوا۟ ٱلْعِدَّةَ وَلِتُكَبِّرُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ عَلَىٰ مَا هَدَىٰكُمْ وَلَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ ‎⟫
> *The month of Ramadan in which was revealed the Quran, a guidance for mankind and clear proofs for the guidance and the criterion (between right and wrong). So whoever of you sights (the crescent on the first night of) the month (of Ramadan i.e. is present at his home), he must observe Saum (fasts) that month, and whoever is ill or on a journey, the same number [of days which one did not observe Saum (fasts) must be made up] from other days. Allah intends for you ease, and He does not want to make things difficult for you. (He wants that you) must complete the same number (of days), and that you must magnify Allah [i.e. to say Takbîr (Allahu-Akbar; Allah is the Most Great) on seeing the crescent of the months of Ramadan and Shawwal] for having guided you so that you may be grateful to Him.*
> is the month of Ramadan in which the Qur'an was revealed, a guidance for mankind and clear proofs of guidance, and a criterion between right and wrong. That you may complete the reckoning, and that you may magnify Allah for that He has guided you, and that you may be grateful.
> — **Quran 2:185 (inline widget)**

He further explained, "We sent down this Qur'an in ⟪ar:’لیلۃ القدر‘⟫ the month of Ramadan:

> ⟪quran 97:1⟫
> ⟪ar:‏إِنَّآ أَنزَلْنَهُ فِى لَيْلَةِ ٱلْقَدْرِ ‎⟫
> *Verily! We have sent it (this Quran) down in the night of Al-Qadr (Decree)*
> indeed We have sent it down on the Night of Destiny.
> — **Quran 97:1 (inline widget)**

History tells us that the entire Qur'an was not revealed in 30 days of the city of Allah, but it was revealed slowly to the heart of the Prophet in 23 years. The fact is that the pen (the first intellect) wrote down the entire meaning of the Qur'an on the preserved tablet in the night of Qadr in the city of Ramadan, which is the 23rd night of this month. Then Allah revealed these meanings slowly to the pure heart of the Messenger of Allah (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) over a period of 23 years, and he continued to translate them into words.

The main theme of the Qur'an is man. All its statements are for the welfare of man in this world and the hereafter.
