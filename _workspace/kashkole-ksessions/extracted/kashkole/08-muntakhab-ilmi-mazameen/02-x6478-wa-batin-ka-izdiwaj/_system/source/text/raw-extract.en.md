Tawil is the knowledge of the beings and limits created by the Creator of the Subhana, to know their ranks and to make them wilayat, and to be the Creator's monotheism in comparison to them is the truth. The more we go beyond the circles of interpretation, the closer we get to the Creator (or the Truth).

⟪ar:دور طیبی⟫ The sciences that used to be facts in the⟪ar:سیدنا سلیمان صاحب⟫ past have the status of interpretation today⟪ar:دور قائمی⟫. What is understood is called interpretation and what is not yet understood and the quest to find it is called facts.

Humility in worship is, in fact, the real worship. There will come a time when we are compelled to adopt because of our humility⟪ar:’سکوت‘⟫. Where there is silence, the parables begin. We cannot reach Allah, so we resort to His signs and His names. Although worship is for Allah alone, we can worship Him only through these examples.
