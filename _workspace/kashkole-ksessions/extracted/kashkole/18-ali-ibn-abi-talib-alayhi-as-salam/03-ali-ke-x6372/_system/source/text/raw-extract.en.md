#Ali's Letters
*Source: KASHKOLE, Binder 6 (Ali ibn Abi Talib), Chapter 47. 3 topics. Language: ur.*<!-- section 1 (id=474, raw_sort=1): مالک اشتر کے نام خط -->He gave this letter ⟪ar:مولانا علی ابن ابی طالب علیہ السلام⟫⟪ar:مالک اشتر⟫to him when he made him the ruler of Egypt and sent him to Kufa:

O Lord! The ruler of Egypt should crush his own desires and subdue them with the sword of gratitude, because the soul commands evil until the merciful servant of the Lord joins the weak. Owner! I am sending you to a region where different rulers have ruled. They will look at your affairs as you see the deeds of your predecessors. Your most beloved repository should be righteous deeds. Hold back your desires! Beware of spending one's soul on that which is not permissible. This miserliness is justice for him. Make love and affection for your subjects the hallmark of your heart. Do not be like a beast for them, which knows that it is a boon to tear them apart.

There are two types of people. Either they are your religious brothers, or they are God's creatures like you. They can also lead to mistakes. Forgive them as you would like your Lord to forgive your sins. For forgiving the fault of his subjects, his own guardian is more punished than others. Wherefore thou shalt conceal their sins as much as ye can, that your sins may be covered.<!-- section 2 (id=463, raw_sort=2): حضرت سلمان فارسی کے نام خط -->The parable of the world is like a serpent , which is gentle to touch , but full of poison that kills . Turn away from the things of this world that you like, for very few things in this world will go with you. Keep the desires of the world away from you while also acknowledging that you have abandoned it. Fear the harm that comes from falling in love with the world! Because whoever is engrossed in the desire of the world, this world throws him into hardship and turns his desires into fear and terror.

Do you not see that the conditions of the people of the world change morning and evening? Someone dies and people cry over it! Someone is alive and he's getting sick! There's no trouble! Someone is looking for the world, but death is looking for him! A person who is ignorant is in negligence and does not think that those who take account of him are not heedless. And the previous ones are following in the footsteps of the ancients.

Everyone in the world is engrossed in the hope of the coming day and wants tomorrow to come sooner so that there will be a better situation for him. He doesn't care about the passage of time and the shortening of lifespan. Not a leaf or even a thorn is useless in the garden of the world. Even the worst man has been created for one purpose or the other. It's a different matter that you don't understand this secret.

Compiled by: Late Haj Mullah Ahmad Khurshid Hassan
