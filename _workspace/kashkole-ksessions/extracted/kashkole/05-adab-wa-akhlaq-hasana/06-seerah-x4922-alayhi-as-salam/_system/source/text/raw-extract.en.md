# Biography of the Prophet (peace be upon him)
*Source: KASHKOLE, Binder 27 (Etiquette and Ethics), Chapter 140. 1 topics. Language: ur.*<!-- section 1 (id=3574, raw_sort=1): طفیل بن عامر کا اسلام -->The disbelievers and hypocrites of Mecca had made it known about the Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him) that he was a great magician. Whoever hears your word becomes your slave. ⟪ar:طفیل بن عامر طوسی⟫He who was the great leader of his people, narrates that when he came to Mecca, the people said the same thing to him. They took precautionary measures and put cotton in their ears so that the voice of the Messenger of Allah could not be heard in their ears even by mistake.

While circumambulating, he saw a very handsome man standing and praying and reciting aloud. Tufail asked the people, "Who is this?" He replied, "This is the magician we warned you about." "When I saw his face, I thought it couldn't be the magician's face," Tufail says. Then they thought that I am the leader of my people, not a foolish person so that anyone's words can affect me. I listen to them. If it's right, I'll accept it, and if it's wrong, I'll reject it. Thinking this, he took out the cotton from his ears and sat near the Messenger of Allah. When the Messenger of Allah (swt) finished his prayer, he asked him:
⟪ar-quote:یا محمد! ما عندک؟⟫ "What do you have, O Muhammad?"
He ⟪ar:(ﷺ)⟫ said:
⟪ar-quote:أِنَّ الحَمدَ لِی اللہَ أحمَدَہُ وَ أستَعِینَہُ وَ أومِنُ بِہِ وَ أتَوَکَّلُ عَلَیہِ ۔ مَن یَّھدِہِ اللہُ فَلاَ مُضِلَّ لَہ وَ مَن یَّضِلَّ لَہُ فَلاَ ھَادِیَ لَہ⟫ "I have the praise of Allah, with whom I praise Him, and seek His help, in Him I believe, and in Him I trust." Whomsoever Allah guides, he never goes astray, and whomsoever He sends astray, he is never guided.

When Tufail heard this, he trembled and said spontaneously:

⟪ar-quote:أعد أعد! فقد بلغنا قاموس البحر⟫
*Say it again! Say it again! This one sentence of yours has drunk the seven seas!*

He then repeated his statement:

⟪ar-quote:أِنَّ الحَمدَ لِی اللہَ أحمَدَہُ وَ أستَعِینَہُ وَ أومِنُ بِہِ وَ أتَوَکَّلُ عَلَیہِ ۔ مَن یَّھدِہِ اللہُ فَلاَ مُضِلَّ لَہ وَ مَن یَّضِلَّ لَہُ فَلاَ ھَادِیَ لَہ⟫

With tears in his eyes, Tufail said, "O Muhammad, teach me the Kalimah! I believe in you and in your God.
