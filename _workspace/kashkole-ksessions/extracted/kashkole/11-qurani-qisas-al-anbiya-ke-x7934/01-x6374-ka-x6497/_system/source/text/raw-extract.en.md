### Atonement for intentional sin:

The sins that are committed with pleasure from the heart and praise with the tongue can only be forgiven by repentance and repentance, and not without it! It is obligatory for such a person to turn sincerely to Allah for forgiveness of his sins and to restrain his tongue from praising evil. The heart is the king and ruler of the body; the tongue is its minister and counselor, and the organs are its servants. Therefore, a sin committed with the intention of the heart, to satisfy one's own desire, is indeed a very bad sin. Therefore, whatever is done against the commandments of Allah and for the love of other than Allah, he will have to suffer a painful punishment. In the same way, the act committed by the tongue will be attributed to the heart, and the one who commits a sin with the tongue will receive the same punishment as the former.
