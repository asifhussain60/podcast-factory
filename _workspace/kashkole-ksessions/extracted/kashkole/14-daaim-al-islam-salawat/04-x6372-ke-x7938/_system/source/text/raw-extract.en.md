The interpretation of the early time is the basis of the interpretation of the intermediate time, and the interpretation of the last time is the basis of the qiyamah. Therefore, if a person spends the last hour without praying, his salvation is not possible. That is, a person who does not know the truth even at the appearance of Qa'im, will not receive religion and his Hereafter will be ruined.

The second reason is the interpretation of the early time, which is the initial limit for the believer, the interpretation of the middle time is the proof, and the interpretation of the last time is the Imam.

The Virtue of Prayer in the First Time:

For this reason, prayer has been given the virtue of the first time. Since the narrator is the closest to the believer, it is better to respond to his invitation and to serve him by fulfilling the obligations that have been imposed on him in his invitation. The longer you join the invitation of truth, the less chance you will have to act in that invitation.

Every system of Allah works in circles. Humans also have a circle. The circle of man lies between his two outstretched arms. Whatever a person does, he will do it in this circle. If the circle is opened, it will form a straight line, which is called the straight path, which is the shortest, most correct and straight path from falsehood to truth. All the heavens, moons, suns, stars, years, months, weeks, days, everything in this universe is working in circles.

The number of five is called a circle because it works in a circle. Whichever number is multiplied by five, there will be either a journey, or five will come in the end. For this reason, the circles of all the hierarchies also work in five.

When the time for prayer comes, the spiritual windows of the Kaaba, which is opposite the physical Kaaba, open the openings and the illuminations of the prayer begin, which illuminate the soul of the worshipper. Each mosque has a higher level of status on each musla.

The time of prayer is better than prayer. The time is on the muhtajab and the prayer is on its hijab. When the time for a prayer comes, the muhtajab's strategy begins, which affects his hijab (imams), and then the imam's strategy affects the appearance of the musal. All these ishraqs are in the part of the earth where the time for prayer is held, and when the time for prayer ends, they are released to another region. But there is only one angel present at all times, which is the veil of the eternal (the eighteenth form of the circle of the eternal world, which dominates the seventeen) whose function is to forgive. Wherever a person prays in 24 hours, whether it is missed prayers or naafil prayers, the eighteenth form always affects his appearance.
