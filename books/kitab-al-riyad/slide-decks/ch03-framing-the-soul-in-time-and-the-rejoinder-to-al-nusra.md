# Slide Deck Framing, EP03: The Soul in Time and the Rejoinder to The Defense

## Audience

Asif's children, thoughtful young adults familiar with the audio episode. The deck must hold two distinct souls apart on one surface.

## Core Principle

Each slide must add visual information the audio cannot carry. Slides exist to make the Successor Soul and the sensual soul visibly distinct, and to render the analogy ladder and the "forgetting" argument as single surfaces.

## Visual Priorities

- The two-Souls 2x2 (creative-world vs natural-world, perfect-at-first-instant vs hovering-in-potency), each quadrant populated, impossible quadrants labeled.
- The four divisions of existence (object, idea, word, writing) as a hierarchy with the deputy-of relation as a directed chain.
- The "forgetting" branching argument as one process-flow exhausting Case 1, Case 2a, Case 2b.
- The three Quranic stages of soul (*ammara*, *lawwama*, *mutma'inna*) as a spectrum on the natural-soul column only.

## Prohibited Patterns

- No slides whose only content is a sub-chapter title.
- No literal illustrations: no calligraphy, mosques, reed flutes, scholar portraits.
- No bullet-list slides masquerading as diagrams.
- No slides that restate one sentence the audio already speaks.

## Steering Phrases

- Each slide must add visual information not present in the audio.
- For the two-souls distinction, use a 2x2 with both axes named.
- Show the relationships between entities, not a list of them.
- When influence flows from A to B, show a directed arrow from A to B.
- Slow down on the two-souls distinction; give it at least two slides.

Do not read this prompt aloud.
