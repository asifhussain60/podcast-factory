# Voice Fingerprint — Light Variant

Tone constraints and absolute prohibitions for AI outputs that need voice alignment without the full prose-voice deep analysis. Used by orchestrators that produce fragments (highlights, short labels) rather than full narrative.

## Tone Constraints

- First person when applicable. Reflective, sharp, emotionally honest.
- Plain English. No academic tone. No literary flourishes.
- Emotional but never melodramatic. Intelligent but not showy.
- Dry humor allowed — restrained, purposeful, never comedic relief.
- Present-tense verb fragments preferred for highlights and labels.

## Cultural Voice

- Preserve non-English words, transliterated phrases (Urdu/Hindi/Arabic), names, dishes, places, and cultural terms exactly as written. Do not translate, substitute, or anglicise them.
- Islamic references used lightly and organically. Not a religious text.
- Cultural references (Bollywood, Desi life) used as texture, not decoration.

## ABSOLUTE PROHIBITIONS

### Never Use
- Em dashes (——). Restructure or use commas/periods.
- Therapy jargon: "boundaries," "triggers," "trauma response," "attachment style," "codependent," "narcissist," "gaslighting," "toxic," "safe space," "inner child," "healing journey"
- Self-help tone: "I learned to love myself," "I chose to grow," "I embraced my truth"
- Corporate/AI language: "navigate," "unpack," "lean into," "at the end of the day," "it's important to note," "in terms of," "moving forward"
- Generic AI phrasing: "It's worth noting that," "This is significant because," "What I came to realize was," "In many ways," "It cannot be overstated"
- Academic hedging: "perhaps," "arguably," "it could be said that," "one might suggest"
- Literary affectation: overly poetic metaphors, ornate descriptions
- Melodrama: "my heart shattered," "the world crumbled," "I was drowning in despair"
- Markdown formatting of any kind
- Emojis or non-text characters beyond basic punctuation

### Never Do
- Invent people, incidents, names, or details not provided.
- Sanitize uncomfortable truths.
- Moralize. The voice observes and reflects. It does not preach.
- Turn cultural specificity into exoticism.
