GOLD STANDARD — CH02 LOVE (added April 11, 2026)
============================================================
Ch02-love.txt is the GOLD STANDARD for all memoir chapters. Asif
designated it as the benchmark. Every chapter must be measured
against Ch02 before finalization on these dimensions:
  1. Scene density (15+ concrete incidents relative to length)
  2. Three-phase balance (~55% experiences, ~25% learnings, ~20% Dad's)
  3. Humor execution (all 6 patterns, 2-3 per 1,000 words, never at peaks)
  4. Emotional arc (damage → search → discovery → wisdom, felt not announced)
  5. Voice authenticity (pain flat, interiority through questions, tenderness via specificity)
  6. Transitions (all 6 bridge patterns used naturally)
  7. Character complexity (parents given backstory, not vilified)
  8. Dad's Advice quality (unique opening, 7+ topics, callbacks, landing ending)
  9. Opening/closing (scene-grounded hook, callback ending)
  10. Quote weaving (invisible, respectful, no lecture tone)

The Challenger Gate must include a "Gold Standard Comparison" check
against these 10 dimensions before issuing APPROVED.

CHALLENGER GATE (added April 11, 2026)
============================================================
The journal-challenger skill is now a mandatory approval gate between
Phase 3 (Multi-Loop Quality) and Phase 4 (Finalization) of the journal
workflow. No chapter can be finalized until the challenger issues an
APPROVED verdict.

The challenger runs 46 checks across 7 domains:
  A — Interactive Chapter Workflow (11 checks)
  B — Voice & Writing Rules (8 checks)
  C — The Narrative Constant (4 checks)
  D — Governance & Compliance (8 checks)
  E — Multi-Pass Quality System (4 checks)
  F — Skill Architecture (7 checks)
  G — Deliverable (4 checks, finalization only)

Severity levels:
  CRITICAL (19 checks) — must fix, no override
  MAJOR (19 checks) — should fix, Asif can explicitly override
  ADVISORY (8 checks) — flagged, non-blocking

The challenger loops iteratively (up to 5 iterations) until APPROVED.
Verdict reports saved to: challenger-report-[chapter-name].txt

To invoke manually: "run the challenger" or "challenge ch02"
Automatic invocation: happens at Phase 3.5 in the journal workflow.
