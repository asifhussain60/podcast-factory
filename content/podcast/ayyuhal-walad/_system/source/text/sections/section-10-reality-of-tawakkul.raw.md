# Reality of Reliance on God (Tawakkul)

REALITY OF RELIANCE ON GOD ( TAWAKKUL )
 20

You have also asked, what is reliance on God ( Tawakkul )? You should know that Tawakkul is
that, one should have firm belief and certitude in wh at Allah, the Exalted, has promised. In other
words, one should believe that you will surely get whatever Allah, the Exalted, has destined for
you. Then if the entire world tries to stop it, it (Allah’s Decree for you) cannot be stopped. But
for that which is not written in your destiny [by Allah], if you and the entire world make efforts
for it, you will never get it.
