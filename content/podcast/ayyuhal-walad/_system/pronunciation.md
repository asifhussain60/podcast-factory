## Book-level anti-mangling overrides (history of failure)

Three terms have a documented history of being mangled by NotebookLM. Each is locked in every episode's framing with an explicit "Do not say X" anti-mangling guard.

- **Tasawwuf** — `ta-SAW-wuf`. NEVER "tassel wolf", "tasso wolf", "tasa wolf".
- **Dhu'l-Nun al-Misri** — `thool-noon al-mis-ree`. NEVER "shake stone noon mystery" or letter-spelled.
- **Ihya Ulum al-Din** — full canonical form every time. NEVER "the Ihya", "EI", "IUD".
