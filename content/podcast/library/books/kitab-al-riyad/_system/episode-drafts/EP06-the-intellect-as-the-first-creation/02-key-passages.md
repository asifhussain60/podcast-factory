### Passage 1

> Al-Kirmani's proof that the Intellect is not a body: *If the Intellect were a body, it would be preceded in existence by others, and it would not be the first creation. Nothing precedes the first intellect in its existence, since it is itself the act of creation, behind which is only God the Creator Almighty.*

### Passage 2

> Al-Kirmani's settled formula on the *hudud*: *The blessings overflow from the side of all the hudud. The reason being that there is no shortage in those blessings by which perfection is attained in all beings, nor any withholding on the part of the hudud. The limitation lies rather in the incapacity of the receivers to accept them — each according to the capacity of the one able to receive.*
