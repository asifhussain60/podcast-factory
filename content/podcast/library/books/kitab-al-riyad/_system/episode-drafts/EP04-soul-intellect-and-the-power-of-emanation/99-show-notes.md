# Show notes — EP04

**Title:** Soul, Intellect, and the Power of Emanation

**Blurb:** The middle stretch of Chapter One of *Kitab al-Riyad* — sub-chapters eleven through twenty-three — where al-Sijistani exhausts his attacks on the perfection of the Soul through a parade of analogies, and al-Kirmani holds the formula "perfect in itself, deficient only in relation" at every turn. The Two Weighty Things hadith arrives at the structural center; the dolphins of the Sea of Oman arrive near the end. The episode closes at the threshold of the sperm-and-man analogy that opens episode five.
