### Passage 1

> Al-Kirmani's settled formula on the First and the Second: *The First and the Second are two counterparts — the mineral of honor, perfection, and elevation. There is no honor above them, and Allah has made them the mineral of perfect honor and perfect light, complete elevation, complete knowledge — with no ignorance there, no cycle, no darkness whatsoever. They founded the higher world, which is the mineral of purity, al-Quds (sanctity), and life.*

### Passage 3

> Al-Kirmani's correction on parts and sides: *The one possessing parts is the one possessing diameters estimated by area. The Second, being not an embodiment, has no parts in the sense al-Nusra requires; the Second is one self that acquires two proportions in its additions — the cause-proportion in the First, and the effect-proportion (ma'lul) in the First. To call those proportions limbs is to confuse the language of relation with the language of geometry.*

### Passage 4

> Al-Kirmani on diminution: *There is no deficiency in the Second; nor is the Second far from the First. Rather, the Second is like the First; and the diminution is necessary, not from its very nature, but it became incomplete — not from the side of its own selves, which is its perfection, but from a side whose second perfection it acquires by way of benefit.*
