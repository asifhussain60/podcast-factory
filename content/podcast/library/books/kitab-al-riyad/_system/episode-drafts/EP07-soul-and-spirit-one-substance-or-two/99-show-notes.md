# Show notes — EP07

**Title:** Soul and Spirit — One Substance or Two?

**Blurb:** Six sub-chapters of *Chapter Three* of *Kitab al-Riyad* — al-Kirmani's judgment among the *hudud* on whether the Soul (the Second) and the lower soul (*al-Hayuli*, prime matter) are similar to the First or not. The chapter answers: the First and the Second are like-counterparts, the mineral of honor, perfection, and elevation; *al-Hayuli* is the trace of the Second, not its child; the higher and lower worlds do not need to resemble each other to be in contact, because the contact is the work of the power learned from the First, running through the Second, impressing the substrate. Al-Nusra's body-categories — sides, distance, diminution — are refused one by one. The closing sub-chapter accepts al-Nusra's final reconstruction of the chain (Intellect → Soul → nature) once he has dropped the body-talk. The doctrinal payload sets the architecture for the rest of *al-Riyad*: the *hudud* are linked to their principles by office, not by likeness.
