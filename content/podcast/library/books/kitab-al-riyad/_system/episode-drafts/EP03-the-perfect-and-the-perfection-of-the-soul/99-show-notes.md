# Show notes — EP03

**Title:** The Perfect and the Perfection of the Soul

**Blurb:** The opening of *Kitab al-Riyad* in full: the damaged invocation that survives, al-Kirmani's preface and prayer for the ruling Imam, his statement of method, the table of ten chapters, and the first ten sub-chapters of Chapter One — where the foundational doctrine of the book (perfect in itself, deficient only in relation) is established against al-Sijistani's persistent attempt to fix the Soul on a single rank-scale that would make it deficient in itself.
