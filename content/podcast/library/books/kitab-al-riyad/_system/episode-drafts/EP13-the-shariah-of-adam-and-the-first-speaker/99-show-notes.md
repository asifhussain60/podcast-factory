# Show notes — EP13

**Title:** The Shariʿah of Adam and the First Speaker

**Blurb:** The first nineteen sub-chapters of *Chapter Nine* of *Kitab al-Riyad* — al-Kirmani's adjudication of whether Adam (peace be upon him) had a *Shariʿah*. The author of *al-Nusra* presses that he did not — the *qurban* (offering) verse is a parable, *one of Adam's sons did not know how to bury his brother until Allah sent a raven*, the verse of *Shariʿah* (Quran 42:13) names Noah, Abraham, Moses, Jesus, and Muhammad ﷺ but not Adam, the angels are not assigned and are not obligated, and *if Adam had been the owner of a Shariʿah, Allah would have commanded the angels to prostrate themselves through him*. The author of *al-Islah* had affirmed the opposite — the *qurban* settles it on its apparent reading, the universally affirmative verses settle it independently, and the *speaker* cannot be a speaker without a *Shariʿah* drawn among his nation. Al-Kirmani works the dispute point by point: the *qurban* on apparent + the deep *ta'wil* of *the two sons* as *wali* and *opposite* and the *raven* as *Muhammad ibn Abi Bakr*; the *narrative tradition* naming Adam as *prescriber of the rites of the dead*; the *prostration of the angels* establishing Adam's *Mustafa*-rank; the *parallel verse to Israel* showing that *silent intermediaries* are not *no intermediaries*; the *natural-world angels* distinguished from the *abode-of-eternity high limits*; the *roster argument* refuted by the parallel roster of Quran 4:163; the *three souls* and the *six gates of necessity* constitutive of *human-being-as-such*; the *Noah-parallel* showing *violation* presupposes *prior limits*; the *tent at the qibla* and Seth as *first asas*; the refusal of the *bare-world-sufficient-for- tawhid* doctrine. *The Shariʿah of Adam (peace be upon him) is fixed.* The chapter bridges to its own second half — the *prophets-as-teachers* doctrine, the *monotheism-and-the-ranks* architecture, and the *wilayah of Noah*.
