# Obedience to a Spiritual Guide

OBEDIENCE TO A SPIRITUAL GUIDE

Such spiritual guides are very difficult to find. But if someone has obtained this wealth and this
blessing that he/she found such a spiritual guide and this spiritual guide includes him/her among
his disciples ( Mureed s) then it is necessary for this disciple to have outer and inner respect (honor
and etiquettes) for his/her spiritual guide.
