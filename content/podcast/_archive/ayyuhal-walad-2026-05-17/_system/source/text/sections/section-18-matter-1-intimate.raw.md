# Matter 1 — Developing an Intimate Relationship with Allah

1. THE METHOD OF DEVELOPING AN INTIMATE RELATIONSHIP WITH ALLAH
The first thing is that, every matter that is betw een you and Allah should be conducted [by you] in
such a way that if your servant does the same for you then you, instead of being sad, will like it,
commend it, and will not be in any way angry about it. Likewise, if there is anything you do not
 26

like about your servant doing it for you, you should also realize that if you fell short in your
servitude to your Lord then your Creator will also not like it. The reality that needs to be mentioned here [and the fundamental point that needs to be driven home here] is that your servant
is not created by you but only hired by you. But you been created, raised (cherished), and owned by your Real Creator and Owner [whom you are a slave of, therefore, your obligation to not in an way displease your Lord either in thought, word, or deed, is tremendous].
