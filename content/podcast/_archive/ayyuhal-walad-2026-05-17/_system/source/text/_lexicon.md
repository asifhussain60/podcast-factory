# Lexicon — Ayyuhal Walad

Running list of proper nouns, transliterated Arabic terms, and technical terminology used across the series. Spellings and phonetic guides confirmed here are the canonical form for every episode bundle.

## Names

- **Imam Abu Hamid Muhammad al-Ghazali** (im-AAM a-BOO HAA-mid moo-HAM-mad al-ghaz-ZAA-lee) — author of the epistle, 1058–1111 CE.
- **Irfan Hasan** — English translator (working from the Urdu translation).
- **Junaid Baghdadi** (joo-NAYD bagh-DAA-dee) — early Sufi master.
- **Hatim bin Ism** (HAA-tim ibn ISM) — Sufi master, narrator of the eight benefits in section 3.
- **Shaqiq al-Balkhi** (sha-QEEQ al-BAL-khee) — Hatim's teacher.
- **Hasan al-Basri** (HA-san al-BAS-ree) — early Muslim ascetic frequently quoted.
- **Sufyan al-Thawri** (suf-YAAN ath-THAW-ree) — early jurist and ascetic.
- **Shibli** (SHIB-lee) — Sufi master quoted at the close of the response chapter.
- **Luqman** (LUQ-man) — Quranic sage whose advice to his son is cited repeatedly.

## Works

- **Ayyuhal Walad** (eye-YOO-hal WAA-lad) — *My Dear Beloved Son or Daughter*; the epistle itself.
- **Majmu'a Rasail Imam Ghazali** (maj-MOO-a ra-SAA-il im-AAM ghaz-ZAA-lee) — the three-volume short-works collection Ayyuhal Walad belongs to.
- **Ihya Ulum al-Din** (ih-YAA oo-LOOM ad-DEEN) — *Revival of the Religious Sciences*.
- **Kimiya as-Sa'ada** (kee-MEE-yaa as-sa-AA-dah) — *The Alchemy of Happiness*.
- **Jawahir al-Quran** (ja-WAA-hir al-qur-AAN) — *Priceless Gems of the Quran*.
- **Arba'in** (ar-ba-EEN) — *Forty Steps*.
- **Minhaj al-Abidin** (min-HAAJ al-aa-bi-DEEN) — *Best Way for the Worshippers of God*.

## Terms

- **Ahadith** (a-HAA-deeth) — Prophetic traditions; plural of hadith.
- **Sunnah** (SOON-nah) — the outward and inward way of life of the Prophet.
- **Shari'ah** (sha-REE-ah) — the revealed law.
- **Tahajjud** (ta-HAJ-jud) — the optional prayer in the last third of the night.
- **Mujahadah** (moo-JAA-ha-dah) — intense inner spiritual struggle.
- **Ma'arifah** (maa-ri-FAH) — deep intimate knowledge of Allah.
- **Tasawwuf** (ta-SAW-wuf) — Sufism; the inner discipline of the soul.
- **Tawakkul** (ta-WAK-kul) — reliance on God.
- **Ikhlas** (ikh-LAAS) — sincerity, single-hearted devotion.
- **Taubatun Nasuh** (taw-ba-tun na-SOOH) — true repentance that does not return to its sin.
- **Niyya** (NEE-yah) — intention.
- **Bid'a** (BID-ah) — innovation outside the established principles of the Quran and Sunnah.
- **Fard Kifayah** (fard ki-FAA-yah) — communal obligation (vs. individual).
- **Du'a** (du-AAH) — supplication.
- **Zakat** (za-KAAT) — obligatory alms.
