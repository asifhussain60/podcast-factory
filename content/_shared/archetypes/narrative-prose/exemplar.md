# Exemplar Episode — Narrative Prose (Literary Immersion)

This document shows a well-formed episode structure for the narrative-prose archetype.
It is illustrative, not prescriptive — adapt framing language to the specific text.

---

## Opening

Host A places the listener inside the narrative moment:
> "The scene opens in [place], and [protagonist] is facing a choice that the narrative
> has been building toward since the opening pages. [Author] doesn't announce it — there's
> no authorial intrusion, no commentary. The prose simply slows down. The detail
> multiplies. The reader is being made to feel the weight before the character does."

Host B names the significance:
> "And what makes this moment remarkable is that [Author] has structured the whole
> arc so that we arrive here already knowing what [protagonist] cannot yet know. We're
> reading the dramatic irony in real time — and [Author] is absolutely deliberate about
> that."

---

## Discussion

The hosts move through the narrative in scene-arc order:

**Scene framing:** Establish where we are in the story. Both hosts speak from inside the
narrative — not as summarisers looking back, but as readers in the present tense of the
story.

**Analytical reflection:** What is [Author] doing structurally or thematically in this
passage? Host A names the technique; Host B traces its doctrinal or historical resonance.

**Forward hook:** How does the scene's resolution change what follows? End on a question
the next episode will answer.

---

## Closing

Host A gives the scene its due:
> "This is [Author] at their most controlled — the narrative surface is plain, almost
> sparse, but the architecture underneath is dense. Every detail is load-bearing."

Host B opens the next question:
> "The question we carry forward is whether [protagonist]'s choice here is consistent
> with what [Author] argues in the next movement — or whether it's the crack in the
> structure the rest of the narrative is built around."
