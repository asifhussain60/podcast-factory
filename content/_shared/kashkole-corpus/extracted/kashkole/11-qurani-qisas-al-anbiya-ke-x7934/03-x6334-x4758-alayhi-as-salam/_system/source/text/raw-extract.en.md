When Allah intended to create Adam, it was revealed to the earth: "O earth! I am going to create a creature out of you. Some of them will be those who will obey me, and some will disobey me. I will admit the obedient to Paradise and the disobedient to Hell . After this revelation, the Almighty sent Gabriel (peace be upon him) to the earth, saying, "Go and bring dust from the earth." Gabriel (peace be upon him) turned back without taking anything and said to Allah, "Since the earth stopped me by swearing by your honor, I did not dare to go forward."
