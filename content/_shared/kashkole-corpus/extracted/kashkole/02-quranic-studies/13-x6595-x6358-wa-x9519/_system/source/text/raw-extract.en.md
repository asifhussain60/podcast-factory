Story of Adam - importance of knowledge

As I was explaining the last time. The first 4 ayats of the Quran provide us with information about Allah. The Quran speaks to the ⟪ar:اولو الباب⟫. who are people of sound essence. And sound essence is a combination of two things. A healthy heart. and a healthy intellect. Because the word ⟪ar:عقل⟫. linguistically can mean both things. It can mean the heart as well as the intellect. And this ⟪ar:عقل⟫is a gift that was given to human beings. so that they could recognize their creator.

When Allah decided to create Adam AS. the angles objected and said.
⟪ar-quote:وَإِذْ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلْمَلَٓئِكَةِ إِنِّى جَاعِلٌۭ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ خَلِيفَةًۭ.  قَالُوٓا۟ أَتَجْعَلُ فِيهَا مَن يُفْسِدُ فِيهَا وَيَسْفِكُ ٱلدِّمَآءَ وَنَحْنُ نُسَبِّحُ بِحَمْدِكَ وَنُقَدِّسُ لَكَ⟫

They said you want to create someone who will sow corruption and spill blood?. Cause if you're creating man to glorify you. and do HAMD for you. we're already doing that. You don't need man for that. Because we're right here. and we can see you. and we can hear you. and that's why we are capable of your HAMD. 

You're creating Adam and the children of Adam for the earth. They're not going to be able to see you or you. Which means they will not be able to recognize you. And by not recognizing you. they will sow corruption in that earth. And Allah responed.

He said. Oh Adam. inform them about all the names.
⟪ar-quote:فَلَمَّآ أَنۢبَأَهُم بِأَسْمَآئِهِمْ قَالَ أَلَمْ أَقُل لَّكُمْ إِنِّىٓ أَعْلَمُ غَيْبَ ٱلسَّمَوَتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَأَعْلَمُ مَا تُبْدُونَ وَمَا كُنتُمْ تَكْتُمُونَ⟫

When Adam informed them about the names. Allah said. Did I not say to you. that I posess the KNOWLEDGE of everything. of what you reveal and what you hide.

So we see that the word KNOWLEDGE. ⟪ar:علم⟫. is constantly repeated in these conversations. And the message to the angels was. YES. I AM creating Adam as my representative on the earth. and he will NOT be able to see me physically. but as Adam just demonstrated. I have given him the ability to learn. to know. to acquire knowledge. AND. I have already taught him my names. my divine attributes. BEFORE sending him down on earth. So even though he will not see me. he will see my attributes being reflected in the universe that I've created for him. He will see my signs. inside him and outside of him. And they will REMIND him of me. And THROUGH those attributes. his HEART. will recognize me.

And so these 4 ayats. they provide us with the knowledge of Allah. ⟪ar:بسم اللہ⟫ . With the name of Allah. with the name of the essence that is sum of all of its attributes. And then he took on its ownership. its rububiyyah. So this is what HE did for the creation. And in return for his rububiyyah. the creation now owes him a debt. A debt of submission. a debt of HAMD. of praise and gratitude.  This is the message of  ⟪ar:الحمد للہ رب العالمین⟫.
⟪ar:بسم اللہ الرحمن الرحیم.⟫was about WHO he is. ⟪ar:الحمد للہ رب العالمین.⟫is about WHAT he did. Then   ⟪ar:الرحمن الرحیم⟫. gives us the knowledge of HOW he executes his RUBUBIYYHA. The emotion behind his interaction with his creation. which is of intense love, care, concern and mercy. and with CONSTANT love, care concern and mercy.

And then finally ⟪ar:مالک یوم الدین⟫. The first 3 ayats apply to ALL creation. including us. Because he is the Rabb of everything that is known. He is Rehman and Raheem in his interactions with EVERYTHING in existence. But ⟪ar:مالک یوم الدین⟫. is ONLY about human beings. Why?. Because everything else is ALREADY paying its debt to its creator. It is ONLY human beings who have yet to pay their debt. This is why THIS is the central ayah. It's right in the center. The first 3 ayats are building up to this on. And the ayats after this. are in recognition of the fact. that he is Malik Yaum AdDeen. which I'll show you in a bit InshaAllah. But this is the TRUE essence of the Fateha. That HE is our OWNER. He is the king. and we OWE him a debt of submission. We owe him ⟪ar:دین الاسلام⟫.
⟪ar-quote:ان الدین عند اللہ الاسلام⟫

No doubt. the true debt that creation owes Allah. is their submission. And that day is coming. when we WILL be taken to account for what we owe.

So the first 4 ayats are essentially explaining Allah's relationship with his creation. They're constructing a mindset. that everything that is CREATED. is in a state of UBOODIYAH. and they have absolutely NO choice in the matter.
⟪ar-quote:ثُمَّ ٱسْتَوَىٰٓ إِلَى ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَهِىَ دُخَانٌۭ . فَقَالَ لَهَا وَلِلْأَرْضِ ٱئْتِيَا طَوْعًا أَوْ كَرْهًۭا قَالَتَآ أَتَيْنَا طَآئِعِينَ⟫

He said to the heavens and the earth. come to me. willingly or unwillingly. Allah did not give them free will. But when it came to us.

Say to them. The truth of their Rabb has been revealed. Whoever wants can believe. and whoever wants can reject. So the entire ALAMEEN is demonstarting that Allah created them for submission. and they have no choice in the matter. This means YOU and I are created for submission. for UBOODIYAH. for slavery as well. The only difference is. they don't have a choice. but we do.

Why choose Allah as master?

So reflecting on these 4 ayats. we reach the concolusion. that UBOODIYAH. servitude. is what is expected of us. The question then becomes. WHY should we choose to become Allah's slaves?. Because slavery by definition means. giving up our free will. It means to submit our will to the will of Allah. Not to DO what WE want. but to DO what Allah wants us to do. That's the concept of slavery. The only question is. WHY?. And so we started looking at the slavery of Allah. and we began comparing it to the slavery of the world.

The first thing we saw was that slavery by definition is a humiliation. whereas freedom is honor. And so right off the bat Allah honored us by giving us free will. Free will can ONLY be an act of the REHMAN and the RAHEEM. 

Then. we see that Allah is ⟪ar:رب العالمین⟫. He is  ⟪ar:رب السموات و الارض⟫. He is not just my master. he is the master of the ALAMEEN. He is the master of the heavens and the earth. And they are in CONSTANT UBOODIYAH. CONSTANT slavery to him. they are in a state of constant HAMD. right?. The sun the moon. the stars. they know their job. and they're doing it. CONSTANTLY. They NEVER take a break. They don't say I'm tired today I can't do my job. The sun doesn't say I've been rising and setting for millions of years. I'm burnt out. I need a vacation. Pun intended.

Then. in comparison to EVERY other creation. we see that Allah gave us the MOST. And when you give someone a LOT. you expect a LOT in return. Right?. That's only fair. So if Allah gave us the MOST. he would be ABSOLUTELY just. in expecting and demanding the MOST from us in return. And YET. he expects the BARE MINIMUM. the VERY least. SubhanAllah! .Of ALL the creations. this master gave us the MOST. The rest of creation is CONSTANTLY in UBUDIYYAH to him. They're ALL working round the clock. in CONSTANT obedience to Allah(Azza Wa Jal). to SERVE us. But then despite giving US the MOST. ALKAUSAR. he expects the LEAST from us in return. 1 hours of salaat in a day. 1 out of 12 months of fast. only from sunrise to sunset. 2.5% of our wealth in zakaat. the wealth he gave us in the first place. It's incredible. What kind of a master is this?. What master in the world. gives SO much. and expects SO little in return?. We've never heard of such a master.

On top of that. if that wasn't easy enough. he ALSO sent us revelation. to explain his reasons to us. Does a master EVER explain his reasons to his slaves?.  Can ANY slave tell his master. Explain the purpose of this command to me?. Can a slave negotiate with its master?. Can a slave say. I'll obey your command ONLY WHEN you explain it to me. when you tell me WHY I need to do this. Can a slave do that?. NO!. But did THIS master explain things to us in the Quran?. He did. Why?. So that we could find ease in our submission to him. To make UBOODIYAH easy for us. Because when we understand the benefits of something it becomes easier to take advantage of it.
⟪ar-quote:يُرِيدُ ٱللَّهُ بِكُمُ ٱلْيُسْرَ وَلَا يُرِيدُ بِكُمُ ٱلْعُسْرَ⟫

Allah desires EASE for you. He does not desire hardship for you. This is a VERY unique master.No master in the world does this.

Now here's another question. Are these acts of worship meant to benefit HIM?. or are they meant to benefit US?. Who is slavery ALWAYS designed to benefit?. The master? or the slave?. ALWAYS the master. But this master. he designed the ALAMEEN for us. gave US the MOST. asked the least in return from us. explained the reasons of his commands to us. But whatever he asked us to do. benefits who?. It benefits US and ONLY us. SubhanAllah!. Every slave in the world is bought to benefit the master. Every command issued to a slave is for the master's benefits. What kind of a master is this?. whose EVERY SINGLE COMMAND is meant for the slaves benefit. and not ONE SINGLE COMMAND is for his own benefit. If that is not love. If that is not care. If that is not mercy. If that is not ease. If that is not Rehma. then YOU tell me what is. AllahuAkbar!

So the more we reflect on the rububiyyah of this master. the more we realize that he is ABSOLUTELY INCREDIBLE. He is EXTREMELY loving. he is EXTREMELY merciful. He is not like ANY other master we will EVER find in this world. No master does for his slave. what Allah does for us. No master LOVES his slaves. He may take care of them. be kind to them. but no master ever LOVES his slaves. No master grants his slaves ALKAUSAR. No master showers his slaves with uncountable and invaluable gifts. No master grants his slaves. FREE WILL. They enforce their OWN will upon the slaves. They make the slaves do what THEY want to do. **NO master JUDGES the work of a slave according to the slaves capabilities**. They buy slaves for their OWN benefit. No master RUSHES to fulfill the needs of his slaves. SLAVES are always rushed to fulfill their masters needs and desires. But this master rushes to fulfill OUR needs.

This master is UNLIKE. ANY OTHER master we have ever seen in the world. There is NO master. that is SO LOVING. SO caring. SO merciful. SO lenient. that we have forget our place before him. We forget we are his property. Because he is MALIK. He is owner. And if he is OWNER. then by definition. that makes me property. Which means he can do whatever he wants with me. He can break me. He can rip me apart. He can burn me. drown me. He can do WHATEVER he wants with me. And if he chooses to do that. there is ABSOLUTELY NOTHING. that I can do. or you can can do. or ALL of us together can do. OR EVERYTHING in this UNIVERSE combined can do. to protect me against his wrath.
