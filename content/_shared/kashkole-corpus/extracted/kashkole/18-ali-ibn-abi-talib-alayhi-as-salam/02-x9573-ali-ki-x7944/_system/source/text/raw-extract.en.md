### Ali's face:

⟪ar:اللہ عزوجل⟫Blessed is the best and most noble servant of God. ⟪ar:محمد مصطفی صلی اللہ علیہ وسلم⟫ ⟪ar:رب العٰلمین⟫⟪ar:رحمت للعالمین و سید المرسلین⟫He was honored with the title of K. The Messenger of Allah (s.a.w.a.) said, "Ali's status with me is like my head with my body." If the head is separated from the body, then the human being is destroyed. It is the head that identifies a person, as if the head is the door to recognize a person. Referring to this, the Prophet (s) said: ⟪ar-quote:اَنَا مَدِینَۃُ اَلعِلمِ وَعَلِیٌّ بَابُھاَ⟫ "I am the city of knowledge and Ali is its gate." Whoever wants to seek knowledge, he should ask Ali.

The face of a human being is the most beautiful and noble organ in his whole body. If there is a face of a higher being, then perfections will be equally high in him. The highest of the Prophets and Messengers is the Holy Prophet (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him). And if you yourself call someone your face, how lofty and virtuous will be the person to whom you have alluded!! The Messenger of Allah (s.a.w.a.) said:

⟪ar-quote:مَنْ اَرَادَ اَن یَّنظُرُ اِلَی آدَمَ فِی عِلْمِہِ وَ اِلَی نُوْحِ فِی التَّقوٰی وَ اِلَی اِبْرَاھِیْمَ فِی خُلَّتِۃِ وَاِلَی مُوْسٰی فِی ھَیْبَۃِ وَ اِلَی عِیسٰی فِی زُھدِہِ

True Testimony:

True testimony is in the form of one of the following statements of the witness: (1) To confirm the witness's statement that it is actually his.
