# Nahj al-Balagha fi al-Arabiya

## Praise is due to Allah whose worth cannot be described

Praise is due to Allah, whose worth cannot be encompassed by speakers, whose bounties cannot be counted by those who enumerate them, and whose claim to obedience cannot be satisfied by those who strive to meet it. He lies beyond the reach of intellectual aspiration and the diving of understanding. For His description no bound has been set, no eulogy exists, no time is appointed, and no duration is fixed. He brought forth creation through His Omnipotence, dispersed the winds through His Compassion, and made firm the trembling earth with mountains.

⟪ar-quote:الحَمْدُ للهِ الَّذِي لاَ يَبْلُغُ مِدْحَتَهُ القَائِلُونَ، وَلاِ يُحْصِي نَعْمَاءَهُ العَادُّونَ، ولاَ يُؤَدِّي حَقَّهُ الُمجْتَهِدُونَ، الَّذِي لاَ يُدْركُهُ بُعْدُ الهِمَمِ، وَلاَ يَنَالُهُ غَوْصُ الفِطَنِ، الَّذِي لَيْسَ لِصِفَتِهِ حَدٌّ مَحْدُودٌ، وَلاَ نَعْتٌ مَوْجُودٌ، وَلا وَقْتٌ مَعْدُودٌ، وَلا أَجَلٌ مَمْدُودٌ۔ فَطَرَ الخَلائِقَ بِقُدْرَتِهَ، وَنَشَرَ الرِّيَاحَ بِرَحْمَتِهِ، وَوَتَّدَ بِالصُّخُورِ مَيَدَانَ أَرْضِهِ⟫

The foremost in faith is the acknowledgement of Him. The perfection of such acknowledgement lies in testifying to His Unity. The perfection of witnessing to His Oneness is to declare His purity (tawḥīd, the doctrine of absolute monotheism), and the perfection of His purity is the denial of attributes—for every attribute stands as evidence that it differs from that which it describes, and everything described differs from the description itself.[^cite-1]

Thus, whoever attaches attributes to Allah recognizes His like. Whoever recognizes His like regards Him as paired. Whoever regards Him as paired divides Him into parts. Whoever divides Him into parts fails to understand Him truly. Whoever fails to understand Him truly points at Him with similitude. Whoever points at Him thereby circumscribes Him. Whoever circumscribes Him has enumerated Him. Those who ask "In what is He?" treat Him as contained within something. Those who ask "Upon what does He rest?" deny that He rests upon anything else.[^cite-2]

⟪ar-quote:أَوَّلُ الدِّينِ مَعْرِفَتُهُ، وَكَمَالُ مَعْرِفَتِهِ التَّصْديقُ بِهِ، وَكَمَالُ التَّصْدِيقِ بِهِ تَوْحِيدُهُ، وَكَمَالُ تَوْحِيدِهِ الاخْلاصُ لَهُ، وَكَمَالُ الاخْلاصِ لَهُ نَفْيُ الصِّفَاتِ عَنْهُ، (لِشَهَادَةِ كُلِّ صِفَة أَنَّها غَيْرُ المَوْصُوفِ، وَشَهَادَةِ كُلِّ مَوْصُوف أَنَّهُ غَيْرُ الصِّفَةِ)، فَمَنْ وَصَفَ اللهَ سُبْحَانَهُ فَقَدْ قَرَنَهُ، وَمَنْ قَرَنَهُ فَقَدْ ثَنَّاهُ،وَمَنْ ثَنَّاهُ فَقَد جَزَّأَهُ، وَمَنْ جَزَّأَهُ فَقَدْ جَهِلَهُ، وَمَنْ جَهِلَهُ فَقَدْ أشَارَ إِلَيْهِ، وَمَنْ أشَارَ إِلَيْهِ فَقَدْ حَدَّهُ، وَمَنْ حَدَّهُ فَقَدْ عَدَّهُ، وَمَنْ قَالَ- "فِيمَ" فَقَدْ ضَمَّنَهُ، وَمَنْ قَالَ- "عَلاَمَ؟" فَقَدْ أَخْلَى مِنُهُ⟫.

He is Being itself, but not through process of becoming. He exists, but not from non-existence. He is present with all things, yet not through physical proximity. He is distinct from all things, yet not through separation. He acts without movement or instruments. He perceives all things even when nothing remains among His creation to be perceived. He is utterly One—such that none keeps Him company, none suffers His absence.[^cite-3]

⟪ar-quote:كائِنٌ لاَ عَنْ حَدَث، مَوْجُودٌ لاَ عَنْ عَدَم، مَعَ كُلِّ شَيْء لاَ بِمُقَارَنَة، وَغَيْرُ كُلِّ شيء لا بِمُزَايَلَة، فَاعِلٌ لا بِمَعْنَى الْحَرَكَاتِ وَالاْلةِ، بَصِيرٌ إذْ لاَ مَنْظُورَ إلَيْهِ مِنْ خَلْقِهِ، مُتَوَحِّدٌ إذْ لاَ سَكَنَ يَسْتَأْنِسُ بهِ وَلاَ يَسْتوْحِشُ لِفَقْدِهِ⟫

## Creation of the Universe

In the beginning, the Most High brought forth creation—originating it without preliminary reflection, without prior experiment, without initiating movement, without struggle of thought. He assigned to all things their times, brought together their variations, established their properties, and determined their forms, knowing them before they were created, grasping the full extent of their bounds and comprehending their innermost natures and intricacies.[^cite-4]

⟪ar-quote:أَنْشَأَ الخَلْقَ إنْشَاءً، وَابْتَدَأَهُ ابْتِدَاءً بِلاَ رَوِيَّة أَجَالَهَا، وَلاَ تَجْرِبَة اسْتَفَادَهَا، وَلاَ حَرَكَة أَحْدَثَهَا، وَلاَ هَمَامَةِ نَفْس اضطَرَبَ فِيهَا. أَحَالَ الاْشياءَ لاِوْقَاتِهَا، وَلاَمَ بَيْنَ مُخْتَلِفَاتِهَا، وَغَرَّزَ غَرائِزَهَا، وَأَلزَمَهَا أشْبَاحَهَا، عَالِماً بِهَا قَبْلَ ابْتِدَائِهَا، مُحِيطاً بِحُدُودِها وَانْتِهَائِهَا، عَارفاً بِقَرَائِنِها وَأَحْنَائِهَا⟫

Then the Almighty opened the vault between the high heavens, split the expanse of the firmament, and carved passages through the air. Through these He sent water—its currents turbulent, its surges heaped upon one another. He bore it upon the back of tempestuous winds and shattering whirlwinds, commanding them to shed it forth again, imposing upon the wind dominion over the rain's force, and establishing measure for it. The wind blew beneath the waters while the waters raged above.

⟪ar-quote:ثُمَّ أَنْشَأَ سُبْحَانَهُ فَتْقَ الاْجْوَاءِ، وَشَقَّ الاْرْجَاءِ، وَسَكَائِكَ الَهوَاءِ، فأَجازَ فِيهَا مَاءً مُتَلاطِماً تَيَّارُهُ، مُتَراكِماً زَخَّارُهُ، حَمَلَهُ عَلَى مَتْنِ الرِّيحِ الْعَاصِفَةِ، وَالزَّعْزَعِ الْقَاصِفَةِ، فَأَمَرَها بِرَدِّهِ، وَسَلَّطَهَا عَلَى شَدِّهِ، وَقَرنَهَا إِلَى حَدِّهِ، الهَوَاءُ مِنْ تَحْتِها فَتِيقٌ، وَالمَاءُ مِنْ فَوْقِهَا دَفِيقٌ⟫

Thereafter, the Almighty brought forth a wind and made its breath constant, sustained its course, intensified its motion, and spread it far and wide. He commanded it to churn the deep waters and raise the waves of the oceans. The wind churned it as butter is churned, casting its violence upon the broad expanse and the open sky, driving its forward surge upon its rear and its stillness upon its flood, until the waters rose up in great billows and the foam gathered into mountains. Then the Almighty lifted the foam upon the open wind and vast atmosphere and fashioned from it seven heavens. He made the lowest as a stillness, a great wave that covers, and the highest as a protective canopy and lofty vault—raised without support, held together without fastening. Then He adorned them with the light of stars and the brightness of meteors, and suspended within them the radiant sun and the luminous moon, beneath a turning sphere, a moving ceiling, and a rotating firmament.

⟪ar-quote:ثُمَّ أَنْشَأَ سُبْحَانَهُ رِيحاً اعْتَقَمَ مَهَبَّهَا، وَأَدَامَ مُرَبَّهَا، وَأَعْصَفَ مَجْرَاها، وَأَبْعَدَ مَنْشَاهَا۔ فَأَمَرَها بِتَصْفِيقِ المَاءِ الزَّخَّارِ، وَإِثَارَةِ مَوْجِ البِحَارِ، فَمَخَضَتْهُ مَخْضَ السِّقَاءِ، وَعَصَفَتْ بهِ عَصْفَهَا بِالفَضَاءِ، تَرُدُّ أَوَّلَهُ عَلَى آخِرِهِ، وَسَاجِيَهُ عَلَى مَائِرِهِ، حَتَّى عَبَّ عُبَابُهُ، وَرَمَى بِالزَّبَدِ رُكَامُهُ، فَرَفَعَهُ فِي هَوَاء مُنْفَتِق، وَجَوٍّ مُنْفَهِق، فَسَوَّى مِنْهُ سَبْعَ سَموَات، جَعَلَ سُفْلاَهُنَّ مَوْجاً مَكْفُوفاً، وَعُلْيَاهُنَّ سَقْفاً مَحْفُوظاً، وَسَمْكاً مَرْفُوعاً، بِغَيْر عَمَد يَدْعَمُهَا، وَلا دِسَار يَنْظِمُها. ثُمَّ زَيَّنَهَا بِزينَةِ الكَوَاكِبِ، وَضِياءِ الثَّوَاقِبِ، وَأَجْرَى فِيها سِرَاجاً مُسْتَطِيراً، وَقَمَراً مُنِيراً- في فَلَك دَائِر، وَسَقْف سَائِر، وَرَقِيم مَائِر⟫

## Creation of the Angels

Then He opened the spaces between the lofty heavens and filled them with all grades of His angels (malāʾika). Among them are those immersed in prostration, never rising. Others are bowed in kneeling, never standing upright. Some stand arrayed in rank, never departing their stations. Others glorify the Lord with constant praise, never wearying. Sleep does not touch their eyes, nor does heedlessness cloud their minds, nor does the languor of body affect them, nor the veil of forgetfulness.[^cite-5]

Among them are the trustees of His revelation, tongues that speak for His prophets, and bearers who convey His decrees and commands to and fro. Among them are the guardians of His servants and the keepers of the gates of Paradise's gardens.

Among them too are those whose feet stand firm upon the lowest earth, whose necks stretch into the highest heavens, whose limbs extend through all regions, whose shoulders align with the pillars of the Divine Throne, whose gaze is lowered before it, who enfold themselves beneath its expanse with their wings, between whom and all else there stretch veils of might and curtains of power. They do not conceive of their Creator through image, nor do they attribute to Him the qualities of created things, nor do they confine Him within places, nor do they gesture toward Him through comparisons.

⟪ar-quote:ثُمَّ فَتَقَ مَا بَيْنَ السَّمواتِ العُلاَ، فَمَلاَهُنَّ أَطْوَاراً مِنْ مَلائِكَتِهِ- مِنْهُمْ سُجُودٌ لاَيَرْكَعُونَ، وَرُكُوعٌ لاَ يَنْتَصِبُونَ، وَصَافُّونَ لاَ يَتَزَايَلُونَ، وَمُسَبِّحُونَ لاَ يَسْأَمُونَ، لاَ يَغْشَاهُمْ نَوْمُ العُيُونِ، وَلاَ سَهْوُ العُقُولِ، وَلاَ فَتْرَةُ الاَبْدَانِ، ولاَ غَفْلَةُ النِّسْيَانِ. وَمِنْهُمْ أُمَنَاءُ عَلَى وَحْيِهِ، وأَلسِنَةٌ إِلَى رُسُلِهِ، وَمُخْتَلِفُونَ بِقَضَائِهِ وَأَمْرهِ. وَمِنْهُمُ الحَفَظَةُ لِعِبَادِهِ، وَالسَّدَنَةُ لاِبْوَابِ جِنَانِهِ. وَمِنْهُمُ الثَّابِتَةُ في الاْرَضِينَ السُّفْلَى أَقْدَامُهُمْ، وَالمَارِقَةُ مِنَ السَّماءِ العُلْيَا أَعْنَاقُهُمْ، والخَارجَةُ مِنَ الاْقْطَارِ أَرْكَانُهُمْ، وَالمُنَاسِبَةُ لِقَوَائِمِ العَرْشِ أَكْتَافُهُمْ، نَاكِسَةٌ دُونَهُ أَبْصارُهُمْ، مُتَلَفِّعُونَ تَحْتَهُ بِأَجْنِحَتِهِمْ، مَضْرُوبَةٌ بَيْنَهُمْ وَبَيْنَ مِنْ دُونَهُمْ حُجُبُ العِزَّةِ، وَأسْتَارُ القُدْرَةِ، لاَ يَتَوَهَّمُونَ رَبَّهُمْ بالتَّصْوِيرِ، وَلاَ يُجْرُونَ عَلَيْهِ صِفَاتِ المَصْنُوعِينَ، وَلاَ يَحُدُّونَهُ بِالاْماكِنِ، وَلاَ يُشِيرُونَ إِلَيْهِ بِالنَّظَائِرِ⟫

## Creation of Adam

Then the Most High gathered earth from rocky terrain and soft plain, from sweet and bitter soil. He mixed it with water until it became pure, then kneaded it with moisture until it grew adhesive. From this he formed a figure—marked with joints, articulations, limbs, and segments. He hardened it until it dried completely for an appointed term and known duration. Then He breathed into it of His Spirit, and it took the form of a human being possessed of intellect that governs him, understanding by which he acts, limbs that serve him, senses that move him, and sagacity that distinguishes between truth and falsehood, tastes and scents, colors and kinds.[^cite-6]

He was molded from clays of varied hues and linked substances, from opposing extremes and divergent properties—heat and cold, moisture and dryness, softness and hardness, sorrow and joy.

⟪ar-quote:ثُمَّ جَمَعَ سُبْحَانَهُ مِنْ حَزْنِ الاْرْضِ وَسَهْلِهَا، وَعَذْبِهَا وَسَبَخِهَا، تُرْبَةً سَنَّهَا بالمَاءِ حَتَّى خَلَصَتْ، وَلاَطَهَا بِالبَلَّةِ حَتَّى لَزَبَتْ، فَجَبَلَ مِنْها صُورَةً ذَاتَ أَحْنَاء وَوُصُول، وَأَعْضَاء وَفُصُول- أَجْمَدَهَا حَتَّى اسْتَمْسَكَتْ، وَأَصْلَدَهَا حَتَّى صَلْصَلَتْ، لِوَقْت مَعْدُود، وَأجَل مَعْلُوم، ثُمَّ نَفَخَ فِيها مِنْ رُوحِهِ فَمَثُلَتْ إِنْساناً ذَا أَذْهَان يُجيلُهَا، وَفِكَر يَتَصَرَّفُ بِهَا، وَجَوَارِحَ يَخْتَدِمُهَا، وَأَدَوَات يُقَلِّبُهَا، وَمَعْرِفَة يَفْرُقُ بِهَا بَيْنَ الحَقِّ وَالبَاطِلِ، والاَذْوَاقِ والَمشَامِّ، وَالاْلوَانِ وَالاْجْنَاس، مَعْجُوناً بطِينَةِ الاْلوَانِ الُمخْتَلِفَةِ، وَالاَشْبَاهِ المُؤْتَلِفَةِ، وَالاْضْدَادِ المُتَعَادِيَةِ، والاْخْلاطِ المُتَبَايِنَةِ، مِنَ الحَرِّ والبَرْدِ، وَالبَلَّةِ وَالْجُمُودِ، وَالْمَساءَةِ وَالسُّرُورِ⟫

Then the Most High exacted from the angels the pledge of His covenant concerning them and the bond of His command, calling them to bow in prostration to Adam and to submit to His glorification. Thus spoke the Most High: *"Be prostrate toward Adam, and they prostrated, save Iblis."*[^cite-7] ⟪quran 2:34⟫ Pride prevented him and wretchedness overcame him. He gloried in his creation of fire and despised the fashioning of clay. Therefore the Almighty granted him respite—to fulfill His wrathful judgment and to complete humanity's trial and to execute His promise. And He said: *"Verily, thou shalt be among the respited until the Day of the Known Time."*[^cite-8] ⟪quran 15:38⟫

⟪ar-quote:وَاسْتَأْدَى اللهُ سُبْحَانَهُ المَلائكَةَ وَدِيعَتَهُ لَدَيْهِمْ، وَعَهْدَ وَصِيَّتِهِ إِلَيْهِمْ، في الاْذْعَانِ بالسُّجُودِ لَهُ، وَالخُنُوعِ لِتَكْرِمَتِهِ، فَقَالَ عزَّمِن قائِل- ( اسْجُدُوا لاِدَمَ فَسَجَدُوا إِلاَّ إِبْلِيسَ)، اعْتَرَتْهُ الحَمِيَّةُ، وَغَلَبَتْ عَلَيْهِ الشِّقْوَةُ، وَتَعَزَّزُ بِخِلْقَةِ النَّارِ، وَاسْتَوْهَنَ خَلْقَ الصَّلْصَالِ، فَأَعْطَاهُ اللهُ تَعالَى النَّظِرَةَ اسْتِحْقَاقاً لِلسُّخْطَةِ، وَاسْتِتْماماً لِلْبَلِيَّةِ، وَإِنْجَازاً لِلْعِدَةِ، فَقَالَ- (إنَّكَ مِنَ المُنْظَرِينَ إِلَى يَوْمِ الْوَقْتِ الْمَعْلُومِ)۔⟫

Thereafter the Most High made Adam to dwell in a house where He granted him ease of life and security of habitation. He warned him against Iblis and his enmity. Yet his enemy, through envy of Adam's dwelling in Paradise and his companionship with the righteous, beguiled him. He transformed Adam's certainty into doubt and his resolve into weakness. He traded his joy for fear and his honor for shame. Then the Most High extended to Adam the occasion for repentance, taught him the words of His Mercy, promised him return to His Paradise, and sent him down to the place of trial and the generation of offspring.

⟪ar-quote:ثُمَّ أَسْكَنَ سُبْحَانَهُ آدَمَ دَاراً أرْغَدَ فِيهَاعَيْشَهُ، وَآمَنَ فِيهَا مَحَلَّتَهُ، وَحَذَّرَهُ إِبْلِيسَ وَعَدَاوَتَهُ، فَاغْتَرَّهُ عَدُوُّهُ نَفَاسَةً عَلَيْهِ بِدَارِ الْمُقَامِ، وَمُرَافَقَةِ الاْبْرَارِ، فَبَاعَ الْيَقِينَ بِشَكِّهِ، وَالعَزِيمَةَ بِوْهْنِهِ، وَاسْتَبْدَلَ بِالْجَذَلِ وَجَلاً، وَبِالاْغْتِرَارِ نَدَماً. ثُمَّ بَسَطَ اللهُ سُبْحَانَهُ لَهُ في تَوْبَتِهِ، وَلَقَّاهُ كَلِمَةَ رَحْمَتِهِ، وَوَعَدَهُ المَرَدَّ إِلَى جَنَّتِهِ، فَأَهْبَطَهُ إِلَى دَارِ الَبَلِيَّةِ، وَتَنَاسُلِ الذُّرِّيَّةِ⟫

## Allah Choosing His Prophets

From his progeny, the Most High selected prophets and took from them the solemn covenant concerning His revelation and the trust of bearing His message. Yet when most of His creatures abandoned the covenant of Allah with them—when they forgot His right and took rivals alongside Him—Satan turned them away from knowing Him and severed them from His worship. Therefore Allah sent forth His Messengers and sent in succession His Prophets to call them to fulfill the covenants of His creation (fitrah), to remind them of His neglected blessings, to exhort them through preaching, to unveil for them the treasures of intellect hidden within, and to show them the signs of His Power: the sky raised above them, the earth spread beneath them, the means of sustenance that give them life, the deaths that end them, the maladies that make them infirm, and the events that succeed one another in their affairs.

⟪ar-quote:وَاصْطَفَى سُبْحَانَهُ مِنْ وَلَدَهِ أَنْبيَاءَ أَخَذَ عَلَى الْوَحْيِ مِيثَاقَهُم، وَعَلَى تَبْليغِ الرِّسَالَةِ أَمَانَتَهُمْ، لَمَّا بَدَّلَ أَكْثَرُ خَلْقِهِ عَهْدَ اللهِ إِلَيْهِمْ، فَجَهِلُوا حَقَّهُ، واتَّخَذُوا الاْنْدَادَ مَعَهُ، وَاجْتَالَتْهُمُ الشَّيَاطِينُ عَنْ مَعْرفَتِهِ، وَاقتَطَعَتْهُمْ عَنْ عِبَادَتِهِ، فَبَعَثَ فِيهمْ رُسُلَهُ، وَوَاتَرَ إِلَيْهِمْ أَنْبِياءَهُ، لِيَسْتَأْدُوهُمْ مِيثَاقَ فِطْرَتِهِ، وَيُذَكِّرُوهُمْ مَنْسِيَّ نِعْمَتِهِ، وَيَحْتَجُّوا عَلَيْهِمْ بَالتَّبْلِيغِ، وَيُثِيرُوا لَهُمْ دَفَائِنَ الْعُقُولِ، وَيُرُوهُمْ آيَاتِ الْمَقْدِرَةِ- مِنْ سَقْف فَوْقَهُمْ مَرْفُوع، وَمِهَاد تَحْتَهُمْ مَوْضُوع، وَمَعَايِشَ تُحْيِيهِمْ، وَآجَال تُفْنِيهمْ، وَأَوْصَاب(5) تُهْرِمُهُمْ، وَأَحْدَاث تَتَابَعُ عَلَيْهِمْ⟫

The Most High never left His creation without a Prophet sent by Him, a Scripture revealed from Him, a binding proof, or an established sign. The Messengers—few in number though they were—never felt diminished by the multitude of those who rejected them. Among them was a predecessor who named the one to follow, or a follower who was introduced by his predecessor.

⟪ar-quote:لَمْ يُخْلِ اللهُ سُبْحَانَهُ خَلْقَهُ مِنْ نَبِيٍّ مُرْسَل، أَوْ كِتَاب مُنْزَل، أَوْ حُجَّة لاَزِمَة، أَوْ مَحَجَّة قَائِمَة، رُسُلٌ لا تُقَصِّرُ بِهِمْ قِلَّةُ عَدَدِهِمْ، وَلاَ كَثْرَةُ المُكَذِّبِينَ لَهُمْ- مِنْ سَابِق سُمِّيَ لَهُ مَنْ بَعْدَهُ، أَوْ غَابِر عَرَّفَهُ مَنْ قَبْلَهُ⟫

## The Final Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

In this manner ages passed and times rolled on. Fathers departed and children succeeded them, until the Most High sent forth Muhammad, peace be upon him and his progeny, as His Prophet—fulfilling His promise and completing His prophethood. The covenant was taken from the prophets concerning him; his moral characteristics were renowned and his noble birth was established. The people of the earth in that age were divided into sects, their intentions scattered, and their paths diverged. Some fashioned likenesses for Allah to rival His creation, others corrupted His Names, and still others turned away to other than Him. Through Muhammad, Allah guided them from error, and by his station delivered them from ignorance.[^cite-9]

⟪ar-quote:عَلَى ذْلِكَ نَسَلَتِ القُرُونُ، وَمَضَتِ الدُّهُورُ، وَسَلَفَتِ الاْباءُ، وَخَلَفَتِ الاْبْنَاءُ. الَى أَنْ بَعَثَ اللهُ سُبْحَانَهُ مُحَمَّداً(صلى الله عليه وآله) لاِنْجَازِ عِدَتِهِ وَتَمامِ نُبُوَّتِهِ، مَأْخُوذاً عَلَى النَّبِيِّينَ مِيثَاقُهُ، مَشْهُورَةً سِمَاتُهُ، كَرِيماً مِيلادُهُ. وَأهْلُ الاْرْضِ يَوْمَئِذ مِلَلٌ مُتَفَرِّقَةٌ، وَأَهْوَاءٌ مُنْتَشِرَةٌ، وَطَرَائِقُ مُتَشَتِّتَةٌ، بَيْنَ مُشَبِّه للهِ بِخَلْقِهِ، أَوْ مُلْحِد في اسْمِهِ، أَوْ مُشِير إِلَى غَيْرهِ، فَهَدَاهُمْ بهِ مِنَ الضَّلاَلَةِ، وَأَنْقَذَهُمْ بمَكانِهِ مِنَ الجَهَالَةِ⟫

Then the Most High chose for Muhammad to meet Him, accepted him for His own presence, deemed him too noble to abide in this transitory world, and resolved to remove him from the place of trial. Thus He drew him toward Himself with honor. May Allah bestow blessings upon him and upon his progeny.

⟪ar-quote:ثُمَّ اخْتَارَ سُبْحَانَهُ لِمحَمَّد صلى الله عليه لِقَاءَهُ، وَرَضِيَ لَهُ مَا عِنْدَهُ، فَأَكْرَمَهُ عَنْ دَارِالدُّنْيَا، وَرَغِبَ بِهِ عَنْ مُقَارَنَةِ البَلْوَى، فَقَبَضَهُ إِلَيْهِ كَرِيماً صَلَّى اللهُ علَيهِ و آلِهِ⟫

## The Holy Qur'an and Sunnah

The Prophet left among you that which all Prophets left among their peoples—for the Prophets did not abandon you to wandering without a clear path or standing sign. He left the Book of your Lord (al-Qurʾān), clarifying what is permitted and what is forbidden, what is obligatory and what is discretionary, what abrogates and what stands abrogated, what is concise and what is expansive, what is permissible and what is binding, what is particular and what is universal, offering lessons and parables, the lengthy and the concise, the manifest and the obscure—explaining its summary meanings and clarifying its dark passages.[^cite-10]

⟪ar-quote:وَخَلَّفَ فِيكُمْ مَا خَلَّفَتِ الاْنْبيَاءُ في أُمَمِها، إذْ لَم يَتْرُكُوهُمْ هَمَلاً، بِغَيْر طَريق واضِح، ولاَعَلَمٍ قَائِم. كِتَابَ رَبِّكُمْ [فِيكُمْ-] مُبَيِّناً حَلاَلَهُ وَحَرامَهُ، وَفَرَائِضَهُ وَفَضَائِلَهُ، وَنَاسِخَهُ وَمَنْسُوخَهُ، وَرُخَصَهُ وَعَزَائِمَهُ، وَخَاصَّهُ وَعَامَّهُ، وَعِبَرَهُ وَأَمْثَالَهُ، وَمُرْسَلَهُ وَمَحْدُودَهُ، وَمُحْكَمَهُ وَمُتَشَابِهَهُ، مُفَسِّراً جُمَلَهُ، وَمُبَيِّناً غَوَامِضَهُ⟫

Within it exist verses the knowledge of which is incumbent upon you, and verses concerning which it is permitted to you to be ignorant. It contains that which appears obligatory according to the Book but whose abrogation is indicated by the Sunnah (the practice and teachings of the Prophet), and that which appears obligatory according to the Sunnah but which the Book permits you to set aside. There are likewise ordinances binding for a given time but which lose their force in times to come. The prohibitions differ: some are grave, carrying the threat of Hell-fire, whilst others are slight, with hope of pardon. And there are those of which a small portion suffices, yet they are capable of expansion.[^cite-11]

⟪ar-quote:بَيْنَ مَأْخُوذ مِيثَاقُ عِلْمِهِ، وَمُوَسَّع عَلَى العِبَادِ في جَهْلِهِ، وَبَيْنَ مُثْبَت في الكِتابِ فَرْضُهُ، وَمَعْلُوم في السُّنَّهِ نَسْخُهُ، وَوَاجب في السُّنَّةِ أَخْذُهُ، وَمُرَخَّص في الكِتابِ تَرْكُهُ، وَبَيْنَ وَاجِب بِوَقْتِهِ، وَزَائِل في مُسْتَقْبَلِهِ، وَمُبَايَنٌ بَيْنَ مَحَارِمِهِ، مِنْ كَبير أَوْعَدَ عَلَيْهِ نِيرَانَهُ، أَوْ صَغِير أَرْصَدَ لَهُ غُفْرَانَهُ، وَبَيْنَ مَقْبُول في أَدْنَاهُ، ومُوَسَّع في أَقْصَاهُ⟫

## The Duty of Hajj

Allah has made obligatory upon you the pilgrimage (ḥajj) to His Sacred House—the turning-point toward which those who journey repair like beasts clustering around water in spring, like doves circling toward their nests. The Almighty made it a sign of their humbling before His Greatness and their submission to His Majesty. He chose from His creation those who answered His call and confirmed His word, who stood in the stations of His Prophets and resembled His angels encompassing the Divine Throne—securing the profits of His worship and hastening toward His promised forgiveness. The Almighty made it a standard for Islam and a sanctuary for those who turn toward it. He imposed its observance as obligatory and established its claim as something for which you bear responsibility. Thus spoke the Almighty: *"And to Allah is due from mankind pilgrimage to the House—for those who have the means to reach it. And whosoever denies, then verily Allah is self-sufficient, having no need of the worlds."*[^cite-12] ⟪quran 3:96⟫

⟪ar-quote:وَفَرَضَ عَلَيْكُمْ حَجَّ بَيْتِهِ الحَرَامِ، الَّذِي جَعَلَهُ قِبْلَةً لِلاْنَامِ، يَرِدُونَهُ وُرُودَ الاَنْعَامِ، وَيأْلَهُونَ إِلَيْهِ وُلُوهَ الحَمَامِ. جَعَلَهُ سُبْحَانَهُ عَلاَمَةً لِتَوَاضُعِهِمْ لِعَظَمَتِهِ، وَإِذْعَانِهِمْ لِعِزَّتِهِ، وَاخْتَارَ مِنْ خَلْقِهِ سُمَّاعاً أَجَابُوا إِلَيْهِ دَعْوَتَهُ، وَصَدَّقُوا كَلِمَتِهِ، وَوَقَفُوا مَوَاقِفَ أَنْبِيَائِهِ، وَتَشَبَّهُوا بمَلاَئِكَتِهِ المُطِيفِينَ بِعَرْشِهِ، يُحْرِزُونَ الاْرْبَاحَ فِي مَتْجَرِ عِبَادَتِهِ، وَيَتَبَادَرُونَ عِنْدَهُ مَوْعِدَ مَغْفِرَتِهِ. جَعَلَهُ سُبْحَانَهُ لِلاِسْلامِ عَلَماً، وَلِلْعَائِذِينَ حَرَماً، فَرَضَ حَجَّهُ، وَأَوْجَبَ حَقَّهُ، وَكَتَبَ عَلَيْكُمْ وِفَادَتَهُ، فَقَالَ سُبْحَانَهُ- (وَللهِ عَلَى النَّاسِ حِجُّ الْبَيْتِ مَنِ اسْتَطَاعَ إِلَيْهِ سَبِيلاً وَمَنْ كَفَرَ فَإِنَّ اللهَ غَنِيٌّ عَنِ العَالَمينَ)⟫.
