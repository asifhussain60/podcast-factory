But the companions who gathered with Imam Hussein in the field of Karbala were the purest and most sincere.

Man is frustrated and upset because instead of pleasing his Lord, he is busy making people secret and happy.   (Ali (a.s.)

Your good times tell the world who you are, and your bad times tell you what the world is!  (Ali (a.s.)

If you see a man's virtue, describe him! But if it is flawed, there is a test of your virtue.   (Ali (a.s.)

Silence is worship without effort, silence is awe without empire, silence is a fortress without a wall, silence is success without weapons. 

Silence is the fortress of the believers, Shiva is the strength of the humble, the dominance of the wise, the repository of wisdom, the answer of the ignorant.

People believe in four things of Allah, but they act against Him:

1. They consider themselves to be the slaves of Allah, but they act freely.

2. They say that Allah is the Protector of His sustenance, but their hearts are not satisfied with this.

3. And they say, "The Hereafter is better than this world, except that they hasten wealth for this world, and the Hereafter is sin."

4. They say, "We are indeed going to die, but we act in such a way that we will never die."

That foundation, the building that is never deserted, is justice

The bitterness that ends sweet is patience.

The sweetness that has a bitter end is lust

The disease for which there is no cure is ignorance.

The calamity from which a person should flee is luxury

Never seek three things, for you will never find. 

1. A scholar whose knowledge is complete on the scale of action

2. An agent whose sincerity is equal to his knowledge

3. A brother who is flawless, and has no defects or defects.

Man is not given anything in this world except that his share of the Hereafter is diminished

Religion is the real intellect, the intellect is the real knowledge and the real knowledge is patience

The wise attain exaltation by lowering himself, and the ignorant exalts himself by showing himself to be great

The test of the brave is in the battlefield, the test of the friend is in the time of adversity, and the test of the wise is in the state of anger >and wrath.

Four things are among the hardest actions: forgiving when you are angry, giving charity when you are in poverty, keeping yourself chaste even when you are alone, and not having hope or fear of anyone when you say the right thing

Three intoxicants climb very fast: the intoxication of wealth, the intoxication of beauty, and the intoxication of knowledge. The first two addictions are declining and are about to end, but the addiction of knowledge is progressive and advancing.

High morals are in these three things: forgiving even when you have the power to take revenge, being humble even in a state of humiliation, and giving without asking and without hope.

Ten things eat away at ten things: goodness, evil, arrogance of knowledge, repentance of sin, falsehood of sustenance, justice of oppression, charity, anger of intellect, sorrow of life, shame and generosity, backbiting of good deeds.

There are six major disadvantages for the wealthy:

1. Always sad, restless and restless

2. There is always a lack and a loss in his worship

3. It increases disobedience to God.

4. He's going to have to give more account.

5. Reduces the payment of people's rights

6. Reward is less

There are six benefits for the poor

1. There are fewer sorrows and more satisfaction.

2. He remembers God more.

3. Protected from enmity and enmity

4. Calculations are reduced

5. Punishment is also reduced.

6.
