### Physical Life:

Physical life is really just the self's use of one's own body. When the soul stops using its body, it is called physical death. In the same way, consciousness is nothing but the self using the bodily senses. And sleep is just the name of giving up the use of the senses of the self for a while.

### Soul of Life:

In contrast to physical life, the soul is an essence that exists from its own personality, and it is not dependent on anyone else for its existence, just as the soul is dependent on the body for physical life. The soul is (alive) in ⟪ar:’حی‘⟫ its essence, ⟪ar:’بالفعل‘⟫ ⟪ar:علامۃ بالقوۃ⟫(the knower and wise about its powers and powers). He manifests his actions in his body, his forms, his soul and his forms. For example, when the soul wants to worship, it makes the body complete the parts of the prayer. The body cannot do anything unless it ⟪ar:’محرک‘⟫ exists. The life of the body is to be in the neighbourhood of the soul and to be close to it. That is, the body should always be close to its self. Just as the air is dark in its essence, it is illuminated by the proximity of the light of the sun, the moon, and the quake.
