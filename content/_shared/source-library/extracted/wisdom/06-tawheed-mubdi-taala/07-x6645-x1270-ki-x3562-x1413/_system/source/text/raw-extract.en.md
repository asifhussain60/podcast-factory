(2) Doors of the Gate of Muhammad (PBUH)

There are belts in this universe: one that is visible (the physical world) and the other that is invisible (the spiritual world). What is visible are the worlds in or below the constellation belt which are surrounded by three dimensions. This world is the opposite of the self. What is not visible are the celestial sphere, or the worlds above it, which are not enclosed in three dimensions. This world is the opposite of the universe. The vastness of the heavens is infinite and it is the abode of the Holy Spirits. Circumference refers to that which cannot be encompassed, and it refers to the one who surrounds it, which is only Allah.

Doors of Bab Ali (3)

These three letters are essential for the word La ilaha illa Allah. If any of these letters are omitted or any other letters are added, the word will not be recited and will be destroyed. When the Day of Judgment comes, everything will be destroyed. The force that governs the universe is actually Ali's three forces, which are Alif, Lam and Ha. There are three types of forces in the universe.

There are 3 physical forces that affect the orbit of the world, which Maulana Ali explains: "Movement from the sky to the earth, from the earth to the sky, from the sky to the earth, from the sky to the earth."

The first force is the one that is pressing the Kaaba from the constellation (centerifugal). (7) Chapters

Doors of Bab Hassan (7)

The earth is divided into seven places where the seven lights of Allah are flowing. That is, Saturn, Jupiter, Mars, Sun, Venus, Mercury and the moon are released. All the heavens are circumambulating around the Kaaba, which is the center of the universe.

Planets are those that revolve around the Earth and rotate around themselves. Stars are those that only revolve around the earth but do not rotate in their place.
