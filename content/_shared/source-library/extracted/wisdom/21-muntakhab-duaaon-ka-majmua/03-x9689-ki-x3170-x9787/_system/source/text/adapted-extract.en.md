# Ten Daily Duʿāʾs

This chapter from KASHKOLE presents a selection of devotional invocations organized by the days of the week, each designed to align the believer's heart and mind with the rhythms of spiritual practice and cosmic order. The duʿāʾ (invocation, supplication) stands as a fundamental expression of the human soul's yearning toward the Divine, and these daily prayers embody the tradition of personal communion that runs through Ismaili spirituality.
