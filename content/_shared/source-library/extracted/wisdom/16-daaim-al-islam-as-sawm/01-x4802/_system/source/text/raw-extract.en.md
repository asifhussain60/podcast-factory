### Outward fasting:

Every adult Muslim is obliged to fast for one month in a year, known as '⟪ar:رمضان الحرام⟫', which lasts for 30 days without missing one day. In the state of fasting, the Muslim restrains himself from all halal animal habits (eating, drinking, gathering) during the day in obedience to Allah, which he used to perform unhindered in the other months. It is mentioned in the Hadith that Ramadan is one of the names of Allah and is the best of all the months. In this month, angels can be seen and the gates of heaven are opened for good deeds.
