## The Repentance of the Second Emanator

⟪ar:توحید مبدع تعالی⟫ The intellect took the first place of unity and the realm of unity. The first intellect (al-ʿAql al-Awwal, the First Intellect) took the place of the first intellect and started the invitation at his command, which was accepted by the seven intellects and their circles. In this way, the first source became the leader of all the other intellects, and thus the circles of eight intellects were established in the spiritual world after the first intellect.

In the meantime, the companion of the first man—the second emanator (al-Munbaʿith al-Thānī, the Second Emanator)—was left behind due to his illusion of equality. The rest of the world joined him in this negligence. When the second intellect fell below the horizon of the ninth intellect, descending through the ranks to the tenth, he realized his own ⟪ar:انحدار⟫ degraded status. He woke from his negligence and contemplated his error: what was the reason that all of them had achieved perfection while he was left behind? He approached the First Source with hope, praying to the nearest to Him—the last person of the ninth intellect—and asking for His mercy, making Him the best means of salvation.[^cite-1] The last person of the ninth intellect reminded him of his sin. All he thought was that he had denied the primacy and greatness of his companion, the first man, which caused him to be left behind. He confessed his mistake with sincere remorse.

#### Origin & Duration

The first person to repent in the spiritual world was the second emanator. Prior to this, the practice of Tawheed (tawḥīd, the declaration of divine unity) was taking place, but the second one repented first and then affirmed Tawheed. The last person of the ninth intellect explained to the second man that he could be forgiven only by the one to whom he had sinned. But when the second source tried to turn to the first source, the world of all intellects stood in front of him. He could not find any means of accessing the first source except the last person of the ninth intellect. Therefore, he became humble and destitute, and in order to offer forgiveness to his Lord, he made the last person of the ninth realm of the intellect a medium and offered his repentance to Him.[^cite-2]

He only bestowed His mercy and compassion on the second source, and became the gateway from himself to the first source. He brought the remorse of the second source to the upper circles, until his repentance reached the circle of the first source. From the ninth intellect to the twenty-seven persons in the context of the first intellect, all interceded for the second intellect.[^cite-3] He submitted that the fault of the second source was not intentional, because he had not lived up to his own equality. Through the intercession of all of them, the first intellect accepted the repentance of the second and forgave him. With forgiveness, the first intellect poured upon him the rays of perfection with His mercy through all the intellects, for Allah is the Almighty.

⟪quran 3:89⟫
⟪ar:‏إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ تَابُوا۟ مِنۢ بَعْدِ ذَلِكَ وَأَصْلَحُوا۟ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌۭ رَّحِيمٌ ‎⟫

> *Except for those who repent after that and do righteous deeds. Verily, Allah is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.*

When someone makes a mistake and performs an act of sincere repentance, Allah does two things in return: the first is to forgive him, and the second is to have mercy upon him.

#### Repentance and Return to Obedience

⟪ar:' توبہ'⟫ Repentance (tawba) means he asks for forgiveness for his mistake, while ⟪ar:'انابت'⟫ return to obedience (ānāba) means to act and try to correct his mistake. For example, if Hamid tells Ahmad something wrong and later realizes his mistake, he will first regret his mistake and ask for forgiveness from Ahmad—that is his repentance. Upon receiving Ahmad's pardon, it becomes obligatory for Hamid to tell the right thing to Ahmed—that is his return to obedience. Doing both repentance and return to obedience corrects the mistake, which accords with the negation and affirmation inherent in the testimony ⟪ar:لا الہ الا اللہ⟫ (there is no deity save Allah).

For this reason, it was not enough for Asher to repent merely out of regret. Allah (swt) also made it obligatory for Asher to guide the disobedient group who had been corrupted by seeing him and to promote him to the second perfection (kamāl thānī, secondary perfection). As our master al-Muʾayyad—may Allah sanctify his spirit—says: ⟪ar-quote:من کسر عظم فعلیہ جبرُ⟫ "It is obligatory for him who breaks a bone to mend it."

#### Facts

Asher was ordered to summon the entire group and lead them out of their error. The first to accept his invitation were the settlers, who ⟪ar:کلمم⟫ are the ⟪ar:محتجبین⟫ (those who veil themselves). Then ⟪ar:مستقرین⟫ (those firmly established) were born by ⟪ar:مستودعین⟫ (those in whose keeping) the ⟪ar:حدود اور حجب⟫ (limits and veils) by the invitation of the last person. ⟪ar:مستقرین⟫ All and ⟪ar:مستودعین⟫ others became witnesses to ⟪ar:مستقرین⟫ Asher that Asher feasted. It was actually Asher's ⟪ar:انابت⟫ return to obedience.

#### Those Who Repent with His Repentance and Those Who Answer the Summons

Asher repented of his own self without any compulsion ⟪ar:ملحم⟫. As soon as he repented, seeing him, a group of people repented with him, which ⟪ar-quote:تاَبُو بِتَوبَتِہِ⟫ was called the group of *those who repent with his repentance*. The group did not need to be invited. He ⟪ar:دروس⟫ taught ⟪ar:انبعاث⟫ emanation to himself and his repentance to Asher, and with Asher he attained perfection. Then ⟪ar:تابو بتوبتہ⟫ the last person was commanded to invite all the people to repentance and tawheed, and that is in reality Asher. So Asher invited the whole disobedient group. A group ⟪ar:لَبَّیک⟫ of people responded to his invitation and accepted his call. These groups ⟪ar-quote:لَبُّ اَلنِّدَاہُ⟫ were called *those who answer the summons*. They also joined the circle of Asher and all of them attained perfection.

## The Acceptance of Repentance Through Words

In the story of Adam, Allah says in the Qur'an: "Adam received from ⟪ar:' کلمات'⟫ his Lord and repented through these words, and Allah accepted his repentance." Indeed, Allah is the Acceptor of Repentance, the Merciful. That is, Adam had to resort to ⟪ar:کلمات⟫ words for repentance. In the larger circle, this verse refers ⟪ar:' آدم روحانی'⟫ to Adam, who is the second source—the Spiritual Adam—and ⟪ar:'رب'⟫ the Lord refers to the first source. In addition, this verse ⟪ar:تواب⟫ contains the word "the Recipient of Repentance" and ⟪ar:رحیم⟫ "the Merciful," which refer to the two blessings of Allah.

⟪quran 2:37⟫
⟪ar:‏فَتَلَقَّىٰٓ آدَمُ مِن رَّبِّهِۦ كَلِمَتٍۢ فَتَابَ عَلَيْهِ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ هُوَ ٱلتَّوَّابُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ ‎⟫

> *Then Adam received from his Lord Words. And his Lord pardoned him (accepted his repentance). Verily, He is the One Who forgives (accepts repentance), the Most Merciful.*

#### Interpretation

Wherever Adam is mentioned in the Qur'an, Asher is referred to in a sense, because Asher is the first Adam to be established in the spiritual world. ⟪ar:الکلمات⟫ There are seven letters that signify the seven intellects and their realms. The second source received substance through the intercession of the seven intellects.[^cite-4] These intellects are formed between ⟪ar:الکلمات⟫ the second source (the Spiritual Adam) and Allah (the first intellect).

#### The Alphabetical Principle of Words

Any meaningful utterance ⟪ar:' کلمہ'⟫ is called a word. When letters are combined in a certain order ⟪ar:حروف معجم⟫ to create meaning, it is called a word (kalima). For example, in the order of *Allah*, there is the first alif, followed by two lams, and the last is ha. If the order is changed, the word will change. All these beings were in their primordial state ⟪ar:حروف معجم⟫. When he attained perfection, a certain order of exaltation and limitation was created in him, due to which he ⟪ar:حروف معجم⟫ became a meaningful word, just as the word of Allah became the word of Allah in a certain order of four letters ⟪ar:حروف معجم⟫.

With this pardon, he joined the second emanator ⟪ar:مادۃ القدسانی⟫ and attained perfection and became Asher. When he became perfected, he was separated from the darkness of equality that had overwhelmed him. ⟪ar:عصمت کلی⟫ He attained perfection and became innocent, and he became a luminous intellect and joined the ranks of the illuminating world. For this reason, Allah says (interpretation of the meaning):

⟪quran 89:27-30⟫

> *(It will be said to the pious): "O (you) the one in (complete) rest and satisfaction! Come back to your Lord, Well-pleased (yourself) and well-pleasing unto Him! Enter you, then, among My honoured slaves, And enter you My Paradise!"*

That is, from the second source it was said to the first intellect: "O self that is satisfied! Return to your Lord, the first intellect. When you are pleased with your Lord—that is, you have acknowledged the primacy of the first source and repented sincerely before Him—and He is pleased with you—that is, He has accepted your repentance and forgiven you—join the chain of the Eight, and enter My Paradise, that is, enter the Paradise of the Second Perfection!"

Asher did not grow individually, but with his community. With ⟪ar:تابو بتوبتہ⟫ him is the group of those who repent with his repentance and ⟪ar:لب النداہ⟫ those who answer the summons, and all have attained perfection. ⟪ar:تابو بتوبتہ⟫ There were also twenty-seven guides (rusā), and in some places it is reported that there were one hundred guides, which is indicated by the *tasbīḥ* (glorification) of the Sunnah of Fajr: ⟪ar-quote:سبحان ربی العظیم و بحمدہ استغفر اللہ العظیم و اتوب الیہ⟫ "Glory be to my Lord the Almighty, and by His praise I ask forgiveness from Allah the Almighty and I turn to Him." This is the same glorification that Asher and ⟪ar:تابو بتوبتہ⟫ those who repent with his repentance performed for the first emanator.

## The Essence of the Word in Repentance

⟪ar:توحید رب العالمین⟫ Just as there were three states in doing and in attaining ⟪ar:'الہ'⟫ the perfection of reason, the same three states were created for Asher in repentance.

Realizing his mistake, the second man repented to himself without anyone else ⟪ar:ملحم⟫, just as the first ⟪ar:ملحم⟫ intellect had done tawheed on its own. This is the state of *alif* (A). He repented when he saw Asher, the way the ⟪ar:منبعث اول⟫ first emanator saw ⟪ar:عقل اول⟫ the First Intellect do tawheed. ⟪ar:تابو بتوبتہ⟫ This is the state of *lam* (L). He repented at the invitation of the last person, just as the seven intellects repented at the invitation of the ⟪ar:منبعث اول⟫ first emanator. ⟪ar:لب النداہ⟫ This is the state of *ha* (H).

## The Summons of Asher

When Asher and his circle had ⟪ar:لب النداہ⟫ attained perfection, the last of them, who ⟪ar:'عاشر'⟫ is named Asher, was ordered to invite the rest of the lost and misguided group. Since all of them were corrupted by seeing him, the task of correcting them was also entrusted to Asher. ⟪ar-quote:من کسر عظماً فعلیہ جبرہ⟫ "He who breaks the bone has to mend it."

So he began his summons (daʿwā, the invitation) in the spiritual world and made everyone aware of their mistake and called them to guidance. He told them all: "There is no salvation for you except that you obey Me and obey those who ⟪ar:حبل اللہ المتین⟫ are above Me—the rope of Allah, the most firm." But none of them listened to Asher. They all said in unison: "⟪ar-quote:لَا نُسمِعُ وَلاَ نُطِیع⟫ We do not listen to you and do not obey you." ⟪ar-quote:لاَ فَضلَ لَھُم عَلَینَا⟫ "There is no superiority for them over us."

At that time, darkness began to arise in his personality, and he began to fall from the ranks of perfection. His intentions began to differ and he began to retreat. Of all these, the most fierce in ⟪ar:'ابلیس روحانی'⟫ his stubbornness and denial was he who became the Spiritual Satan, the furthest away from Asher.[^cite-5] Asher was convinced that the souls of this disobedient group had become so oppressed that their density could not be removed by keeping them in the spiritual world. He intended to create a world in which this disobedient group ⟪ar:اشخاص الفیۃ (انسانی قامت)⟫ could be brought into physical form and invited. Those who accept the summons would be elevated to subtlety; those who do not obey would be left in the same world of filth. ⟪ar:قامت الفیۃ⟫ He thought of it because the Creator had created the soul in the spiritual world in the same form—the best of forms. Asher merely tried to remove their spiritual ailments and bring them from darkness to light and from sickness to health.[^cite-6]

Just as the summons to the seven intellects was established in the spiritual world by the command of the first intellect, Asher also intended to establish his summons in the physical world. The difference was that the summons of the first intellect was without time and space, whereas the summons of Asher was within the confines of time and duration. Just as the candle (which is subtle) cannot be removed without its tablet (which is dense), in the same way, in order to remove these beings from the spiritual world, it was necessary to convert the light into physicality.

## The Summons of the Spiritual Satan

In the spiritual world, there are two states of the second source due to which two states have been created. The first state is that which denies the divinity (ilāha) of ⟪ar:نفی الہیت⟫ all things and confesses the Originator (Mubdiʿ), and affirms the primacy of ⟪ar:الہیت⟫ the first intellect. This is called the second source. On the other hand, there is one who turned to ⟪ar:وحم مساوات⟫ the illusion of equality and thought: "We are both equal to the first source." When the second man repented and attained perfection, he was separated from ⟪ar:وحم مساوات⟫ this illusion of equality. ⟪ar:وحم مساوات، ' أبلیس روحانی'⟫ It was the essence of this illusion that was separated and established as a calling in the spiritual world.

| Evil Face | Satanic—consider ourselves and the Originator to be equal; claim equality with the first source |
| | *In the luminous case: he repented. In the evil case: the form of mystery and arrogance.* |

#### The Spiritual Satan

When ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ the oppressive form was separated, it was 'seen' into a form which was called in the terminology 'the Shayṭān' (Satan). Then ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ the Spiritual Satan, by the order of the evil force, established a counter-summons of wickedness, which was accepted by countless people. This satanic summons has the same structure as the merciful summons and is bound by the constraints of the chapters of the *Kalima* (Word) ⟪ar:لا الہ الا اللہ⟫ (there is no deity save Allah).

#### The Misguided Summons

The Spiritual Satan ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ started his summons against Asher and told the disobedient group that it was Asher who led them all astray in this world. "We are all corrupted because of him! What we suffer now is the same as what the second man did, and we did what he did. Because of that, we fell into the hierarchy and reached this point. Now Asher wants us to listen to him again and accept him. How can we assume that if we accept his summons, we will not fall into even greater trouble?" That is, we ⟪ar:' ذات'⟫ are divided in our essence, and should we listen to Asher's words, we fear we will become even more ⟪ar:' نفس'⟫ divided in our soul. He was the most intense ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ in hatred and rebellion against Asher.

⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ The whole disobedient group heard this summons and became arrogant and began to follow the path of hatred he had mentioned. The entire world became involved in this act. Every one of them ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ shook his ears at his word, believed his word, and acted as he did.

## The Existence of Three Sects

When Asher summoned the disobedient group in the world of light, no one listened to him. In contrast to his summons, the counter-summons of the Spiritual Satan was established. He called for the denial of Asher, of all intellects, and even of the Creator. At his summons, the essences began to shake, and three sects arose in this regard.

#### The Penitent

In the beginning, this sect followed the second man, and in the same way, they ⟪ar:نفی الٰہیت⟫ denied the divinity of all things and affirmed the Creator of the Universe. ⟪ar:الٰہیت⟫ Then they acknowledged the primacy of the first intellect, but considered the first source and the second source to be equal in rank. This group became the spouse of the second man because they were corrupted when they saw him. This group was referred to in the terminology as ⟪ar:'حوا'⟫ the Spiritual Eve.

| Adam (Spiritual) | Eve (Spiritual) |
| --- | --- |
| The Second Source | Those who held the same opinion as the Second Source |

Allah says in the Qur'an:

⟪quran 2:35⟫
⟪ar:‏وَقُلْنَا يَٓـَٔادَمُ ٱسْكُنْ أَنتَ وَزَوْجُكَ ٱلْجَنَّةَ وَكُلَا مِنْهَا رَغَدًا حَيْثُ شِئْتُمَا وَلَا تَقْرَبَا هَذِهِ ٱلشَّجَرَةَ فَتَكُونَا مِنَ ٱلظَّلِمِينَ ‎⟫

> *And We said: "O Adam! Dwell you and your wife in the Paradise and eat both of you freely with pleasure and delight of things therein as wherever you will, but come not near this tree or you both will be of the Zalimun (wrong-doers)."*

In this verse, the word "tree" refers ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ to the Spiritual Satan. Those who were moved to sorrow by following the Spiritual Satan were not called penitent until ⟪ar:تابو بتوبتہ⟫ they became those who repent with Asher's repentance and ⟪ar:لب النداہ⟫ those who answer the summons. The ignorant were those who turned their ears to the first ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ saying of the Spiritual Satan and committed the error, then fell and regretted their action. In the beginning, everyone denied Asher's statement. But then a group of them felt sorrow and thought that ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ the Spiritual Satan had prevented us ⟪ar:الملائکۃ⟫ from joining the eight circles. This group ⟪ar:نادمین و مستغفرین⟫ was called the penitent and those who seek forgiveness.

#### The Doubtful and the Perplexed

The second sect was one that did the affirmation of tawheed (tawḥīd) and ⟪ar:تجرید⟫ purification, ⟪ar:تنزیخ⟫ but negated the first intellect from its proper status and saw only the condition of primary perfection (kamāl awwal). They thought that all beings—the first intellect and the first source—and we are equal in rank. Later, when the summons of Asher and ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ the Spiritual Satan were both established in the spiritual world, this group doubted the words of both. They could not decide whether to listen to Asher or ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ to the Spiritual Satan. They were in a state of great surprise and astonishment. This group ⟪ar:شاک و متحیرین⟫ was called the doubtful and the perplexed.

#### The Obstinate and the Arrogant

The third sect was that when ⟪ar:ابلیس روحانی⟫ the summons of the Spiritual Satan was established, they denied the Creator themselves and prevented others from advancing towards the summons of Asher. This sect was most intense in its disbelief and hostility to Asher. From this sect, all the devils (shayāṭīn) were born. This group ⟪ar:مصر و مستکبرین⟫ was called the obstinate and the arrogant.

## The Mention of Asher in the Qur'an

Allah says in various places in the Qur'an:

⟪quran 3:6⟫
⟪ar:‏هُوَ ٱلَّذِى يُصَوِّرُكُمْ فِى ٱلْأَرْحَامِ كَيْفَ يَشَآءُ ۚ لَآ إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ ‎⟫

> *He it is Who shapes you in the wombs as He pleases. La ilaha illa Huwa (none has the right to be worshipped but He), the All-Mighty, the All-Wise.*

⟪quran 66:2⟫
⟪ar:‏قَدْ فَرَضَ ٱللَّهُ لَكُمْ تَحِلَّةَ أَيْمَنِكُمْ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ مَوْلَىٰكُمْ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلْعَلِيمُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ ‎⟫

> *Allah has already ordained for you (O men), the dissolution of your oaths. And Allah is your Maula (Lord, or Master, or Protector, etc.) and He is the All-Knower, the All-Wise.*

⟪quran 59:24⟫
⟪ar:‏هُوَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلْخَلِقُ ٱلْبَارِئُ ٱلْمُصَوِّرُ ۖ لَهُ ٱلْأَسْمَآءُ ٱلْحُسْنَىٰ ۚ يُسَبِّحُ لَهُۥ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَوَتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ ‎⟫

In these verses, ⟪ar:' العزیز الحکیم'⟫ "the All-Mighty, the All-Wise" is used for Asher. ⟪ar:عالِم⟫ Anyone's knowledge is temporary and fragmentary. If he does not repeat and sustain his knowledge consistently, it will be lost after a period of time. But ⟪ar:علیم⟫ the All-Knowing (ʿalīm) refers to knowledge as a permanent and abiding state. His knowledge reaches such perfection that it can never be diminished, destroyed, or wasted.

#### The All-Knowing

When Asher carried out the process of transforming the Divine Subtlety into Physical Form, he was fully aware of how much cleansing ⟪ar:أموار و أکوار و أدوار⟫ (cycles, circles, and eons) this disobedient group would require. He knew: how many Imams and Guides will come in each era, how many Summoners will each have, how many limits (ḥudūd, the boundary-figures of the daʿwa) will each Imam manifest, how the gatherings will be constituted, by whom, and in what numbers they will gather, and how and in how long the entire world will be refined into subtlety. He knew all this knowledge from the beginning.[^cite-7] That is why he was called the All-Knowing.

#### The All-Wise

Moreover, he has made and will continue to make all these decisions with great wisdom. This is why he ⟪ar:حکیم⟫ was called the All-Wise (ḥakīm). In the system that he ⟪ar:جُثَث، قوائم، ائمۃ، حدود دعوت⟫ has established to refine this world—comprising physical bodies, cosmic principles, Imams, and the limits of the summons—he has employed all his wisdom and has not rushed. He did not change or reconsider any of his decisions after making them. That is, he made the right decision the first time, because his planning is inexhaustible in wisdom.

#### Facts

In the same way, in the prayer of the Opening Chapter (Fātiḥa), it is said "the All-Mighty, the All-Wise," which refers to Asher.

⟪ar:عاشر علیہ السلام⟫ Asher—upon him be peace—is also called ⟪ar:سریع الحساب⟫ "the Swift Reckoner" and ⟪ar:سریع العقاب⟫ "the Swift Avenger." That is, the manner in which he transformed the density of the spiritual world into physicality and then established a system to convert this density back into subtlety through the Pillars of Islam (daʿāʾim al-islām) was exceedingly rapid. He divided one body into different parts and made each part independent so that all could develop according to their ability and strength, and the part that remained behind would not stand in the way of the moving part.[^cite-8]

Our components came into being in the form of simple and complex objects in the composition of this world. For example, food. It had to endure the pain of the fire; only then would it be able to nourish us. But water, fruit, and air reach us without any pain. This is because the solid foods we eat—meat and such—are actually our sins that have been placed in the fires, and have returned after suffering punishment and reparation. Some of the calculations that remain are fulfilled by the heat of the fire during cooking. These foods also contain parts from others with whom accounts are settled, and this comes forth as benefit. In this way, many calculations are completed simultaneously. When we eat these foods, they integrate into our body ⟪ar:قامت الفیہ⟫. For this reason, Asher ⟪ar:سریع الحساب⟫ was called the Swift Reckoner, because he was the quickest to complete the calculations. When our clothes become dirty, we do not wash them ourselves—we give them to the washerman! He cleans them and returns them, and we put them on. In the same way, Allah Almighty divides a unit into separate pieces and then reunites them in different ways. Similarly, if there is even a little dirt on our body, we immediately take a bath and clean it. Our prayers and supplications for forgiveness lead to the remission of these small sins.

## We Shall Make Easy the Path of Ease, and Easy the Path of Difficulty

Explaining the inner meanings of these verses, ⟪ar:سیدنا اسماعیل بن ھبۃ⟫ he says in his book ⟪ar:مِزَاج التَّسنِیم⟫ that the good news (al-ḥusnā, the excellence) refers in two ways to two states. ⟪ar:حُسنٰی⟫ In the deeper sense, the good news (al-ḥusnā) is the summons of Asher. #### We Shall Make Easy the Path of Ease

For those who confirmed Asher's summons, He made it easy for them to receive spiritual sustenance (rizq rūḥānī) before ⟪ar:کَرَّات⟫ their physical death. Upon death, the ⟪ar:رزق روحانی، نفس ناطقۃ⟫ spiritual provision and the speaking soul (al-nafs al-nāṭiqa) return, and they ⟪ar:صورت شریف⟫ become as a noble form and are able to ascend to the spiritual realm again.

#### We Shall Make Easy the Path of Difficulty

Those who rejected the summons of Asher were punished by placing them in this world under the same oppressors ⟪ar:کُبَرَاءِ أُمَّت⟫ to whom they had inclined, so that they could not escape from their clutches. These difficulties accord with their own intentions. Because of ⟪ar:صورت خبیثہ⟫ their evil form and their ⟪ar:ائمۃ الضلال⟫ misleading guides, they are prepared for torment, which manifests in their body upon death and leads them to chastisement.

## Asher's Establishment of the Pillars of Islam

The Almighty created the Divine Beings and celestial essences from the realm of eternity to the realm of eternity, and He bestowed upon them three essential powers: life, knowledge, and power. Some exercised their powers and proceeded as intellects. Some—notably Asher—delayed and changed from intellect to soul-form. The soul began with Asher. After his delusion of equality, he repented again and came back to the realm of reason and attained secondary perfection. He then established his summons in the spiritual world. Those who accepted Asher's summons accepted it, and those who opposed opposed. Those who accepted ⟪ar:تابو بتوبتہ⟫ were left in the spiritual world as those who repent with his repentance and ⟪ar:لب النداہ⟫ those who answer the summons. Those who resisted had to come into this world. ⟪ar:کلمم⟫ They were somewhat delayed in accepting, so they were placed ⟪ar:عالم حیات⟫ in the middle world of life.

When the disobedient group did not obey Asher and did not accept his summons and did not submit to him, they fell in the ranks and became cruel to themselves, until they fell into seventy spiritual ailments (amrāḍ). Due to these diseases, there was a deterioration in their essence, causing it to change from intellect into soul-form. After that, those who delayed further gave rise to many kinds of soul-transformation: ⟪ar:نفس کلیۃ⟫ the universal soul, ⟪ar:نفس ناطقۃ⟫ the speaking soul (which is formed from ⟪ar:خمیرہ ندامت⟫ the essence of remorse), ⟪ar:نفس حسیۃ⟫ the sensory soul (which ⟪ar:شمس⟫ was born from the sun), ⟪ar:نفس نامیۃ⟫ the growing soul (from the realm of stars to the sphere of Saturn). Those who delayed further after that became bodies separated from the soul and fell into the ranks of the falling body. Within the body there is a heart as well as excrement. After that, they became animals, plants, and minerals—so much filth and waste accumulated.

#### Facts

The soul is the corrupted form of the intellect, and the body is the corrupted form of the soul.

Asher was obliged to prepare a world of density for all those who would become corrupted, serving as their abode. Then he would guide them from there, elevate them, and return them to the spiritual world. Since seventy diseases arose, Asher prepared seventy medicines to cure them. These are the seven pillars of the Pillars of Islam (daʿāʾim al-islām). There are teachings of the pillars in every era, but their outward forms have varied according to the people and the times. Asher established this system: the soul of the one who stands firm in both the outward and inward dimensions of these pillars will be purified. The soul will be born anew from repentance. Ten medicines have been placed within each of the seven pillars. There are a total of seventy medicines in this way, and none can be skipped. Through this, our soul will be transformed into intellect and will grow in the spiritual world.

## The Caliphates of Asher

When the second emanator offered his repentance, he attained secondary perfection and became Asher, joining the chain of spiritual intellects. Now whoever sits in his place will be called Asher, because Asher is the name of a station. So far, Asher's chair has changed three times. The first was Asher who was born in the spiritual world. The second was ⟪ar:لب النداہ⟫ the last person who took the place of Asher and ⟪ar:فعل اھبطو⟫ by his plan brought forth physical incarnation and the first perfect being (jitha ibdāʾiyya) was born. Faḍīl, the man of this body, died as soon as he was born in the manner of Asher, becoming the third Asher.[^cite-9] Since there were circles with Asher, the last person in the Asher circle is called Asher.

When the first cycle of ⟪ar:مور اعظم⟫ the Mighty Affair (400,000 aquifers of souls) was completed, there appeared at its end one ⟪ar:قائم المور⟫ who established the Mighty Affair and gathered the entire community of the first perfect being. This ⟪ar:قائم المور⟫ one was made from ⟪ar:باب الابواب⟫ the Gate of Gates of the first perfect being—⟪ar:شخص فاضل⟫ an excellent person ⟪ar:ولد تام⟫ born from the pit (the manifest world) with his son, who was ⟪ar:کیمروت اعظم⟫ born as the supreme form of resurrection—⟪ar:قلب ھیکل⟫ the heart of the temple—in accordance with the Asherian system. He ⟪ar:ولد تام⟫ assumed the caliphate of his father and ⟪ar:کیمروت اعظم⟫ became the supreme form of another perfect being. Now they were preparing to make their ⟪ar:ولد تام⟫ own ⟪ar:ھیکل⟫ temple from this other perfect being. When the second cycle arrives, they will gather the whole of the other perfect being and ⟪ar:صاحب مور ثانی⟫ the lord of the second affair ⟪ar:ولد تام⟫ will merge with his own perfect child and acquire the status of ⟪ar:مور ثانی⟫ the second affair ⟪ar:شخص فاضل⟫ and the excellent person—⟪ar:عاشری مقام⟫ the station of Asher. This will be his fourth caliphate.

When the assembly of the original perfect being (jitha ibdāʾiyya) and their congregation advance to the station of Asher, Asher takes his congregation and obtains the caliphate of the new intellect. The ninth intellect obtains the caliphate of the eighth intellect, the eighth obtains the caliphate of the seventh, the seventh the caliphate of the sixth, the sixth the caliphate of the fifth, the fifth the caliphate of the fourth, and the fourth the caliphate of the third intellect. The third intellect joins the first intellect with its entire congregation.[^cite-10] In the spiritual world, only the first intellect and the first source will not change their place. This system of development will continue thus, until one day all the intellects join their congregations in the first source ⟪ar:حضیرۃ القدس⟫ in the sanctuary of holiness. Then only the spiritual world will remain, and this is the true form of the circle!

#### Facts

First of all, the seven intellects that were ⟪ar:عقول ابدائیۃ⟫ formed in the spiritual world are called the Seven Intellects (al-ʿuqūl al-ʿAbd-āʾiyya). When they had advanced toward the circle of the first source, seven more intellects arose from below—from the Asherian circles and the ranks beneath—came to their places, and took their caliphate. These ⟪ar:انبعاثی عقول⟫ are called the Emanating Intellects (al-ʿuqūl al-Anbāʿāthī).

## Who Is Asher at This Time?

When Asher repented and attained perfection, he entrusted the matter of the physical world to those beneath him. Now Asher truly refers ⟪ar:آدم ابدائیۃ⟫ to the one who is the leader of the world of life and who is the one about to remain in this ⟪ar:رتبہ وحدت⟫ world of physicality—the station of divine unity. He has the same place in the physical world as the first intellect has in the spiritual world. Bashīr—⟪ar:(آدم ابدائیۃ)⟫ the Spiritual Adam—became the place where the rays of the first intellect fell, and in the physical world he became the one who illuminates and supports the Imam in every age. The whole physical world is established by his command.[^cite-11]

#### What Is the Position of the Intellect at This Time?

It is said that Asher, who was in ⟪ar:انبعاث⟫ the realm of emanation, has progressed and reached the realm of the first source. It took him a long time to reach the realm of the first source. His original station as the second emanator was the third rank. But he was tenth in the hierarchy. The intellect of Adam is spiritual because he also fell into error and repented. It took a long time for him to return to his original place. Now he is in the paradise of the first source—that is, he has reached his station, which is the final destination of all. No one has access to the first intellect because it is the station of divine unity (maqām al-waḥda).

#### Who Is Asher of This Era?

This is the final form of our last revelation—⟪ar:ستۃ صورات⟫ the sixth manifestation—seventy, which has been prepared. This is the seventh form of unveiling and seventy.
