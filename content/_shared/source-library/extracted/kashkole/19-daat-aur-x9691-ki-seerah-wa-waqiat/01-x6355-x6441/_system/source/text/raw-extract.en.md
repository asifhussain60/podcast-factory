### In the court of Malik al-Ashtar Mu'awiya:

It has been narrated that in Mu'awiya's court, his fear was such that the brave men would remain silent in front of him. Once, Malik Ashtar arrived with the Musharraf letter of Maulana Ali (a.s.) and sat next to Muawiya's throne. When Muawiyah asked Musharraf to give the letter, he said, "Get down from your throne and take it from me!" Muawiyah's minister ⟪ar:عمرو ابن عاس⟫took Musharraf's letter and gave it to Muawiyah.

Muawiyah read the Musharraf letter and threatened and said, "O master! My army is as numerous as the stars in the sky! He replied, "My Lord Ali is the sun, and when he rises, all the stars are hidden from his fear." Muawiya said, "The number of my army is as large as the grains of grain scattered on the ground." You replied that Ali has a rooster named 'Malik'! When the rooster is hungry, he sucks all the grains in front of him and leaves no left.

Sayyidina Mu'ayyad al-Shirazi was the first preacher to formulate the philosophy of the origin of life as a science. Before him, the sciences of the Prophets were hidden in the wisdom of Maulana Ali (a.s.) and other Imams.

Although Sayyidina al-Mu'ayyad held a very high position, Imam (a.s.) subjected him to such severe tests that if he had been replaced by someone else, he would have become an atheist. While in the same city, he was not allowed to meet the Imam for five years. One day, Sayyidina Mu'ayyad received a pamphlet from the Imam with the Imam's seal and it was written, "We order you to go to battle right away!"
