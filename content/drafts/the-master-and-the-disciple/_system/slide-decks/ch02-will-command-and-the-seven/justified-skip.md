## (c) Why no slide is warranted — chapter-specific reasoning

Two properties of *this* chapter (not generic "philosophical text" properties) make a slide deck a category error, even though the affinity matrix would otherwise predict good fits:

**1. The chapter's hermeneutic explicitly forbids measurement-parables for the central referents.** The Master settles a Quranic objection mid-chapter: the verse *Did He not also forbid striking parables for God?* is reconciled with *God has the highest similitude* by distinguishing **measurement-parables** (forbidden) from **signifier-parables** (commanded). *The highest similitude* is read as *the highest **proof***, not the highest measurement. A slide is, by construction, a measurement-parable — it places entities in space, sizes them, ranks them, axiates them. A hierarchy tree of *speakers → chiefs → arguments → summoners* would render as measurement what the chapter insists must be received as signification. A 2x2 of *apparent / inward* against *speech / silence* would carve the space the Master refuses to carve. The chapter's own central settlement (R-RECURRING-THESIS: *the apparent is evidence of the hidden*, spoken three times verbatim) functions only if the apparent stays prose; rendered as diagram, it becomes the very thing it is evidence-of, which collapses the figure.

**2. The chapter's authorial restraint withholds the enumerations a slide would have to surface.** The framing (`00-framing.md` §"Stable role-labels", §"Name discipline", §"Do not") forbids: enumerating the seven speakers by individual prophetic name; listing the twelve chiefs or twelve arguments by name; opening the inward-of-the-inward (the yolk); voicing Arabic personal names, surah names, or the four ranks in their Arabic terms. The chapter receives the **FORM** of the seven without the **ENUMERATION** and the **FORM** of the three layers without the **YOLK**. A slide deck would force one of two failures: either (i) populate the structural cells (Speaker 1, Speaker 2, …, Chief 1, …) and betray the chapter's discipline by enumerating what the Master holds back, or (ii) leave the cells blank and produce a structurally meaningless template — a hierarchy tree with un-named nodes is, per `slide-deck-patterns.md` §"Anti-Patterns" #1 (description-not-structure) and #5 (generic placement), an anti-pattern the Challenger catches. The chapter's discipline is precisely the discipline a slide cannot honor.

These two properties — the measurement/signifier distinction at the center of the chapter's own hermeneutic, and the explicit withholding of enumeration at every structural moment — are what make this chapter a `not-needed` rather than a `defer-and-author-spine-first`. Authoring the spine and re-running the density gauge would not change the verdict: the highest-density beats would be precisely the enumeration-and-architecture beats the chapter forbids slidifying. The three parables the chapter does commend (body/soul, air-encompassing-all, hidden egg) are signifier-parables that work as **spoken images carried by silence at the close**, not as illustrated structures — the framing's `## Landing` directive ends the episode on the egg "spoken" and "both hosts then go silent," which is the antithesis of an illustrated slide.
