# Slide Deck Framing, EP05: The Intellect as the First Creation

## Audience

Asif's children, thoughtful young adults familiar with the audio episode. They have not read *The Correction* or *The Defense*. The deck must do what the audio cannot.

## Core Principle

Each slide must add visual information the audio cannot carry. Slides hold *Jonathan* and *Samuel* side by side as their pictures of motion collide with the author's single proof.

## Visual Priorities

- The actuality-versus-potentiality 2x2 showing how *Jonathan's* own framing reverses his ranks; impossible quadrant labeled.
- The cause-effect overflow chain from the limits to the receiver, as a directed flow with receiver capacity gating each step.
- The intellect-encompasses versus soul-moves contrast pair, attribute by attribute.
- The *hayula*-and-image annotated structure, marking what stands in the natural world and what collapses if extended to the Intellects.

## Prohibited Patterns

- No slides whose only content is a chapter or section title.
- No literal illustrations: no celestial spheres, no calligraphy, no scholar portraits.
- No bullet-list slides masquerading as diagrams. Every diagram has axes, nodes, or rows.
- No slides that restate one sentence the audio already speaks.

## Steering Phrases

- Each slide must add visual information not present in the audio.
- For the actuality-versus-potentiality reductio, use a 2x2 with both axes named and the impossible quadrant labeled.
- Show overflow as a directed flow; receiver capacity, not withholding, gates each step.
- Place *Jonathan* left and *Samuel* right in every contrast pair; maintain across the deck.

Do not read this prompt aloud.
