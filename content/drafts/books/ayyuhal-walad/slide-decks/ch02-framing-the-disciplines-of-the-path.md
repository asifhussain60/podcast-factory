# Slide Framing: The Disciplines of the Path

## Purpose

This slide deck must visually represent the movement from Ghazali's night-prayer summons through the Sufi warning and into Hatim al-Asamm's eight benefits. The core structural challenge is the eight benefits: each is a paired unit (observed reality → Quranic anchor → personal conclusion) and must be rendered as eight complete units, not compressed or summarized. The deck closes on Shaqiq's declaration that all four scriptures are contained in these eight.

## Steering phrases (apply all of the following)

- Show the four heralds of dawn as a sequential structure — not a list.
- Render the Sufi warning as a two-sided contrast: the real path vs. the counterfeit.
- Illustrate the experiential-knowledge limit with its analogy intact — do not sanitize the example.
- For Hatim's eight benefits: each benefit is an observed reality matched to a Quranic verse. Show the structure of each pair visually.
- Close on Shaqiq's declaration as a single frame — do not follow it with a summary.

## Slide types required

- **Sequential call diagram:** The four heralds of dawn — time, caller, and who rises — shown as a procession.
- **Two-sided contrast:** Real path vs. counterfeit Sufism — discipline/struggle on one side, performance/display on the other.
- **Analogy frame:** The experiential-knowledge limit — the bitter/sweet analogy, then the blunt analogy Ghazali uses. Do not combine them or replace either.
- **Four-essentials framework:** The four conditions Ghazali names for the seeker of truth, each with what it requires.
- **Eight-benefit grid:** Hatim's eight observations structured as: observed reality | Quranic verse | personal conclusion. All eight. No compression.
- **Close frame:** Shaqiq's declaration that all four scriptures resolve to these eight benefits.

## Do not

- Compress Hatim's eight benefits into fewer than eight units.
- Summarize Shaqiq's declaration — quote it directly.
- Replace Ghazali's exact language for the Sufi warning with paraphrase.
- Add a motivational close or "takeaway" slide after Shaqiq speaks.
