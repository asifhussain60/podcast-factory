# Slide Framing: The Seeker's Path: Conditions and Stations

## Purpose
This slide deck must visually represent the interconnected conditions and inner stations Ghazali outlines for the spiritual seeker. It needs to show how each element builds upon the others, the relationships between the seeker, the guide, and the Divine, and the internal architecture of the spiritual journey, which cannot be conveyed through linear text.

## Steering phrases (apply all of the following)
- Illustrate the progression and interdependence of the seven conditions.
- Diagram the relationship between the seeker, the perfected guide, and the prophetic lineage.
- Visualize the internal struggle against the lower soul and its external influences.
- Map the four inner stations (Tasawwuf, Servitude, Tawakkul, Ikhlas) as a progressive journey of inner transformation.
- Use visual metaphors for purification, growth, and alignment.

## Slide types required
- **Flowchart/Progression Diagram:** For the "Seven Conditions" (correct belief, repentance, settling claims, Shari'ah knowledge, guide, refusing nafs, humility/contentment).
- **Network/Hierarchy Diagram:** To show the "Perfected Guide" and the "Prophetic Lineage" (seeker -> guide -> chain -> Prophet).
- **Venn Diagram/Overlap Diagram:** To illustrate the "Two Qualities of Tasawwuf" (obedience to Allah + sympathy to creation).
- **Layered/Concentric Circle Diagram:** For "Servitude" (obey commands -> content with destiny -> surrender desires).
- **Two-Part/Balance Diagram:** For "Tawakkul" (tie camel + trust in Allah).
- **Internal vs. External Diagram:** For "Ikhlas" (deeds for Allah vs. avoiding *riya* and external praise).
- **Cause-and-Effect Diagram:** For "Refusing the Deceptions of the Lower Soul" (corrupt companions -> lower soul access -> Satan's domination).

## Do not
- Restate the audio discussion in bullet-point slides.
- Produce "title + stock-photo illustration" slides.
- Use generic relationship labels ("influences", "relates to") — name the specific relationship.
