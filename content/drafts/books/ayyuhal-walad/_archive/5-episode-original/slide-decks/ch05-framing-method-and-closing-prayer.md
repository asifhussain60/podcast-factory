# Slide Framing: The Four Methods of Living

## Purpose
This slide deck must visually represent the four methods of living outlined by al-Ghazali, showing their interconnectedness and how they form a coherent framework for daily life. It should illustrate the core principle underlying all four methods and the specific tests associated with each, making abstract concepts concrete and actionable.

## Steering phrases (apply all of the following)
- Diagram the four methods as a unified system, not separate items.
- Use visual metaphors for each method's core principle and test.
- Show the relationship between the "four cautions" and the "four methods."
- Illustrate the "self as a measure" principle for relating to God and people.
- Visualize the impact of the "one week left" thought experiment on knowledge.

## Slide types required
- **System Diagram:** Illustrating the four methods as interconnected components of a holistic approach to living, showing the central principle that unifies them.
- **Analogy/Metaphor Diagram:** For "relating to God," visually representing the servant/master analogy and the concept of *Ihsan*.
- **Mirror/Reflection Diagram:** For "relating to people," showing the "self as a measure" principle and its application to others.
- **Decision Tree/Filter Diagram:** For "study what will benefit you," illustrating the "one week left" thought experiment and its implications for knowledge selection.
- **Scale/Continuum Diagram:** For "do not stockpile food," showing the relationship between *Tawakkul* (reliance on God) and the amount of provision stockpiled.
- **Flowchart/Cycle Diagram:** Illustrating how the closing supplication reinforces the principles of the entire letter and the four methods in daily life.

## Do not
- Restate the audio discussion in bullet-point slides.
- Produce "title + stock-photo illustration" slides.
- Use generic relationship labels ("influences", "relates to") — name the specific relationship.
