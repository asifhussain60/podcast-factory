# Slide Framing: Haatim's Eight Pivots

## Purpose
This slide deck must visually represent the core spiritual transformations Haatim undergoes, showing the contrast between common worldly perspectives and the Quranic insights that lead to his eight benefits. It should illustrate the "pivot" from one way of seeing to another, emphasizing the architectural shift in his life rather than just the intellectual understanding.

## Steering phrases (apply all of the following)
- Illustrate the "before and after" of Haatim's understanding for each benefit.
- Use visual metaphors for abstract concepts like "beloved," "lower soul," "grandeur," and "reliance."
- Show the contrast between worldly pursuits and divine principles.
- Emphasize the "pivot" or "reorientation" at the heart of each benefit.
- Depict the cumulative effect of these eight shifts on Haatim's spiritual journey.

## Slide types required
- **Two-column comparison diagram:** For each of the eight benefits, show "Worldly View" vs. "Haatim's Pivot (Quranic Insight)."
- **Flowchart/Process diagram:** Illustrate the sequence of Haatim's realization: Observation -> Quranic Verse -> Reorganization of Life.
- **Metaphorical diagram:** For "The first benefit: the beloved that follows you in," depict different "beloveds" (e.g., wealth, family, honor) accompanying a person to various points, with good deeds being the only one entering the grave.
- **Hierarchical/Pyramid diagram:** For "The fourth benefit: grandeur belongs to God-consciousness," show worldly sources of grandeur at the base and *taqwa* at the apex.
- **Cause-and-effect diagram:** For "The fifth benefit: envy ends when you trust the distribution," illustrate how trusting divine distribution breaks the cycle of envy and competition.
- **Concentric circles/Target diagram:** For "The sixth benefit: there is only one enemy," show Satan as the central enemy, with human conflicts as outer distractions.
- **Journey/Path diagram:** For "The eighth benefit: reliance on the only one worth relying on," depict a path with various unreliable supports, leading to a single, strong anchor of reliance on Allah.

## Do not
- Restate the audio discussion in bullet-point slides.
- Produce "title + stock-photo illustration" slides.
- Use generic relationship labels ("influences", "relates to") — name the specific relationship.
