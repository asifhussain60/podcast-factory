# Normalized — ch03-the-path (house voice via Gemini)

Young one, after today, ask me about what is unclear to you, but only with your heart. As the Exalted says, "and had they waited patiently until you came forth to them, it had been better for them." Heed the advice of Khidr, upon our Prophet and upon him be blessing and peace: "And do not ask me about anything until I mention it unto you." Do not rush the time when it will be revealed to you. Recall the words: "I will show you my signs so do not be in a hurry." Therefore, do not question me before the appointed time. Be certain that you will only reach it through a journey. Consider the question: "Have they not travelled through the earth, and seen?"

Young one, truly, if you journey, you will witness wonders at every stage. Give your whole self without reservation, for the essence of this path lies in giving your spirit generously. As Dhu 'l-Nun al-Misri, may Allah have mercy on him, told one of his students: "If you are able to give your spirit without reserve, come; and if not, then do not busy yourself with the idle practices of Mysticism."
