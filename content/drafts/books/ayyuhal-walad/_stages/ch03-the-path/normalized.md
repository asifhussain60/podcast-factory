# Normalized — ch03-the-path (house voice via Gemini)

Young man, from now on, ask me about what is unclear to you only with the language of the heart. As the Exalted says, "and had they waited patiently until you came forth to them, it had been better for them." Heed the advice of Khidr, peace be upon him and our Prophet: "And do not ask me about anything until I mention it unto you." Do not rush to reach the time when it will be revealed to you. Remember the verse: "I will show you my signs so do not be in a hurry." Therefore, do not ask me before the appointed time. Be certain that you will only reach that time through spiritual journeying. "Have they not travelled through the earth, and seen?"

Young man, by Allah, if you embark on this journey, you will witness wonders at every stage. Devote yourself fully, for the essence of this path lies in abundant spiritual effort. Dhu 'l-Nun al-Misri, may Allah have mercy on him, once told a student: "If you can give your spirit without reserve, then come. Otherwise, do not occupy yourself with the superficial aspects of Mysticism."
