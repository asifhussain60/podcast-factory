## (c) Why no slide is warranted — chapter-specific reasoning

Three properties of *this* chapter (not generic "philosophical text" properties) make a slide deck a category error, even though the affinity matrix would otherwise predict good fits — including, in the inner-interpretation passages, near-perfect fits:

**1. The chapter's central recurring thesis is itself an anti-measurement figure, spoken three times verbatim.** R-RECURRING-THESIS for this episode is *this world is seen by the eye of wealth and children, and the Hereafter is seen by the eye of knowledge and certainty.* The thesis is a **two-eyes** figure — the eye is the interior faculty by which a domain is *seen*, not a quadrant in a 2x2 grid. The framing (`00-framing.md` §"Opening directive", §"Three-part focus" Beat 4, §"Landing") binds the thesis to a strict three-strike cadence at the open, at Beat 4's chapter-internal delivery, and at the close after the deferral at the door — *same words, same order*. A slide rendering of "wealth-and-children" against "knowledge-and-certainty" as a contrast pair, or as the axes of a 2x2 partitioning *this-world / Hereafter* against *perishing / lasting*, would substitute the **diagram** for the **eye** — converting the figure the listener is supposed to *internalize three times* into a flat external rendering. The chapter's own settlement in Beat 4 ("the outward is not valid except by the inward, and the inward is not made good except by the outward") collapses the moment the contrast is rendered as a binary cell map: the diagram would foreground the *opposition* the chapter spent the beat *integrating*.

**2. The chapter's discipline of attribute-only revelation forbids the one diagram the inner interpretations would otherwise demand.** The chapter's climactic move — the first direct speaking of the Imam in the book — is delivered **BY ATTRIBUTE ONLY**: *the one whose right Allah has made binding upon me and upon every believer, in whose hand are the keys of the heavens and the landmarks of the kingdom, whose palms are opened with the light of Sinai — he is the cause behind the signs.* The framing's `## Name discipline` and `## Do not` blocks bind this discipline three layers deep: (i) the generous one is named ONLY by attributes — never by personal name; (ii) the seven speakers, twelve captains, and ten arguments are named ONLY as ranks — never enumerated individually; (iii) the forbidden pairing of the leadership-title with the personal name of the Father of Imams must never appear. The two inner-interpretation passages in Beat 3 (the inward of *there is no power except in Allah*; the inward of Pharaoh's dream) read, on the surface, as the chapter's most diagram-ready material — every cell has a named value. But every visual rendering of these mappings would force one of two failures: either (i) populate the structural cells (Speaker 1, Speaker 2, …, Captain 1, …, Argument 1, … / the Imam *named*) and betray the chapter's three-layered discipline by enumerating what the Master holds back, or (ii) leave the cells blank — *the year-turning figure → [the Imam, by attribute only]*, *the seven causes → [unnamed]*, *the lean cows → [the seven speakers, by rank only]* — and produce, per `slide-deck-patterns.md` §"Anti-Patterns" #1 (description-not-structure) and #5 (generic placement), the precise anti-patterns the Challenger catches. A genealogy diagram with un-named nodes, or a hierarchy tree whose terminal cells say *withheld*, is the structural shape of the chapter's withholding turned into a slide — which is to render the withholding as content, the opposite of what the discipline requires.

**3. The chapter's two narrative climaxes are enacted ethical actions, not structural arguments.** The chapter's two largest moments are (i) the boy's *enactment* of the wealth-doctrine — placing all his wealth in the Master's hands in five shares (purification-due, expiation, thanksgiving, the share of the brethren, the share for the Master) — followed by the Master, *tears flowing*, refusing his own share and *reapportioning* the rest as *trusts held for their rightful owners*; and (ii) the narrator's pivot when the Master *beams and weeps* at the boy's description of the brethren *yellow with vigil, weak with fasting*. The framing flags moment (ii) as the natural site for R-SURPRISE-MOVE — *let it land as the narrator means it*; *do not pause to explain*. Both moments are **narrator-pivot moments** whose force is exactly that the listener is *not* prepared for them. A slide rendering of "the five shares" as a pie chart (or the natural alternative, an annotated-structure listing the five categories and their reapportionments) would (a) pre-announce, by its very presence in the deck, an action the audio surprises the listener with — collapsing R-SURPRISE-MOVE — and (b) render as static enumeration what the chapter delivers as a *sequence of speech acts* (the boy *places*, the Master *refuses*, the Master *reapportions*, the boy *asks who*). The chapter's `## Landing` directive ends the episode on the deferral at the door spoken in the Master's own voice, with the recurring thesis spoken for the third and final time and the listener *carrying the silence out of the episode*; an illustrated final slide of "the door of the chamber" would supply, in image, the contents the chapter has just sealed shut.

These three properties — the two-eyes thesis as an anti-measurement figure bound by a verbatim three-strike cadence, the three-layered discipline of attribute-only revelation that hollows the inner-interpretation diagrams the affinity matrix would otherwise predict, and the chapter's two narrator-pivot enactments whose force depends on *not* being pre-rendered — are what make this chapter a `not-needed` rather than a `defer-and-author-spine-first`. Authoring the spine and re-running the density gauge would not change the verdict: the highest-density beats would be precisely the inner-interpretation and five-share beats the chapter's discipline forbids slidifying. The three governing images the chapter does commend (the sower preparing the land but not bringing out the plant; the archers unanimous in shooting and differing in striking; the two eyes) are signifier-figures that work as **spoken images carried inside the conversation**, and the framing's `## Tone constraints` block forbids inventing further analogies — *no other analogies are invented; no contemporary comparisons are introduced* — which is the very freedom a slide deck would have to take to fill its frames.
