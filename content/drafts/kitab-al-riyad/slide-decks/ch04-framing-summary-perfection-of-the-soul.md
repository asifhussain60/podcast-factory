# Slide Deck Framing, EP04: On the Perfection of the Soul, Unified Summary

## Audience

Asif's children, returning to refresh the argument. They have heard EP01 through EP03 and want a one-pass map of the chapter's spine, not a fresh exposition.

## Core Principle

This is a synthesis and review deck. Each slide must condense a tension already developed across EP01 through EP03 into a single visual moment, so the deck makes three movements simultaneous on the page. If a slide could be a sentence the audio recap already speaks, omit it.

## Visual Priorities

- The two-error map (deficient Soul versus undifferentiated Soul) flanking the chapter's governing question.
- The identity-versus-rank formula as a paired contrast, with Quranic, aphoristic, and celestial anchors stacked beneath as three witnesses.
- The two-souls equivocation (Successor Soul versus natural human soul) as the central diagnostic, paired with the sperm-and-man analogy.
- The Speaker-Foundation correspondence as a hierarchy.

## Prohibited Patterns

- No slides whose only content is a movement title.
- No literal illustrations: no calligraphy, no mosques, no scholar portraits.
- No bullet-list slides masquerading as diagrams.
- No closing summary slide.

## Steering Phrases

- Each slide must add visual information not present in the audio.
- Treat the audio as the linear recap; slides make three movements simultaneous.
- Render Successor Soul and natural human soul as side-by-side columns, attribute by attribute.
- Show the relationships between entities, not a list of them.
- Place the higher-rank entity to the left in every pair.

Do not read this prompt aloud.
