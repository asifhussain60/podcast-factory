# Slide Deck Framing, EP15: Kitab al-Riyad, Book Summary

## Audience

Asif's children, either new to the book or returning for review. The deck is a short orientation to the whole thirteen-chapter argument.

## Core Principle

This is a whole-book orientation deck. Each slide must render the four-movement architecture simultaneously, hold identity-versus-rank in one frame, pair *qada* and *qadar*, lay the prophetic chain as one descending line, and resolve the movements into the silence of *divine oneness*.

## Visual Priorities

- The four-movement hierarchy as opening orientation: chapters, domain, what each establishes.
- Soul-Intellect identity-versus-rank as a named-axis 2x2, mirroring EP01.
- *Qada* and *qadar* as a contrast pair with a shared row.
- The prophetic chain from Adam through Imam Hussain to the present as a directed genealogy.
- The closing dissolution into *divine oneness* as concentric layers, innermost labeled silence.

## Prohibited Patterns

- No slides whose only content is a movement or chapter title.
- No literal illustrations: gardens, mosques, calligraphy, portraits.
- No bullet-lists masquerading as diagrams. Every diagram has axes, nodes, rows, or layers.
- No slides that restate one sentence the audio already speaks.

## Steering Phrases

- Each slide must add visual information not present in the audio.
- For identity-versus-rank, use a 2x2 with both axes named and the impossible quadrant labeled.
- Show the prophetic chain as a directed genealogy with edges, not a list of names.
- For the closing, use concentric layers with the innermost labeled silence.
- Place the higher-rank entity to the left in every pair across the deck.

Do not read this prompt aloud.
