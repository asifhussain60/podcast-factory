# Slide Deck Framing, EP07: Souls, Parts of the First Truths, or Only Traces?

## Audience

Asif's children, thoughtful young adults familiar with the audio episode. They have not read *The Correction*, *The Defense*, or *Asrar al-Ma'ad*.

## Core Principle

Each slide must add visual information the audio cannot carry. Slides make parts-versus-traces simultaneous, render the trilemma as one structure, and show the two-emanation architecture as a hierarchy.

## Visual Priorities

- The parts-versus-traces dispute as a side-by-side contrast pair, with the author's third word, *second emanation*, as a settled third column.
- The trilemma against parts: three alternatives the Second could be, each invalidated, with the eliminated conclusion at the bottom.
- The two emanations as a hierarchy: First, universal Soul, speaking souls, with named proportions of receivership.
- The capacity 2x2: composition vs rank, bounded vs unbounded, with the impossible quadrant labeled.

## Prohibited Patterns

- No slides whose only content is a chapter or sub-chapter title.
- No literal illustrations of *the limits*, the Sixties, or the Prophet's vision: no stock imagery, no calligraphy, no portraits.
- No bullet-list slides masquerading as diagrams.
- No slides that restate a sentence the audio already speaks.

## Steering Phrases

- Each slide must add visual information not present in the audio.
- For the capacity question, use a 2x2 with composition-vs-rank and bounded-vs-unbounded as axes; label the impossible quadrant.
- For the two emanations, show a hierarchy with named ranks and named proportions of receivership.
- Place *The Correction* on the left and *The Defense* on the right in every contrast; maintain across the deck.

Do not read this prompt aloud.
